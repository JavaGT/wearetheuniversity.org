Vice-Chancellor's Update | Monday 18 December 2023

undefined

Mon Dec 18 2023 13:00:00 GMT+1300 (New Zealand Daylight Time)

Kia ora koutou

With a well-earned Summer break fast approaching, we can reflect on yet another successful year for the University.

We have acknowledged the success of our academic and professional staff and students throughout the year. These collective outstanding efforts define our University and make it the globally recognised institution it is. Everyone has contributed to this and we can all be proud of what has been achieved. I want to thank you all for your commitment and tireless mahi throughout the year.

Academic Promotions

Congratulations to our academic staff who were promoted recently. You can see the full list of names on the staff intranethttps://www.staff.auckland.ac.nz/en/news-events-and-notices/news/news-2023/12/professorial-promotions-for-2024-announced.html of those promoted to Associate Professor and Professor.

FMHS graduation

Congratulations also to our students who graduated last Thursday in the FMHS graduation ceremonies. You can read about some of their stories on the News pages herehttps://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/news/list.html?tag=news:communications-team/health-medicine.

Revised Travel Policy

The recently revised Travel Policy and associated documents are now live on the Policy Hub. The changes will help to ensure that travel costs are effectively managed, monitored and authorised, and that we meet our commitments to sustainability. More information is available on the Policy Hub herehttps://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/about-us/about-the-university/policy-hub/enabling-environment/finance-capital-risk/travel.html.

I wish you all a well-deserved and relaxing holiday with your whānau and friends. Meri kirihimete. Season’s greetings.

Ngā mihi nui

Dawn

Professor Dawn Freshwater

Vice-Chancellor
