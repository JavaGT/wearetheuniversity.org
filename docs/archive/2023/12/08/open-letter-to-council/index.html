undefined

Fri Dec 08 2023 13:00:00 GMT+1300 (New Zealand Daylight Time)

Attachment: Open letter to UoA Council regarding staff safety.pdf Kia ora koutou, You are receiving this message because you indicated you would like to receive updates on the open letter. 29% of you indicated you would prefer to remain anonymous so I have used bcc for this message.

The letter (attached) was sent to Council this afternoon. At time of submission, we had 119 people on the list. We will continue to collect signatories because we had a lot of out of office messages. Please continue to spread the word.

Thank you all for your support for this issue. Many of you shared your own stories and this brings home the importance of this action. Arohanui to those who have been dealing with challenging circumstances.

I did an interview with Mediaworks this afternoon and I expect it to be on the radio news tomorrow morning. We are hoping for some more coverage over the weekend.

Noho ora mai, Cate
