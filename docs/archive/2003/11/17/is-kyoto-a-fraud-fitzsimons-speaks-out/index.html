Is Kyoto a fraud? Fitzsimons speaks out

Green Party Co-leader Jeanette Fitzsimons will participate in the Third Annual Vice Chancellor's Debate at Waikato University this evening.

The theme for this year's debate is 'That Kyoto is a Fraud', which Ms Fitzsimons will argue against. National Business Review columnist Owen McShane takes the affirmative, and the debate will the chaired by Waikato University's Vice Chancellor Professor Bryan Gould.

Jeanette Fitzsimons debates
That Kyoto is a Fraud

7pm, Monday 17 November
Gallagher Concert Chamber,
WEL Energy Trust Academy of Performing Arts
Waikato University Campus, Hamilton
