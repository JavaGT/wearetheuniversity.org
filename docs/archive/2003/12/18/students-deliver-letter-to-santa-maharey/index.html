Students Deliver Letter to "Santa" Maharey

Thursday, 18 December 2003, 4:55 pm
Press Release: New Zealand University Student's Association

Media Advisory:
Students Deliver Letter to "Santa" Maharey

Student leaders will be getting into the Christmas spirit tomorrow and dressing in Christmas costumes to deliver a letter to "Santa" Maharey asking for more next year for debt ridden students.

The students will be delivering their list on the steps of parliament, at 9.30 am, on Friday 19 December.
