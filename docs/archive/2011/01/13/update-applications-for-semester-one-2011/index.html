Update: Applications for Semester One, 2011

25 January, 2011.

The 2010 NCEA and CIE results have been published and the University is processing applications for the 2011 Academic Year.

For those applying to start in March 2011, please continue to check your Student Services Online account – offers for most programmes will start being made over the next few days.

To check your application status, log in to Student Services Online.
