Improbable Futures Forum III, Saturday 9 September

Improbable Futures Forum III
After Criticism: Writing Now
Saturday 9 September 4–6pm
Adam Art Gallery, Gate 3, Kelburn Parade

Please join us as Lana Lopesi, Editor-in-Chief of The Pantograph Punch, chairs the final of our three discussion forums organised in association with Adam Art Gallery’s The Tomorrow People exhibition. Lopesi will lead a discussion with artist Hana Pera Aoake, writer Megan Dunn, and author, academic and journalist Max Rashbrooke. In the course of their conversation they will approach the nature of writing now, exploring the forms of writing that are flourishing in the digital domain and in contemporary book publishing. This forum is a partnership with the Cultivating Creative Capital theme at Victoria University.
