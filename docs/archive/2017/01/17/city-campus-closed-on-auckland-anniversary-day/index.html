City Campus closed on Auckland Anniversary Day

The City Campus will be closed on Auckland Anniversary Day, 30 January 2017, because of the Laneway Festival in Albert Park. The 100 and 300 sectors of the City Campus will be fenced off and unavailable for use.

This includes the Recreation Centre, General Library and Kate Edger Information Commons.
