ACT Board Election Results

ACT New Zealand Friday, 24 March 2006 Press Releases - Other

Members of ACT New Zealand have elected Garry Mallett as their new President and Trevor Loudon as Vice-President.

"ACT members have once again elected quality people with talent and passion", said outgoing ACT President Catherine Judd.

"I thank all those who put themselves forward, and congratulate the successful candidates. Newly elected Regional Board Members and Deputy Board Members are as follows.

Regional Board Members

Auckland North: Brendon Cullen Auckland South: David Seymour Wellington: Michael Collins

Deputy Board Members

Auckland South: Warren Jones Waikato/BOP: Ron Scott Upper South: Andrew Falloon
