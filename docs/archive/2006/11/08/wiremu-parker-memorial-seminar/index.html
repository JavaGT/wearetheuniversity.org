Wiremu Parker Memorial Seminar

Wednesday, 8 November 2006, 11:48 am
Press Release: Victoria University of Wellington

Kia ora

Please find attached a panui/media advisory regarding the Wiremu Parker Memorial Seminar being held at Victoria University of Wellington's Te Herenga Waka Marae.

The seminar includes a tribute to the late Dr Wiremu Parker as well as a presentation on research undertaken for the Broadcasting Standards Authority regarding the Potrayal of Mâori And Te Ao Mâori In Broadcasting, and in particular, the Foreshore and seabed issue.

Powhiri: 9am, Friday 10 November at Te Herenga Waka Marae, 46 Kelburn Parade. Further details can be found: www.vuw.ac.nz/marae or in the attached Panui.
