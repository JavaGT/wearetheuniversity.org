Incident on Symonds Street - Update 25 March 2025

undefined

Tue Mar 25 2025 13:00:00 GMT+1300 (New Zealand Daylight Time)

Kia ora koutou,

We can confirm that one of our staff members was injured and is receiving hospital treatment following the incident on Symonds Street yesterday involving a car and pedestrians. Several of our students were also injured and are recovering at home.

A reminder that the Employee Assistance Programmehttps://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/intranet/work-personal/staff-support/eap.html (for staff) and Te Papa Manaaki | Campus Carehttps://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/students/student-support/personal-support/te-papa-manaaki-campus-care.html (for students) can provide confidential, professional services to anyone needing support.

External support is also available from these organisations: Mental Health Crisis Line: 0800 373 7550  Need to Talk https://1737.org.nzhttps://1737.org.nz/ | Call or text 1737 or 0800 1737 1737  Lifeline: 0800 543 354 Free text 4357 (HELP)  Youthline: 0800 376 633 Free text 234
