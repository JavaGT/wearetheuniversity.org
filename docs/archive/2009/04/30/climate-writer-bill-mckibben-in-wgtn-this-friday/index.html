Climate writer Bill McKibben in Wgtn this Friday.

Thursday, 30 April 2009, 1:27 pm
Press Release: Victoria University of Wellington

Victoria University of Wellington is hosting renowned climate writer
Bill McKibben this Friday.

Bill, who is founder of 350.org, will be delivering a lecture on climate
change issues as part of his current New Zealand tour.

Where: Rutherford House Lecture Theatre 2 (note
limited space).

When: Friday 1 May, 6 pm - 7.30 pm

Chair: Peter Glensor, Deputy Chair,
Greater Wellington Regional Council and Chair,
Greater Wellington Transport Committee

Introduction: Associate
Professor Ralph Chapman, Victoria University of Wellington, Director
Environmental Studies.

Background: http://www.billmckibben.com/
