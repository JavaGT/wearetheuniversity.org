Vice-Chancellor's Update | 28 November 2024

undefined

Thu Nov 28 2024 13:00:00 GMT+1300 (New Zealand Daylight Time)

Kia ora tātou

As we enter the final few weeks of the year, I wanted to provide the opportunity for a final all-staff forum, which has been rescheduled to 12pm on Monday 2 December and will cover a range of updates, including those related to the University reviews currently underway.

Pay offer to staff on Individual Employment Agreements Over recent months, we have all been working hard to balance the future financial sustainability of the University with the ability to make a fair pay offer to our staff. Today I made an offer to eligible academic and professional staff on Individual Employment Agreements (IEAs).

The general revision offer for 2025 and 2026 for eligible staff on IEAs is as follows:

  • Year 1: 3% effective 1 February 2025
  • Year 2: 2% effective 1 February 2026

To recognise the disproportionate impact that cost-of-living increases have on staff who are in lower-paid positions, the general revision offer to eligible staff paid on or below $70,000 (FTE) is:

  • Year 1: 3.5% effective 1 February 2025
  • Year 2: 2.25% effective 1 February 2026

Collective bargaining with the unions commenced in July 2024 and remains ongoing. Information regarding ongoing collective agreement negotiations is available on the Intranet page: Bargaining for collective agreements 2024https://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/intranet/stay-informed/staff-communications/notices/bargaining-for-collective-agreements-2024.html.

Ngā mihi nui Dawn

Professor Dawn Freshwater Vice-Chancellor
