Important Hono tasks to complete before you go on leave

undefined

Fri Nov 29 2024 13:00:00 GMT+1300 (New Zealand Daylight Time)

Kia ora koutou

I am pleased to share that we have a revised go-live date for our new HR ecosystem, Hono | Human Connections, Monday 10 February 2025.

To facilitate the transition between systems there will be a shutdown period from Monday 6 January 2025 – Sunday 9 February 2025, following the end-of-year closedown. During this time PeopleSoft, Silkroad and Cornerstone (Career tools) will be unavailable. SmartRecruiters will shut down from Wednesday 15 January – Sunday 9 February 2025.

Next steps Prepare for the system shutdown dates Complete your ramp down and clean-up activitieshttps://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/intranet/stay-informed/improvement-projects-programmes/hono/system-shutdown-business-process-impacts.html before you go on leave:

  • Submit leave you plan to take between now and the go-live date.

  • Complete in-progress online learning courses to avoid restarting in the new system.

  • Download your payslip as required before the system shutdown.

  • Update personal information such as pay deductions (superannuation, childcare and car-parking), visa details, tax codes, or bank account information.

  • Submit your timesheet for any casual hours anticipated during the shutdown period when the portal opens for early submission on Wednesday 4 December 2024.

Complete your Hono training

  • Employees and casual staff can complete training for Hono with a 30-minute eLearning coursehttps://uoa.csod.com/samldefault.aspx?ouid=3&returnUrl=%252fDeepLink%252fProcessRedirect.aspx%253fmodule%253dlodetails%2526lo%253dd7f19d74-bd75-4fa3-8870-d993debeb11e.

  • Professional and academic managers can access links to their training on the staff intranethttps://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/intranet/stay-informed/improvement-projects-programmes/hono/hono-training.html. In-person learning is highly recommended.

If you have any questions, please contact the Hono programme teammailto:hrecosystem@auckland.ac.nz?subject=Hono%20|%20I%20have%20a%20question. 

Ngā mihi nui Andrew Phipps Human Resources Director
