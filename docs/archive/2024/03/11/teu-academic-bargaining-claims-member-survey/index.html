Tēnā tātou katoa,

As noted in last week's TEU Update, our current collective employment agreements will expire this year, and negotiations for new agreements will start soon. Your Branch Committee are keen to hear about what matters to you and your colleagues. What would you like to change about your pay, working conditions, and other aspects of your workplace?

Please take a couple of minutes to fill out this short 5-minute survey herehttps://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc8mX0uXCb7tzgf3uQBd7RuXH817Kz7fq2hkhuFP-D460nHdA/viewform. [https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/fV9fv5PY5q0zYoScIC1G3qm3lnZGYOJkG8lzuHS7FP00WfIaMZuX8sO97RpTRGVygHqozmQjyFc=w1200-h630-p]https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc8mX0uXCb7tzgf3uQBd7RuXH817Kz7fq2hkhuFP-D460nHdA/viewform TEU Bargaining Claims Check inhttps://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc8mX0uXCb7tzgf3uQBd7RuXH817Kz7fq2hkhuFP-D460nHdA/viewform Bargaining for our collective agreement begins again later this year. We would like to hear about what you think our claims should be. For context, in the 2022 bargaining round, our claims were: A pay increase that beats inflation Living wage as a minimum Tupu increases to get staff to top of band more quickly Postdoctoral fellows to be brought under coverage Thank you for filling out this survey as a beginning of developing claims together. docs.google.com There will be many opportunities to get involved in this round of bargaining campaign. Stay tuned and let me know if you are interested in helping out.

Kind regards, Noel
