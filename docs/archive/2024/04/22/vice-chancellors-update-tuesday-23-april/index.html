Kia ora koutou,

While we pause to remember and pay tribute on Anzac Day later this week, much of our focus looks to the future.

Proposed new faculty arrangements

Thank you to everyone who has provided feedback on this proposal so far. If you still need to do so, or wish to provide further input, please note that the online feedback form [https://url.au.m.mimecastprotect.com/s/BgD4C0YKnXhWAAkxIwFLFa?domain=uoa.custhelp.com] closes at 5pm today. Following this, submissions will be reviewed by a Review Committee comprising both staff and students. Council will consider their recommendations and advice from Senate in June.

Universitas 21 2024

The University will be represented at the Universitas 21 (U21) Annual Network Meeting and Symposium this week. The theme for the meeting is 'Global Universities and the Workforce of the Future.' We are one of four universities selected to present case studies on this subject with out Centre for Innovation and Entrepeneurship to be showcased.

Flu vaccines

A reminder that, with winter approaching, flu vaccines and Covid-19 boosters are free to students, as well as permanent and fixed-term staff. Details on where these are available can be found on the 2024 influence and vaccination for staff and students notice page [https://url.au.m.mimecastprotect.com/s/oCf0CgZ0qQfWzzq3Io3yd0?domain=uoa.custhelp.com].

Ngā mihi nui,
