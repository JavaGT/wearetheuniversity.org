Inaugural lecture of Professor Ken McNatty

Wednesday, 9 May 2007, 12:18 am
Press Release: Victoria University of Wellington

Inaugural lecture of Professor Ken McNatty

The mammalian egg and its development to ovulation and fertilisation

Professor Pat Walsh, Vice-Chancellor of Victoria University of Wellington, invites you to an Inaugural Professorial Lecture to be given by:

Ken McNatty, Professor of Cell Biology and Biotechnology

To be held in the Hunter Council Chamber
Level 2, Hunter Building, Victoria University of Wellington
Gate 1 or 2, Kelburn Parade, Wellington

Tuesday 22 May 2007 at 6pm

Contact the Information Desk on 04-463 5283 to register your interest in attending.

Refreshments will be served following the lecture.
For more information on the lecture please visit www.victoria.ac.nz/events
