Maori Party Roadshow Winds Up

Friday, 20 April 2007, 1:02 pm
Press Release: The Maori Party

The Maori Party Members of Parliament will be winding up their three week tour next week, with a focus in the rohe of Tainui.

They will be attending hui at marae in Kirikiriroa; Te Kuiti; Waingaro; Raungaiti; Pukekohe; and Papakura; as well as a series of visits to iwi and Maori providers.

There will be open forums (which public and media can attend) at

- Te Rangiora Marae in Kirikiriroa (Monday, 6.30pm);

- Te Kopua Marae (Wednesday, 6.30pm)

- Te Rapa (Te Wananga o Aotearoa, Rangiaowhia Resource Centre, Thursday 9am);

Waikato University (Thursday 12pm).
