Studio 77: Theatre Programme’s Directing Season

Tuesday, 7 August 2007, 12:32 am
Press Release: Victoria University of Wellington

Studio 77 presents...

The Theatre Programme’s Directing Seasons kick off next week for another year.

Over the Semester we will present four seasons of short plays directed by THEA304 students under the guidance of some of Wellington’s best professional directors.

Book early to avoid disappointment as seats are limited!

Season One starts next Wednesday with -
Alienated
-Three Short Plays-
BY THEA304 DIRECTING STUDENTS

“HAVE YOU MET OUR RABBIT?” by Michael Stevens
“ANOTHER MOON CALLED EARTH” by Tom Stoppard
“KRAPP’S LAST TAPE” by Samuel Beckett

Wednesday 15th – Saturday 18th August 2007
7pm
At Studio 77, 77 Fairlie Terrace
To book: ph 463 5221 or email theatre@vuw.ac.nz
