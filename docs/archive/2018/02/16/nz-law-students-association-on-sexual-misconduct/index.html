NZ Law Students' Association on sexual misconduct

Friday, 16 February 2018, 4:26 pm
Press Release: New Zealand Law Students Association

Statement by the New Zealand Law Students' Association

We condemn any behaviour or conduct that makes a student feel unsafe or uncomfortable in a working environment.

We note the obligation of all employers to create a workplace where employees are protected from any unwanted behaviour.

The responsibility for providing a safe workplace lies with all employers. We will continue to work with firms and universities to promote safe workplaces
