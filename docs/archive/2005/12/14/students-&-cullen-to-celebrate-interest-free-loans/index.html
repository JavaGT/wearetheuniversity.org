Students & Cullen to celebrate interest free loans

Wednesday, 14 December 2005, 8:46 am
Press Release: New Zealand University Student's Association

Students to meet with Cullen to celebrate interest free loans

The Hon Dr Michael Cullen, Minister for Tertiary Education, will meet with student representatives on the steps of parliament on Wednesday 14th December at 10.30am to celebrate the passing of the Bill that removes interest on student loans.

There will be a photo opportunity at 10.30am on the steps of parliament.
