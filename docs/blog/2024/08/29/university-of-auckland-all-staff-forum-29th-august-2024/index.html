The below transcript is from an All Staff Zoom meeting at the University of Auckland. The meeting was held on 29th of August 2024.

Thank you to staff and students who leak information to us. You can contribute by emailing us at email@wearetheuniversity.org. We will protect your anonymity, however be sure to email us from your personal email account and not from a university email account. We will not publish any information that could identify you without your consent. Learn more about leaking to wearetheuniversity.
