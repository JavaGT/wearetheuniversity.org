Description

The Prime Minister’s Chief Science Advisor, Professor Sir Peter Gluckman and education expert Jacquie Bay of the Liggins Institute at the University of Auckland discuss the New Zealand government’s new plan to fight childhood obesity. They explain to 3 New Health Reporter Lucy Warhurst the urgency of the public health problem being created by children on the path to obesity, and the importance of education about how to make good decisions about nutrition.

To support more breakthroughs on child health: https://www.givingtoauckland.org.nz/en/FundCategories/liggins.html
