Description

The Bachelor of Social Sciences is focused around what influences people and society to act in a particular way and how these actions can be monitored and modified. This degree offers contemporary and relevant areas of study such as employment relations, social policy, and environmental planning and is suited most to those independent thinkers with a curiosity about the world and the people who live in it.

In this video, Bachelor of Social Sciences (Honours) student, Moses Simpson, explains what he loves most about this degree. To learn more about this degree, please click here: http://bit.ly/1MUcov2
