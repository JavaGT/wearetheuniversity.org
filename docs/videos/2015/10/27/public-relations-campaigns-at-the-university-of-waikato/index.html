The University of Waikato

Description

Waikato Management School will set you up for a bright business future. In the Public Relations major, our students harness the power of words and creativity to develop and implement a campaign for a real client in a real-world situation.

This year, the four teams of students launched campaigns on behalf of Sport Waikato with the goal of getting people physically active. At the Chesterman Group Public Relations Campaign competition, these students presented their ideas and results to the client and a panel of judges.

For more information on studying Public Relations or Communications at the University of Waikato, check out http://bit.ly/1N3IULl
