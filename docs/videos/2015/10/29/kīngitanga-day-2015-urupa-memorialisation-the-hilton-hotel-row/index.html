University of Waikato Kīngitanga Day 2015
Urupā Memorialisation: The ‘Hilton Hotel Row’
Dr Vincent Malcolm-Buchanan

This study reflects on shifts in Māori memorial traditions that have seen increasing elaborations introduced to urupā throughout the motu, with a particular focus on recent changes in Pikiao urupā at Tapuaeharuru, Rotoiti.
