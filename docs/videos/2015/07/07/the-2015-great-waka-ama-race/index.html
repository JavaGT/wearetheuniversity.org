Description

See the highlights from the toughest race yet in the University’s annual Waka Ama event on the Waitemata Harbour. This year’s instalment saw teams paddle out and back from Takapuna beach to Rangitoto Island, including a steep 4km run to the top! The winning paddlers from Ngā Tauira Māori (Māori students association) will be traveling to Hawaii in September for an expenses-paid trip to compete in the largest outrigger canoe race in the world!
