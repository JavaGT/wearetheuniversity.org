Description

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series hosted by the University of Waikato. Running every Wednesday in August, the series provides an opportunity to examine interesting and topical issues in our community and take part in robust discussion.

The role of a university within its community is to be a critic and conscience of society and to share knowledge. The 2015 Winter Lecture Series kicks off on August 5 at 6pm, and every Wednesday after. All lectures are free and open to the public.
