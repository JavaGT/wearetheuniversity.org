Shortland St actor Matthew Chamberlain, who plays Murray Cooper, says his most memorable teacher moment was during high school when a slightly tubby, bald Englishman walked into the classroom, took one look around and reshuffled the desks to make the environment more enticing and the learning more engaging. Before this teacher, Matthew thought English was dry and dull. But that simple moment of rearranging the classroom helped spark his imagination and planted the seed to become a successful actor.
