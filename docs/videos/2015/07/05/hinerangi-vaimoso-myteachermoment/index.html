Description

Communications Advisor Hinerangi Vaimoso’s most inspiring teacher moment involves a special teacher from the UK who made an effort to learn te reo Māori. As a Māori-Samoan, Hinerangi often felt out of place at her school, which was predominantly pakeha-focussed. One day during a lesson about speeches, her teacher Mr Reynolds told the students “Kaua e whakamā” (don't be shy). Hinerangi said it was just a small gesture, but it made a huge impact.
