Description

Combining Parenting and a Career (CPC) seminars are for University of Auckland staff with parenting responsibilities, and are hosted by the Equity Office, HR and TEU each year.

Associate Professor Clare Wall, head of Nutrition and Dietetics in the Faculty of Medical and Health Sciences, recently gave a CPC seminar on the common misunderstandings about food allergies and intolerances and why both seem to be on the rise. These were her key points.

For more information, visit www.equity.auckland.ac.nz/cpc
