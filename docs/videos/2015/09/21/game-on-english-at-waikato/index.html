Description

A programme at the University of Waikato saw a dozen visiting Japanese teenagers improve their rugby skills and get a better grasp of the English language.

The Game On English programme was hosted by Waikato University's Pathways College and supported by the Waikato Rugby Union (WRU).

The programme was developed by Education New Zealand in partnership with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade and aims to improve the English language skills and sporting ability of Japan's young people.
