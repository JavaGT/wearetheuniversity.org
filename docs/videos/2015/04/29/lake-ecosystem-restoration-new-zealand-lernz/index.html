Lake Ecosystem Restoration New Zealand (LERNZ) is a series of projects and people (based at the University of Waikato) that aims to restore indigenous biodiversity in New Zealandâ€™s lakes. So far, their projects have helped reduce quantities of pest fish and harmful algal blooms and are helping local councils and iwi protect their fresh water sources.

This video showcases the success of the work of LERNZ director Professor David Hamilton and his team, and the ongoing importance of their research to fresh water quality in New Zealand.
