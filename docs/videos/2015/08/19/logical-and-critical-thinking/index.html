Description

A Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) by the University of Auckland. Join this MOOC now: https://www.futurelearn.com/courses/logical-and-critical-thinking

This free 8 week course explores logical and critical thinking and provides opportunities to practice those skills with learners from all over the world.
 
Lead Educators: Tim Dare is Associate Professor and Patrick Girard Senior Lecturer, in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Auckland. Tim’s research focuses on Applied and professional ethics, Legal ethics, Philosophy of Law, Medical ethics, and Ethical Analysis of Predictive Risk Modelling; and Patrick’s on Philosophical logic, modal logic, preference logic, belief revision and metaphysics.
