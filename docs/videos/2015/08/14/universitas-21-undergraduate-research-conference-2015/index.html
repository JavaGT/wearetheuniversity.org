Description

In July 2015 the University of Auckland hosted the annual Universitas 21 Undergraduate Research Conference. Hosted by a different member university each year, the conference brings together talented undergraduates from across the Universitas 21 international network.

The theme of this year’s conference was “People & Places” – a topic broad enough to encompass research from a diverse range of disciplines including the arts, science and medicine. Each of the delegates presented their research over five days of the conference, held at the City Campus and the University of Auckland’s Goldie Vineyard on Waiheke Island.
