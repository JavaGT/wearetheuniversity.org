University of Waikato 2015 Winter Lecture Series

TV personality and friend of the University of Waikato, Te Radar, presents a series of interesting and entertaining facts about Hamilton and the Waikato Region. More than just the home of No. 8 wire, Hamilton and the greater Waikato Region is the proud birthplace of several artists and innovators, and the tertiary provider of many alumni of have gone on to do great things.
