Description

Hamilton welcomes back top international university crews from across the globe for the annual Gallagher Great Race!

Check out world- class river racing on the Waikato River. International competition from Harvard, Cambridge, Sydney and Melbourne compete against local hosts, Waikato University.

Witness the rowing eights as they are challenged in the three-boat upstream race for 3.85km, finishing at Grantham St HQ.

An awesome spectacle, locals are encouraged to show their support from the river bank. A FREE family day out, join us at the finish line at event HQ at Grantham St.

For more information, please visit: www.thegreatrace.co.nz.
