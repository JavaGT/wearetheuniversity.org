Description

Healthcare delivery around the world has become more complex, involving many departments and professions at different levels and at different times. Healthcare providers need more leaders who can manage complexity at a systems and economics level. We need new thinking, to manage the complexities; a different approach to the same problems. Find out how you can become one of those leaders who can effect change when you study the Master of Health Leadership specialising in Health Management.
Learn more at http://www.pathfinders.ac.nz/healthleadership
