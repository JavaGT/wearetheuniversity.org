The University of Auckland has developed a Family Violence Project to support students and staff affected by violence from someone close to them, such as a partner, family member or flatmate. Everyone is entitled to respect, and to live free from fear and abuse. Find out more about support options at our University, including compassionate consideration and campus personal safety planning, for anyone on our campuses experiencing family violence. And learn how you can help if family violence is affecting someone you know.
