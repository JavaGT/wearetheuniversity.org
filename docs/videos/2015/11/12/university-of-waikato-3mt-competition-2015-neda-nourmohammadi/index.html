Description

In this video we speak to winner of the University of Waikato 3MT Competition winner, Neda Nourmohammadi, from the Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences. Neda's presentation was entitled “Shaping the Future: Reconciling the Contemporary Iranian Art with Dynamic Identity”.

From Iran, Neda is now 16 months into her PhD and is gathering data for her research.

To learn more about Neda's research, please click here: http://bit.ly/1M9hajs
