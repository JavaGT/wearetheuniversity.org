Description

The University of Waikato once again plays a key role at the National Agricultural Fieldays opens at Mystery Creek.

The University of Waikato has a strong connection with Fieldays with our first Vice-Chancellor, Sir Don Llewellyn, helping establish the iconic event in the 1960s.

This year marks our ninth year as a strategic partner and the agricultural expo is an important opportunity to showcase the University of Waikato’s research and expertise to the community and show how our graduates are making an impact and solving the world’s problems.

This year’s theme is ‘Growing Our Capability in Agribusiness’ – an area the University of Waikato is embracing by highlighting its research and industry collaborations at its Premier Feature Stand.
