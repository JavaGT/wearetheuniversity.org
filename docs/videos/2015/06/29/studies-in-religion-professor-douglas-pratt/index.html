Description

Religion is a fascinating subject and one which impacts on the world in so many ways. The Faculty of Arts & Social Sciences at the University of Waikato has introduced an interdisciplinary programme of study called Studies in Religion which includes the new core paper – ‘Religion in Secular Society: Contemporary Perspectives’ – on offer in B Semester. For more information about this programme, please visit our website: http://bit.ly/1tO1jSV
