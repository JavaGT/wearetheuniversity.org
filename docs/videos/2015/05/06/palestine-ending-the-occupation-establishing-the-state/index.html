Description

UoA Faculty of Law hosts Dr. Riad Al-Malki, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Palestinian National Authority during his visit to New Zealand.

In this lecture, Dr. Al-Malki argues that the recognition of an independent, viable and democratic Palestinian state by the international community is a pre requisite to achieving a just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East. He will explain how the continuation of the Israeli settlements constitutes the main obstacle of the realisation of the two state solution.
