Description

Winter in the Subantarctic: Antipodes Island terrestrial ecology is a short film about the history of Antipodes Island and the work undertaken by University of Auckland and Department of Conservation scientists in winter 2013 to prepare for the eradication of mice from the island as part of the Million Dollar Mouse campaign.
