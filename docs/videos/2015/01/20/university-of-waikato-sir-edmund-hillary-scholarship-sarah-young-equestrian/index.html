Description

University of Waikato second year communications student and Sir Edmund Hillary Scholar, Sarah Young, has recently been crowned the New Zealand Young Rider Three-Day-Event Champion.

Sarah chats to us about how the Sir Edmund Hillary Scholarship at Waikato has enabled her to maintain her studies while achieving her eventing goals and ambitions.

To learn more about this scholarship, please click here: http://bit.ly/gXutfs.
