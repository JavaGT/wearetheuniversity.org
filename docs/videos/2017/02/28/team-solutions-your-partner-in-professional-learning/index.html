Description

Team Solutions is one of New Zealand’s leading professional development providers in education. Team Solutions’ facilitators work in partnership with hundreds of school leaders and teachers every year, working collaboratively to help them address student learning needs and achievement challenges.
For more information on Team Solutions’ professional development services and expertise, visit the Team Solutions website: http://www.education.auckland.ac.nz/en/about/professional-development/team-solutions-home/professional-learning-and-development-services/customised-pd-services.html.
