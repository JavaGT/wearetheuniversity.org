Description

Professor Edward Humphreys, Chartered Fellow of the BCS - FBCS CITP, CISM recently spoke at the University of Waikato about how we behave, react and respond to psychological phenomena that are associated with or affected by all things related to cyberspace.

In this short video, Professor Humphreys talks about some of the things that relate to the human mind, cognitions, and behaviour in relation to our communications and interactions with cyber-based technologies, services and applications.
