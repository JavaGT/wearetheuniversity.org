Description

Get Good Done is a free two-week programme that helps students and staff of all faculties, levels and disciplines to identify and understand a social, environmental or cultural issue they feel passionate about solving. Participants then create a solution that they can unleash to the world. Find out more at https://www.cie.auckland.ac.nz
