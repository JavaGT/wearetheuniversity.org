The University of Waikato

Description

The University of Waikato’s Master of Digital Business (MDigiBus) is a specialised one-year masters degree that can be studied completely online or on campus, either full-time or part-time.

You'll gain advanced knowledge of how the latest digital and e-commerce technologies – such as big data, gamification and beacon technology - are transforming business operations across all industries. Develop the management skills to lead digital transformation strategies on behalf of public and commercial organisations. The professional stream features an industry internship working at a company of your choice for 2-3 months.
