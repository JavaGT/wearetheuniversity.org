Description

Meet Te Wai Coulston who will be representing New Zealand at the Harvard National Model United Nations Conference in 2018. Te Wai, originally from Gisborne, is studying a conjoint Bachelor of Arts/Bachelor of Commerce at the University of Auckland Business School. In the video he talks about how Māori values can be applied to global challenges, people who inspire him and aspirations for the future.
