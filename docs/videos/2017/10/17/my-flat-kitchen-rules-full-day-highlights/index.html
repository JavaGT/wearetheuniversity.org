Description

In conjunction with What's Cooking and the Dunedin City Council, the university hosted the first 'My Flat Kitchen Rules' competition which saw six student flats battle it out for a four-page spread in Dunedin's first cookbook!

Our high profile judges for the day were:
Ben Bayly (My Kitchen Rules NZ judge, head chef at The Grove and Baduzzi Auckland)
Claire Aldous (Editor Dish Magazine)
Jordan Luck (The Exponents)

The calibre of the finalists was so high that the judges picked two winners - Gihwan Song and Huw Ludemann.

Check out some of the highlights of the day which was hosted on Wednesday 11 October 2017.
