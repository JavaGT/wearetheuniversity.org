Description

Mai Nga Hau E Wha was choreographed in 2014 by Teuila Hughes and Mattie Hamuera (both Bachelor of Dance Studies graduates) and was performed by third year students in Macau and for Action, the Dance Studies end of year showcase.

From the four winds we all unite here in Tamaki-makau-rau, and together we celebrate who we are as one. We acknowledge the cultural diversity we have developed here in Auckland and honour the journey of our ancestors’ migration to this bountiful land.
Music: Mana (Part 1) - Siva Pacifica / Ote’a – Tiruvi (Toti's Tahiti) and Chant written by Theo Apera Peters.
Staging and rehearsal direction: Sarah Knox
