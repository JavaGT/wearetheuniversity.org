Description

Dr Tamatoa Blaiklock talks about his experience of the postgraduate paper Teaching and Learning in Medical Practice GENA823 through the Department of General Practice and Rural Health at the University of Otago's Dunedin School of Medicine.

This paper is based on key aspects of educational theory and provides practical outcomes for doctors wishing for better educational relationships with undergraduate students, registrars, other doctors, and patients. http://bit.ly/2x0InFw
