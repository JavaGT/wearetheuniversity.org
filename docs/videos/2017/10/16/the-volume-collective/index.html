Description

The Volume Collective is a social enterprise made up of University of Waikato students and graduates. The initiative is fully funded by their ever growing audience and any proceeds are directed back into the local arts community.

We spoke with members of the Volume Collective to get a feel for the type of work they undertake, what inspires them and the driving force behind this creative social enterprise.
