'Pie-Us-Together' is an Asian twist on a Kiwi classic. Ting is Malaysian but she lives with flatmates from English and Iranian backgrounds. As the flat chef, Ting is always looking for ways to incorporate different flavours to satisfy everyone in the flat. That's where a Shepherd's pie with curry seasoning and a kumara top came into play. Packed full of mince, veges and a fragrant curry flavour, this hearty meal is one that could be devoured by the judges and flatties with enough left over for lunch the next day!
