Description

Sometimes, simplicity is the key to a great meal - as second-year music student Jake McComb will tell you. His 'drunken steak sandwich' brings together simple ingredients, cooked to perfection and served between a couple of slices of fresh ciabatta.

Here's Jake's description of the meal: "This is the best steak sandwich you'll ever taste, and is the result of years of refinement by me and my mate will from back home in New Plymouth. A big chunk of thick cut fillet steak cooked medium rare and sliced up, toasted ciabatta, special sauces and my secret fried mushroom mix."

The judges were incredibly impressed with the simplicity of the dish which let the flavours of the steak and mushrooms shine. Claire Aldous (Editor of dish magazine) chowed hers down in two seconds flat.
