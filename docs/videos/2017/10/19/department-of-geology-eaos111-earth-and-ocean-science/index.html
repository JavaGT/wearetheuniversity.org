Description

Your introduction to planet Earth, inside and out. Journey with us through geologic time, from the origin of our universe to the present age. Along the way, you’ll encounter shifting continents and oceans, mass extinction, evolution, global cycles, and much more. Join us in EAOS111 to discover how it’s all connected. http://www.otago.ac.nz/geology/study/papers/index.html?papercode=eaos111
