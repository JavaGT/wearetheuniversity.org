Description

Being a student, sirloin and eye-fillet steaks are off the budget. However, with this epic kiwifruit marinade, Laura is able to turn even the roughest cuts into 'filet Mignon' in under a day. The secret is the acidity and sweetness of the kiwifruit which helps to tenderise the meat and add a tangy sweetness. Paired with paprika kumara fries and a handful of greek salad, this was a meal that had the judges singing her praises.

Laura learned to create this dish as a kid and brought it through three flats with all round success. She says "even though out meat is cheap, our tastebuds don't have to be". Well done Laura!
