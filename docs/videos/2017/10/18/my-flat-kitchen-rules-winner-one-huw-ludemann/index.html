Description

Nestled on the bank of St Clair surf beach, a group of eight students live out their surf and study dreams. Our judges visited their home lined with surfboards and wetsuits and devoured their infamous Mussel Curry (with a few cockles thrown in there). Being surfers, the flat mates love living off the land and foraged for the amazingly fresh seafood at Warrington Beach on the coast of Otago. These students have a distinct connection to adventure and nature which is reflected in the beautiful dish they served up for the judges. The whole flat chipped in to create the fragrant green curry which was cheap to make but went a long way. The judges were impressed enough to name them one of two winners of the My Flat Kitchen Rules competition.
