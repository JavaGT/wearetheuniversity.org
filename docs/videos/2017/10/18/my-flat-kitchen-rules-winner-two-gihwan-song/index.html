Description

Influenced by his friends who come from a range of different cultures and ethnicities, Gihwan Song's culinary inspiration is drawn from friends and family closest to him. Being Korean himself and having friends from Thai, Indian and Kiwi backgrounds, Gihwan created a dish for our judges which reflects the diversity found in Dunedin's Studentsville. Korean BBQ Lamb rump with creamy pea puree and mediterranean veges, with a side dish of Asian risotto with crumbed mushroom and parmesan crisps. Wow. That's a mouthful. Added to that impressive lline-up, the presentation of the dish was impeccable and the taste was to die for. Crowing Gihwan as one of the My Flat Kitchen Rules winners was a no brainer for the judges - as you'll be able to see in these highlights.
