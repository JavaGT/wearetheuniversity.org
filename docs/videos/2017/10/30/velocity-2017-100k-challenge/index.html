Description

Velocity is New Zealand’s leading student-led entrepreneurial development programme. The Velocity programme’s flagship competition, the $100k Challenge, gives participants the opportunity to create and develop an idea for a venture. Those entrants who make it through to the shortlist will win a place in the Velocity Launch Pad Programme where they will receive mentoring and workshops to discover how to translate ideas into reality. The finalists additionally compete for the $100k prize pool for seed-capital and support in growing their venture. Learn more about how you can be involved as a participant or mentor at www.cie.auckland.ac.nz
