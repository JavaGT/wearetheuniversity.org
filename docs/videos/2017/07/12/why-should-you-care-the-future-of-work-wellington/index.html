Description

The first instalment of the Winter Symposium Series for 2017. Held at Parliament Theatrette on Wednesday 5 July 2017.

The working title for this year’s symposium is Why Should You Care?

Wellington's topic - The Future of Work

Panellists:

Professor David Baxter, of the School of Physiotherapy, is Professor and Director of the New Zealand Ageing Well National Science Challenge.

Dr Andrew Coleman holds joint positions in the Department of Economics and the New Zealand Productivity Commission.

Professor Richard Porter is Head of the Department of Psychological Medicine, Christchurch, and a consultant psychiatrist with the Canterbury District Health Board.

Dr Sara Walton, of the Department of Management, researches and teaches in the area of business, sustainability and the natural environment.
