Description

Dr Ben Albert and Professor Wayne Cutfield from the University of Auckland-based Liggins Institute outline their new research showing that fresh fish oil given to overweight pregnant rats prevented their offspring from developing a major diabetes risk factor.

Their full report is available at http://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-017-05793-0.
