Description

Pharmacists do more than just dispense prescriptions. Pharmacists are experts on medicines, helping people gain more benefit with less harm.

This video was put together by recent pharmacy graduates to provide people with a better idea about the increasingly diverse roles for pharmacists in New Zealand. We would like to acknowledge the people who generously gave their time to make this video.

You can learn more about the pharmacists featured in the video on our School of Pharmacy website at: https://www.fmhs.auckland.ac.nz/en/sop/about/pharmacy-as-a-career.html.

He Rau Aroha scholarships are offered to Māori and Pacific students studying pharmacy to help address the need within our communities for more Māori and Pacific pharmacists. Find out more at:
https://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/for/current-students/cs-scholarships-and-awards/cs-search-for-scholarships-and-awards/he-rau-aroha-scholarships-for-the-bachelor-of-optometry-and-bachelor-of-pharmacy-885-fmhs.html.

Find out more about studying for a Bachelor of Pharmacy degree at the School of Pharmacy at the University of Auckland at: https://www.fmhs.auckland.ac.nz/en/sop/for/future-undergraduates.html.
