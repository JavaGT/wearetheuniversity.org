Description

The University of Auckland is a provider of the Teacher Education Refresh Programme for the Education Council New Zealand. This programme is designed for primary and secondary teachers who have not achieved a full practicing certificate within a six year period. It is suitable for teachers who have had an extended break away from the NZ education system and overseas trained teachers.
