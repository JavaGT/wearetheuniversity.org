Description

The University of Waikato was pleased to host accomplished security technologist, Eric Hibbard, at their International Organisation for Standardisation (ISO) cyber security event on campus this month.

Eric's experience and expertise span operating systems, storage systems, networking, cryptography, cloud computing, and numerous other technologies. He has also worked in NASA's Ames Research Center.

Today Eric speaks to us about his association with NASA and his role in the development of internet in New Zealand which was made right here at the University of Waikato.
