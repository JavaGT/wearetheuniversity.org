Choreography: Sarah Foster-Sproull in collaboration with the 3rd year Dance Studies students

"Image Test 4: Quae Memoria explores the creation and deconstruction of a group, like cells splitting and reforming in new configurations. On a humanist level it also plays with tensions built and dissipated between women, alongside structures of support and nurture.

Special acknowledgments to Sarah Knox, and Amelia Chong, for creative support and rehearsal direction through the development process and beyond, and Andrew Foster for reworking the sound score to suit this new iteration of the work.
