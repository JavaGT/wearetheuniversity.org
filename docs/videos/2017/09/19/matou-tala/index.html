Description

O a tatou tupuaga, o le tatou faasinomaga lea.
The stories painted by our ancestors, ones that have become the roots of our being.

Matou Tala was performed in Macau in July 2016.

Choreography Teuila Rankin in collaboration with the dancers
Performed by: Tarryn Ata, Xavier Breed, Shannon Brosnan, Millie Burden, Alana Drake, Lara-Ann Jadoo, Cindy Jang, Katie Lavan, Sofia McDonald, Jasmine Ofamo’oni, Morgan Smith and Taitanyk Toniu
Music: Tiavaru – Jon Jonassen, ‘Ote’a Nahiti Na Manu ‘Erua – Mahealani Uchiyama, Ei Mua Ei Muri – Jon Jonassen, ‘Ote’a Heivari’i – Mahealani Uchiyama edited by Tonina Ngatai
Staging and rehearsal direction: Sarah Knox
