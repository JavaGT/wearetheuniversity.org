Description

By 2019, the Bay of Plenty will have a new, University-led tertiary campus in the heart of Tauranga city and students will have even greater study options in the region. The new campus, which the University of Waikato is leading on behalf of the Bay of Plenty Tertiary Partnership, will enhance the University’s current range of qualifications and study options available to students in the Bay of Plenty.

To learn more please visit http://www.waikato.ac.nz/tauranga-campus/.
