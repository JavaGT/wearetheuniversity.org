Description

'Fifty Million Frenchmen' from The Tale of the Oyster (1929) by Cole Porter

Violet Hildegarde: Courtney Summers (Finalist)
Pianist: Richard Marrett

The Graham Allen Musical Theatre Prize Concert is a gala evening showcasing voice students performing their favourite songs from musical theatre repertoire. The students compete for the Graham Allen Musical Theatre prizes, established in 2012, which support and encourage vocal students with an interest in musical theatre.

Special thanks to the friends and family of Graham Allen, The Evelyn Harrison Trust, adjudicators Jane Keller and Richard Marrett, BounceNZ, and voice programme coordinator Dr Te Oti Rakena.

Recorded at the School of Music Theatre on 7 October 2017 and edited by Kimberley Annan.
