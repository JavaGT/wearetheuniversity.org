Description

The Reading of the Oath Ceremony for the graduands of the Otago Medical School (the Dunedin, Christchurch and Wellington Schools of Medicine) was held prior to the students graduation ceremony on Saturday 9th December 2017.

This oath, spoken in unison, is a public commitment, a declaration of the values our new doctors, which they vow to adhere to during their lives as health professionals.

Distinctions, awards and prizes are announced first, and the reading can be seen from 9:00 mins.
