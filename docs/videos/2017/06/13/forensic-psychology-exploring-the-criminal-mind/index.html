Description

Our group of world-leading experts work to understand crime and to answer crucial questions: Who commits crimes? What situations make crimes more likely to happen? How can we obtain information from eyewitnesses without contaminating their memories? Questions such as these are at the heart of the exciting interdisciplinary area called Crime Science.

In Forensic psychology, we focus on all the ways in which psychology can help us to understand factors that influence crime commission and how to prevent crimes.
