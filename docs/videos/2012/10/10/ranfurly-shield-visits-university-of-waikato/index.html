Description

The Ranfurly Shield was on display at The University of Waikato this week ahead of the Waikato vs Hawke's Bay Ranfurly Shield challenge. Dozens of staff and students gathered for their chance of a photo with the prized Log o' Wood.

The rugby union are taking the shield to different locations around the region before Waikato put it up for grabs at their game with Hawke's Bay, this weekend.

The visit was particularly fitting as the Waikato Rugby Union and the University of Waikato work together to help foster rugby talent in the region. This includes the provision of scholarships to encourage young rugby talent to attend Waikato University, which helps the WRU provide attractive packages for promising players, and connects the University further with this part of our community.

As well as aspiring to play for Waikato and the Chiefs, many Waikato University students play club rugby and are involved in regional development squads.
