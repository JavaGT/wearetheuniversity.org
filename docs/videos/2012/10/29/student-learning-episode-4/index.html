Description

Well it's that time of year again folk - exams. In this episode, we catch up with University of Waikato Student Learning Senior Tutor, Katherine Brown.

With university exams just around the corner, it is helpful to make an appointment to speak to the Student Learning Support Team in order to overcome any problems related to your study and practical time management. Make the most of this wonderful university service.

For more information and appointment forms, please see http://bit.ly/Op0dKz.
