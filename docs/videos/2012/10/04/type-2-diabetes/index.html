Description

More than a quarter of a million New Zealanders suffer from type 2 diabetes and that number is expected to double over the next 10 years. Director of the Liggins Institute Professor Wayne Cutfield and Health Sciences student Hannah Burgess talk about why people need to be more aware of the symptoms and take action before it's too late.

Find out more about the Liggins Institute: http://www.liggins.auckland.ac.nz
