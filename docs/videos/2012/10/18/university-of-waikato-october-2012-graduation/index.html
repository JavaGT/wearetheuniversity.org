Description

We caught up with a few University of Waikato students on October graduation day for a chat about their degrees, future jobs and the ceremony location move to Claudelands Events Centre.

Unfortunately the strong winds made for wayward hats and less than ideal filming conditions. Nevertheless everyone seemed to enjoy themselves and we were able to experience another wonderful Waikato graduation ceremony.

For graduation photos, please see the University of Waikato website at http://bit.ly/TdH3sI.
