Description

Have the talk is a month long University of Waikato MCOM333 campaign designed to encourage people to "have the talk" with their loved ones about organ donation.

The "Tough Enough challenge" was an obstacle/Fear Factor style course set up with huge prizes to be won for the winners and spot prizes for those getting involved. Featuring YounG-World.

To find out more about Organ Donation New Zealand visit http://www.donor.co.nz/
