Description

So it's that time of year again - end of year exams. To the international student, there's so much to think about: assignments, study, student support, exam timetables, visas and flights back home. But don't stress...

Here at the University of Waikato, we want to help make your exam period as successful as possible. International Student Advisor, Huy Vu, shares a few tips for flying through your exams successfully, along with some pointers on arranging visas and flights back home. Check out what he has to say.

For photos of recent student trips, along with information for Waikato international students, please 'like' the University of Waikato International Students' Facebook Page: http://on.fb.me/OhoqNK
