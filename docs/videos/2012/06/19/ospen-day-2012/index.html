Description

Watch the highlights from the University of Waikato's 26th annual OsPEn (Osborne Physics and Engineering) Day. Year 12 and 13 students who are high achievers in science came out to attend lectures and demonstrations, and take part in the Capacitor Car Competition. Watch as students race their self-constructed cars up a custom built 9m long ramp.
OsPEn day is held in honour of Dr Roger Osborne, who taught physics at Waikato University from 1970 to 1985. During this time he was responsible for organising and presenting a significant number of lectures for Year 12 and 13 physics students in the Waikato region.
