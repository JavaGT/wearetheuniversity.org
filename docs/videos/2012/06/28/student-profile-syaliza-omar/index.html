Syaliza Omar is currently studying towards a PhD in Biological Sciences at the University of Waikato, New Zealand. Syaliza's pharmaceutical analysis is focused on the high anti-oxidant activity in honeydew honey.

We asked Syaliza why she chose to study at the University of Waikato and how she finds life in Waikato, New Zealand.
