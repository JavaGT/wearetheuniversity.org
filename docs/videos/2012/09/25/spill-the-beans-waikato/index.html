Description

Spill the Beans is a public relations campaign as part of the Waikato Management School paper, MCOM333, to promote organ donation in New Zealand.

Campaign Coordinator, Becki Lake, explains the importance of organ donation in New Zealand and encourages Kiwis to discuss organ donation with friends and family.

Spill the Beans has a flash mob taking place at The University of Waikato this week, along with painted ambassadors on campus to promote the campaign and help spread the word.

For more information about Spill the Beans and organ donation, please see:
