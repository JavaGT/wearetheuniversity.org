Description

The University of Waikato recently held a community planting day on campus. With over 500 plants put in during the event, Environmental Sustainability Coordinator, Rachael Goddard, explains how the day fits within the university's long term environment and lake restoration plan.

For more information about environmental sustainability at The University of Waikato, please see http://bit.ly/NdD3Qm.
