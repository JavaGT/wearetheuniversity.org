Description

This competition, held during Postgraduate Month, is for higher degree (MPhil, PhD, EdD, DMA and SJD) candidates to answer the question "What are you researching?". The answer must be given in a presentation of no more than three minutes to a non academic but intelligent audience. Through this competition, candidates will gain valuable experience in discussing and communicating effectively to a wide variety of audiences, both academic and non-academic, including the wider community.

The winner will receive a $5000 research or conference grant. The grants will be placed in a dedicated account for the candidate through the relevant Faculty or School of Studies and must be used to assist in the candidate's area of research or to present a paper at a conference.

All applicants must complete the formal application entry form online. For more information about Th3sis in 3, along with application forms, please see http://bit.ly/Q6dmUR.
