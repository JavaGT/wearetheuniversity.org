Description

In this episode, we catch up with University of Waikato Student Learning Maths Tutor, Janet Harris.

Janet explains how she and the wonderful team at Waikato's Student Learning are available for FREE one-on-one maths and statistics tuition. They also provide group workshops if there are a number of people experiencing similar problems.

With university exams just around the corner, it is helpful to make an appointment to speak to the Student Learning Support Team in order to overcome any problems related to your study and practical time management. Make the most of this wonderful university service.

For more information and appointment forms, please see http://bit.ly/Op0dKz.
