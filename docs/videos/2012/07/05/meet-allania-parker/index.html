Description

Allania Parker is studying towards a Bachelor of Social Work. Having grown up in a family with a strong focus on helping others, she decided a career in social work would fulfil her passion for social justice and her desire to help others in a practical way.

"I love studying in a smaller campus -- you get to know the people in your class really well and have more time to talk to the lecturers.

"I'm from a small town called Waitara in Taranaki. I've always loved the feel of Auckland City so I knew I would really enjoy living here. I feel like the city changes every week with new festivals and concerts; there's so much to keep you entertained here. I'm discovering new places all the time and every suburb has something different to offer."
