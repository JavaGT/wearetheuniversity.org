Description

Conor Young is studying towards a Bachelor of Dance Studies, pursuing a life-long enjoyment of dance and sports. He is appreciative of the degree programme's mix of practical and theoretical subjects, including dance history, choreography and anatomy.

"I chose The University of Auckland because it's New Zealand's leading University. The Dance Studies programme is very diverse, and centres on developing an individual's dancing skills and creative abilities.

"The staff are experienced dancers from a range of professional backgrounds. They are well integrated with the dance community, both locally and globally, and provide students with quality teaching and numerous opportunities to participate in dance within and outside the University."
