Description

Anna Smith is studying towards a Bachelor of Science, specialising in Geophysics. She has always had an interest in geology and physics, and aims to become a meteorologist or seismologist.

"The range of courses in the Science faculty is great, and I love being able to choose courses that interest me. The lecturers are excellent, and Geology courses always have fun field trips! I grew up on a farm in Gore, near Southland. When I started studying at The University of Auckland I moved into Huia Hall of Residence. It was the best way for me to get into University life and make lots of nice friends in a new city."

Find out more: www.makeyourmark.ac.nz
