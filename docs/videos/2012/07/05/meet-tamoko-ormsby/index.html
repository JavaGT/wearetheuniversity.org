Description

Tamoko Ormsby is studying towards a Bachelor of Engineering. He is interested in energy modelling and optimisation, and ultimately wants to help Māori and Pacific communities with a modern take on sustainability.

"I'm from Ngaruawahia, home of the best mussel fritters. I'm now living in The University of Auckland's Halls of Residence, at University Hall. My experiences so far have been cool. We've got a bit of a whānau going on.

"Study is my main focus, but I'm involved in social groups like the Meat Club and SPIES (South Pacific Indigenous Engineering Students). Plus there are heaps of University Hall events. It's pretty mean."
