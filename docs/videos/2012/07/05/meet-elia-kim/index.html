Description

Elia Kim is studying towards a Bachelor of Commerce/Bachelor of Laws conjoint degree at the University of Auckland, specialising in Economics and International Business. Elia chose this programme of study as he has ambitions to work for the United Nations and saw economics as something that affects everyone, managing the flow and administration of both countries and households.

"I chose to study at the University of Auckland because if I'm going to invest my time and money studying at any university in New Zealand, I want to be at the best. Moreover, I believed that this University would attract the most talented students in New Zealand, and I would have the opportunity to meet other inspiring and talented peers to form strong, lasting networks."
