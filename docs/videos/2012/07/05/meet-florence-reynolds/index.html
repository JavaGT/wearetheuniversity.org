Description

Florence Reynolds is studying towards a Bachelor of Arts/Bachelor of Science conjoint degree at The University of Auckland, specialising in Psychology, Ecology and French. Florence moved from Wellington to Auckland to study because of The University of Auckland's great reputation, and for the opportunity to meet new people and gain independence.

"At first I was scared of Auckland as it's so big, but I realised my preconceptions were probably wrong, so I decided to give Auckland a go and I've loved it since. In my first year I lived in the University's O'Rorke Hall. I met heaps of amazing people and had a really, really fun year."
