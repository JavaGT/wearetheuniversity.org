Description

University of Waikato Vice-Chancellor, Roy Crawford, introduces the 2012 University of Waikato Winter Lecture Series.

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series run by the University of Waikato in August each year. This August the university will be hosting five public lectures held between the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts and the Novotel, Hamilton City.

For more information, please see the University of Waikato website: www.waikato.ac.nz.
