The University of Auckland places great value on the part our postgraduates play in generating new knowledge that contributes to improving our society.

Hear the Vice-Chancellor, Stuart McCutcheon, talk about the support we offer our postgraduate students in this important role by investing heavily in providing an environment of high-quality supervision, financial support and excellent research facilities.
