Description

They came from far and wide. Over 100 participants, nine teams with one mission - to conquer the University of Waikato.

Last week saw the running of the inaugural International Student Great Race, an event to celebrate the arrival of new international students to the University of Waikato, and the beginning of Semester B, 2012.

Teams were required to check in at 12 checkpoints across campus and complete secret challenges along the way. Checkpoints included iconic (and maybe not so iconic) locations around the university campus, such as the Uni Rec Centre and the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts. The challenge enabled the new students to become familiar with key areas around the university, while meeting new friends.

All teams smashed expectations and finished the race well inside the given time. The winning team, Aqua Stones, arrived back at race headquarters with a super quick time of 53 minutes and were awarded with amazing prize packs. Well done guys!
