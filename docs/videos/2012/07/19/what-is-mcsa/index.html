Description

Thanks for taking interest in MCSA! We are a student run club dedicated to bringing communication students skills and knowledge for their future aspirations, and providing networking opportunities with industry professionals! Gain valuable experiences with like minded people on trips, weekly meetings, internships, guest speakers, and special MCSA events throughout the year!

MCSA is also affliated with the Public Relations Institute of New Zealand (PRiNZ), and offers MCSA members special discounted memberships with PRiNZ for only $35, and you save $40!

Visit our website and join now! Or come along to our weekly MCSA Mixer at The Station from 1-2pm on campus to see what we're all about! :D
