Description

Do you know a Waikato adult student that stands out in class? Maybe an individual that has been a major contribution to class or the greater community, in fact? This person may be eligible for an Adult Learners' Award.

Adult Learning Week takes place across the first week of September. To celebrate this occasion, the university is awarding Adult Learners' Awards to outstanding adult students.

All the information around eligibility and application (along with award application forms) can be found on the University of Waikato Scholarships page: http://bit.ly/MHtkrC. Please remember applications close August 13, so make sure you get in early if you would like to nominate someone for this award.
