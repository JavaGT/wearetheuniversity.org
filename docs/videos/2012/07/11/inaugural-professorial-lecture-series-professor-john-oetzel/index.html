Description

Topic: Difficult conversations: culture and communication in teamwork and delivering health services.

Difficult conversations are uncomfortable but sometimes necessary. They can occur in the context of teamwork and healthcare services, particularly when people come from different cultural backgrounds.

Professor Oetzel will discuss what difficult conversations are and why they occur, how communication has positive and negative impacts on these conversations, and some of the core messages about having an effective difficult conversation.

This free public lecture will take place on Tuesday, July 17 in the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, from 6-7pm. We hope to see you there.
