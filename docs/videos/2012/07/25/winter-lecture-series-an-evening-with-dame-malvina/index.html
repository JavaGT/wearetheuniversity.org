Description

Senior Fellow of Music at the University of Waikato, Dame Malvina Major, will give a short history of her singing career, discuss voice technique and run a master class with two students.

Time: 6-7pm
Venue: Dr John Gallagher Concert Chamber, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato. Parking in Gate 2B, Knighton Rd.

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series run by the University of Waikato in August each year. It is a focused and relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for robust discussion about a topical issue or issues within the community.

The role of a university within its community is to be a critic and conscience of society and to share knowledge. The 2012 Winter Lecture Series runs each Wednesday evening throughout August, 6-7pm. All lectures are free and open to the public. For a full schedule, please see http://bit.ly/M4mxm8
