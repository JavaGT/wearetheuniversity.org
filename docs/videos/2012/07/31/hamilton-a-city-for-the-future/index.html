Description

Tainui Group Holdings CEO Mike Pohio will discuss the organisation's inland port plans and Hamilton City Council Mayor Julie Hardaker will talk about the city's 10-year plan. Facilitated by the University of Waikato's Professor of Environmental Planning, Bob Evans.

Wednesday August 08 - Hamilton -- a City for the Future
Time: 6-7pm
Venue: Novotel Hotel, Alma St, Hamilton City.

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series run by the University of Waikato in August each year. It is a focused and relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for robust discussion about a topical issue or issues within the community.

The role of a university within its community is to be a critic and conscience of society and to share knowledge. The 2012 Winter Lecture Series runs each Wednesday evening throughout August, 6-7pm. All lectures are free and open to the public. For more information please see http://bit.ly/M4mxm8
