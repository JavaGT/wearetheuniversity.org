Are you a Science or Engineering student at The University of Waikato? Did you know that there is a team on campus dedicated to arranging amazing work placements for our students?

We caught up with Waikato Work Placement Coordinator, Sue McCurdy, for all the information around work placements and how they are so beneficial for students.

If you would like to know more about work placements at The University of Waikato, please visit http://bit.ly/OhlnGS.
