Description

Join us for a conversation with University of Waikato alumnus and Wales rugby coach, Warren Gatland, on everything from the highs and lows of rugby coaching to falling off ladders. Warren is to be presented with a Distinguished Alumni Award from the University in August.

Date: Wednesday, August 22, 2012
Time: 6-7pm
Venue: Playhouse Theatre, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato. Parking in Gate 2B, Knighton Rd.

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series run by the University of Waikato in August each year. It is a focused and relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for robust discussion about a topical issue or issues within the community.

The role of a university within its community is to be a critic and conscience of society and to share knowledge. The 2012 Winter Lecture Series runs each Wednesday evening throughout August, 6-7pm. All lectures are free and open to the public.
