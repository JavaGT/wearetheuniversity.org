Description

If people of the Waikato were to become locavores - eat food produced locally and nothing else - then they'd quickly become bored and nutrient deficient.

Waikato University Professor of Agribusiness, Jacqueline Rowarth, Psychology Professor, Darrin Hodgetts, and local restauranteur and caterer, David Kerr, are coming together to discuss food issues in the last of the University of Waikato Public Winter Lectures, taking place on Wednesday 29 August.

Come along to University of Waikato The Academy of Performing Arts from 6-7pm, Wednesday, 29 August to hear this lecture and the opportunity to have your say on these food-related issues. We look forward to seeing you there!

For more information, please see http://bit.ly/M4mxm8.
