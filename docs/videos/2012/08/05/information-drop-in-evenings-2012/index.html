Information Drop-In Evenings this August - University of Waikato.

Interested in studying at the University of Waikato and would like to find out more information about any of the following?

Programmes of Study
Entrance Requirements
Scholarships
Accommodation
University Life

Come along to any of our information evenings nearest you, where you'll have the chance to meet individually with Waikato University staff, learn more about your study options and what Waikato University offers.

All information evenings will be drop-in sessions except for the Tauranga and Whānau information evenings, which will include presentations from staff, graduates and students. Some of what we have to offer includes:

Arts and Social Sciences
Communications Studies
Computer Science
Computer Graphic Design
Engineering
Environmental Planning
Law
Management
Māori and Pacific Development
Media and Creative Technologies
Science
Sport and Leisure Studies
Teacher Education
See what else we have to offer...
