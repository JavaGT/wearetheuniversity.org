Description

On Friday, 11 March, over 300 University students and staff came along for a night of Slamming Poetry. First place went to Logan Dobson who impressed with his rap style political poetry. The audience lapped up guest performances from Australian Slam Camp Luka Lesson and 2011 UoA Slam winner Moody.
