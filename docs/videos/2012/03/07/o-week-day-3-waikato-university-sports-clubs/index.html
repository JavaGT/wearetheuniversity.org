Description

Watch what's happening during University of Waikato's O-Week 2012. Watch what some of the University sports clubs are all about.

O-Week 2012 kicks off on March 5th and includes orientation events, entertainment, games, giveaways and much more. There is no need to register and most of the events are FREE and held on campus! O-Week is brought to you by the Waikato Students' Union - http://www.wsu.org.nz/.

Join the University of Waikato official Facebook Page http://www.facebook.com/WaikatoUniversity for the latest updates, news and photos from O-Week 2012. You can also follow us on Twitter http://twitter.com/waikato. We will be using the Twitter hash-tag '#waio' for our updates throughout O-Week. Make sure to also use '#waio' if you tweet too!

Find out more about O-Week 2012 events at http://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/o-week.shtml.
