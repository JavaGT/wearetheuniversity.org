Description

Mohammed Alkahtani from Saudi Arabia chose the University of Waikato to study towards a Master of Laws (LLM) degree at the Te Piringa - Faculty of Law after studying English at the Waikato Pathways College and doing a Graduate Diploma in Law (GradDipLaw). Watch the Arabic version here: http://youtu.be/YLc5efJHsug.

If you want to get in touch with other Saudi students in Hamilton, join the Facebook Group Hamilton Saudi Social Club http://www.facebook.com/groups/saudinz/.

Find out more about postgraduate study at the Te Piringa - Faculty of Law at http://www.waikato.ac.nz/law/graduate.
