Description

Her story is one of passion, colour, generosity and inspirational success. Annah Stretton's business, Stretton Group, remains loyally based in the Waikato and encompasses a chain of 30 fashion retail stores throughout New Zealand and Australia. Stretton's publishing arm which produce HER and PINK magazines gives Annah a voice to reach out across the country to encourage all women to achieve success and independence.

We caught up with Annah when she visited the University of Waikato to present a seminar at the Waikato Management School's Excellence in Practice Series. Here, Annah provides a few tips for the up and coming business person and explains why failure can be so useful in your business career.
