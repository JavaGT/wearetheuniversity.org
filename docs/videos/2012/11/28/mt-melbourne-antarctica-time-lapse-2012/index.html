Description

The University of Waikato's Professor Craig Cary has recently returned from a trip to Antarctica which was a continuation of the Antarctic Geothermal work he has been doing in the area.

The short time lapse video shows the university's ICTAR team on Mt Melbourne, Antarctica; a 2800m active geothermal site. Melbourne is one of three geothermal sites in Antarctica in which the team will survey and review to help protect these unique locations. During their time at the site, the team experienced 50 knot winds and a wind chill below -60 celsius.

To follow the progress of our ICTAR team at Antarctica, please see our 'Waikato on Ice' blog at http://bit.ly/Waikato-on-Ice.
