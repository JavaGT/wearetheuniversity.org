Well we're fast approaching the festive season folks, the time when we all kick back, relax and enjoy Christmas and holiday activities. This includes eating and drinking - sometimes a bit of over indulgence...

We caught up with the friendly bunch at the University of Waikato's Rec Centre for a few tips around staying trim and fit for the Christmas holidays. Whether it's cutting back on sweet things or taking a stroll every morning, our experienced trainers will help you to fit into that brand new Christmas outfit you've just bought....
