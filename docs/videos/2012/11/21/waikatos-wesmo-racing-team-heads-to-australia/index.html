Description

The Waikato Engineering Formula SAE Team has built an open wheeled single-seat style race car to compete in the Formula SAE car race, in Melbourne next month. The car has the motor of a GSX-R 600 (a Suzuki sport bike) in it and is capable of reaching speeds of up to 160kmh.

Formula SAE is an international competition where students must design and build their own car. This competition involves universities from many different countries, and competitions are held in Australia, United Kingdom and Japan.

We caught up with the WESMO boys this week to see how they were feeling in the lead up to the race. You can read more about Waikato Formula SAE Team right here: http://bit.ly/TeRaLN.
