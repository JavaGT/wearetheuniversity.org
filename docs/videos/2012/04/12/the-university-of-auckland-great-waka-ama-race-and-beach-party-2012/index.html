Three hundred people gathered on Cheltenham Beach in Devonport to watch 12 teams of University Students cross the Waitemata Harbour. Teams had to navigate their outrigger canoe from Okahu Bay in Orakei across the harbour to Cheltenham Beach in Devonport. Congratulations to Huia Hall, first team across the line.
