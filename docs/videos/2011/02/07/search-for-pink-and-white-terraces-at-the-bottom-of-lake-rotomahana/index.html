The video footage shows scientists taking sediment samples from the bottom of Lake Rotomahana to examine what that material is made up of. These tests will be able to show if there are any traces of the Pink and White Terraces within the sediments.

Video courtesy of Waikato University vulcanologist Dr Adrian Pittari.
