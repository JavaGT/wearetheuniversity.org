Description

Dr Marcus Wilson is a finalist in the science teacher/communicator category of the 2011 Kudos Awards. He comes from a family "loaded with scientists" but it was an enthusiastic teacher that directed him towards physics. And now, with similar enthusiasm, Dr Wilson is taking physics to the people.

Find out more about Dr Marcus Wilson at http://sci.waikato.ac.nz/about-us/people/mtwilson.
