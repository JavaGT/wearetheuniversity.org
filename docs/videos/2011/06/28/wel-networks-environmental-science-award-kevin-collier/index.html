Description

Dr Kevin Collier's work and research has contributed to many aspects of freshwater science and management in New Zealand. Dr Collier's current research is centered on the ecology of large rivers, and has included initiating a national survey of large rivers to investigate macroinvertebrate and functional indicators for assessing river health.

Find out more about Dr Collier at http://sci.waikato.ac.nz/about-us/people/kcollier.
