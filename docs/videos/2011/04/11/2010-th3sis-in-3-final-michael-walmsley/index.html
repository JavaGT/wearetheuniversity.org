Description

Michael Walmsley from the Faculty of Computing and Mathematical Sciences (Department of Computer Science) is the runner-up winner of the 2010 Th3sis in 3 finalists with his presentation titled 'Leisurely Language Learning - Computer Assisted Reading in a Foreign Language'. Michael received $2,500, to use towards research expenses.

Find out more about the Th3sis in 3 competition at http://www.waikato.ac.nz/sasd/postgraduate/th3sis/index.shtml
