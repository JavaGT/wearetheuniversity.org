Description

Associate Professor Jarrod Haar, from the Department of Strategy & HR Management at the Waikato Management School, explains what impact his research on 'Māori support at the workplace' has on businesses.

Kīngitanga Day is a celebration of the relationship between the University of Waikato and the Kīngitanga (Māori King Movement) - a one day opportunity for students, staff and the wider community to celebrate our distinctive heritage, histories and relationships.

Find more info at http://www.waikato.ac.nz/events/kingitanga/
