Description

Follow the 2011/12 Waikato University Erebus Antarctic team as they travel to Antarctica and then around Mt Erebus, Antarctica. See what it is like living on the mountain and see the Breathtaking beauty that is Antarctica.

For more information about the research Waikato Researchers are conducting on Mt Erebus please go to our website http://igniterra.ictar.aq/index.php

In the 2011/12 Antarctic season the University of Waikato is sending down 7 teams to conduct Antarctic research through the International Centre for Terrestrial Antarctic Research (ICTAR, http://ictar.aq/ ). If you would like more information about general Antarctic research conducted by the University of Waikato please go to http://nztabs.ictar.aq/index.php or check out our blog at http://www.waikato.ac.nz/news-events/waikato-on-ice/
