Description

The VC's XI v Northern Districts Māori cricket one-day match is a new event on the University's sporting calendar. Many of the players in both sides have represented their district associations and the Northern Districts A side. Keir Bettley made his first class debut earlier this summer and continues to vie for a regular place in the Northern Knights side.
