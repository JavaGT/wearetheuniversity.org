Description

Vanessa Byrnes from the Faculty of Arts & Social Sciences presents on "Directing directions with text and Shakespeare in NZ professional theatre" in the final of the Thesis in 4 competition.

Find out more about the 2011 Th3sis in 3 competition at http://www.waikato.ac.nz/sasd/postgraduate/th3sis/index.shtml.
