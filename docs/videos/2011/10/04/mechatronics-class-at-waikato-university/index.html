Description

The Mechatronics class at the University of Waikato undertakes various projects including water depth measurement, control of a model elevator, and control of model trains using microcontrollers and measurement of the length of tissue on a toilet roll using Lego Mindstorms. Watch the highlights of the class at work throughout the semester.

Find out more about our Electronical Engineering programme at: http://sci.waikato.ac.nz/study/subjects/electronic-engineering
