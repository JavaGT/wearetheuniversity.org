Description

The annual chemistry quiz, hosted by the Department of Chemistry at Waikato University, gives Year 12 chemistry students the chance to put their chemistry knowledge to the test in a pop quiz-style challenge.

Shown in the clip, the 'Demo' round included demonstrations featuring liquid nitrogen, exploding hydrogen balloons, chemical reactions that produce light and many more crowd pleasing experiments.

Find out more about the Faculty of Science and Engineering at http://sci.waikato.ac.nz/.
