Description

The University of Waikato and Bay of Plenty Polytechnic jointly hosted the Bay of Plenty Chamber of Commerce networking event 'Business After 5' on Wednesday, 05 October 2011. University of Waikato Vice-Chancellor Professor Roy Crawford talks about the unique partnership the University has in the Bay and how local business can take advantage of the growing research and education opportunities provided through this.

Every month different member organisations take turns to host 'Business After 5' at their own premises to showcase their products and services, while networking with other business people. This month it is the academic sector's turn with the university and polytechnic jointly hosting the event at the Bongard Centre in Tauranga.

Visit our website for more information on our partnership http://uwt.waikato.ac.nz/about/partnership.shtml.
