Waipapa Taumata Rau, University of Auckland, in partnership with Ngāti Whātua Ōrākei, unveiled a new marae, Ngā Tauira, on 10 August. It marked a milestone in the University’s history, celebrating the re-establishment of the historic Tūtahi Tonu wharenui.
