Discover Waikato and its vibrant hub, Hamilton, nestled in the heart of New Zealand's North Island. This region is celebrated for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and unforgettable experiences.

Immerse yourself in the subterranean marvels of the Waitomo Caves, dive into the enchanting world of Hobbiton Movie Set for a taste of ‘real Middle-earth,’ unwind in the bohemian charm of Raglan, renowned for its world-class surf breaks, or lose yourself in the beauty of Hamilton Gardens.

Waikato offers the ideal backdrop for adventure, relaxation, connection, and exploration.
