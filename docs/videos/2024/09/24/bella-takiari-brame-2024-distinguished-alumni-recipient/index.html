Description

A leader in strategic financial and treasury management, Bella Takiari-Brame, is committed to advancing Māori voices and diversity at the governance level.

An aluma of the University of Waikato’s Management School, Bella serves on the boards of Braemar Hospital, ACC, and The Lines Company, and is the Deputy Chair of Ngāti Maniapoto.
