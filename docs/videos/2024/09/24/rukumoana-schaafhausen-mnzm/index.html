Description

Professional director, Rukumoana Schaafhausen MNZM, is an influential member of her iwi (Ngati Hauā, Waikato) and a strong advocate for a future that embraces both prosperity and sustainability.

In 2024, Rukumoana was recognised by the University of Waikato as a Distinguished Alumni for her continued dedication to Māori, governance, and her community.
