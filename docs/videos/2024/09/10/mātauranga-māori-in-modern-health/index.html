Description

The Health Research Council awarded a $1.2 million grant to research the mental health benefits of hauora Māori wānanga, that highlights tikanga, mahi toi and mātauranga tuku iho. The project called Te unaunahi i whakapiripiri ki te ika nui a Maui, explores the transformative power of community-led hauora wānanga using Te Tai Tokerau frames of reference including concepts like hohou te rongo, te unaunahi, te pou eke, te kauae runga and te kauae raro.

Te unaunahi i whakapiripiri ki te ika nui a Maui will be based in the Hihiaua Cultural Centre in Whangārei Te Rerenga Paraoa.
