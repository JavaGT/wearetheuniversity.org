Description

The exterior whakairo at Waipapa Marae were replaced in January 2024. They are replicas of the old carvings, now removed, and were recreated by tohunga whakairo Arekatera Maihi (Ngāti Whātua Ōrakei). The iconic patterns and story have been preserved. Timelapse video by University of Auckland Media Productions.
