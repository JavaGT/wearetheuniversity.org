Introducing Rui, a student from China who embarked on her academic journey at the University of Waikato. Rui was drawn to New Zealand not only for its breathtaking beauty but also for the promising job opportunities the country offers. She chose the University of Waikato due to its affordable tuition fees and its prime location in Hamilton, conveniently situated near Auckland. Rui shares how this location allows her to explore the surrounding areas during her free time, enriching her overall experience.

One of the key aspects that stood out for Rui at the University of Waikato is the small class sizes, providing ample opportunities for one-on-one interactions with tutors. She expresses gratitude for the attentive teachers who contribute to a supportive learning environment. Rui discusses the significance of the internship opportunities provided by the university, which not only enhance her skills but also pave the way for securing a job as a relief teacher. Additionally, Rui sheds light on the well-equipped facilities, including the library and the gym located inside the campus.
