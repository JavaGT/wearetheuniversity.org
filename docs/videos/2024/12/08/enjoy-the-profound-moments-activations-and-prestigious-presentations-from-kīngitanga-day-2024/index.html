Ko Kīngi Tuheitia ki te rangi, ko Te Arikinui Kuīni Nga wai hono i te po Pōtatau Te Wherowhero VIII ki te whenua, rire rire hau, pai mārire.

Whilst this years Kīngitanga Day was ushered in with a tint of sadness as we pay homage to the late Māori King, Kīngi Tuheitia, we were also honoured to have representatives from Te Whare o Te Kāhui Ariki present to celebrate its 15th Anniversary. “Āke, Ake, Ake” was this years theme speaking not only to the resilience of the Kīngitanga as an institution, but also acknowledging the University’s 60 years of establishment. Please enjoy these highlights that captured the profound moments, the myriad of activations as well as the prestigious presentations across both campuses.
