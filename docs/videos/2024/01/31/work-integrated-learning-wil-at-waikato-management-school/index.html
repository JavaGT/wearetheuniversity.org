Description

Discover how students at Waikato Management School can gain real-world business experiences through our Work-Integrated Learning (WIL) papers.

Hear from students and employers as they share insights on how WIL bridges academic learning with invaluable professional skills to foster success.

For employers, WIL is a great opportunity to tap into emerging young talent if you're looking for an extra pair of hands and fresh ideas to assist with projects. Email msc@waikato.ac.nz to get started.
