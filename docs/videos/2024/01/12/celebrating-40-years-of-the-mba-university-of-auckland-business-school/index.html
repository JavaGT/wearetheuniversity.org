Celebrating 40 years of the MBA | University of Auckland Business School [2:48]

Watch on Youtube

University of Auckland Business School

Description

A special gathering of alumni, students and staff celebrated the 40th anniversary of our prestigious MBA programme on 20 October 2023.

Past MBA students from cohorts spanning the last four decades came together to reminisce and reflect on the last 40 years, connecting with staff past and present.

MBA Director, Associate Professor Mike Lee, emceed the night, and alumni guest speakers from each decade shared their memories of the MBA and the impact it had on their lives.
