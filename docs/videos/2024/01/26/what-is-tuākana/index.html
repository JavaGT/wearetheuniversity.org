What is Tuākana? [4:52]

Watch on Youtube

University of Auckland | Waipapa Taumata Rau

Description

Tuākana programmes are across all Faculties and Te Tumu Herenga | Library and Learning Services and is where Māori and Pacific thrive on campus. You will find whakawhanaungatanga, tutorials, wānanga, fonotaga, workshops and dedicated spaces focused on indigenous success. The community is overseen by the Office of Pro-Vice Chancellor Māori and Office of the Pro-Vice Chancellor Pacific at the University of Auckland.

Find out more about Tuākana Learning Communities at auckland.ac.nz/tuakana
