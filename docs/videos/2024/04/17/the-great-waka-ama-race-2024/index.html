Description

The Great Waka Ama Race combines paddling and running across Tāmaki Makaurau's iconic surroundings. Teams paddle a course around Okahu Bay, run the length of Okahu Bay beach twice, before returning to the water for the final paddle section. The winning team will travel to Hawai'i to represent the Waipapa Taumata Rau, University of Auckland at the Queen Lili’uokalani Canoe Race in September. This 30km six-person race is the largest outrigger canoe race in the world.

Our 2024 event was a huge success, with the teams taking on the course at Okahu Bay on a beautiful sunny day in Auckland!

After a demanding paddle-run-paddle course, Engineering Eagles came out on top, pipping Arts Alligators to the finish line by a matter of seconds!
