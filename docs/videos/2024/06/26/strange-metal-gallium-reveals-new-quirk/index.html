Description

Professor Nicola Gaston talks about gallium, a remarkable metal which melts at room temperature. Used in an array of technologies, gallium is also an ingredient in the development of new materials and devices at the smallest – nano – scale. University of Auckland research revealed previously unknown aspects of the metal's structure.
