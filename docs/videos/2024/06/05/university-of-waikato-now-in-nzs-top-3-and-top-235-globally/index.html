Description

The University of Waikato has climbed to 235th in the 2025 QS World University Rankings, placing it in the top 3 universities in New Zealand. Waikato remains #1 in NZ for research citations per faculty member and ranks in the global top 100 for sustainability impact.
Vice-Chancellor Professor Neil Quigley credits Waikato's research-intensive environment and innovative academics for this impressive achievement, particularly as one of NZ's youngest and smallest universities celebrating its 60th year.
