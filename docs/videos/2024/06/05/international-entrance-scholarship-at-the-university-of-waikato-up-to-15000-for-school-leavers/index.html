Description

Are you an international student who is completing their high schooling here in NZ and looking to enrol at university? The University of Waikato is offering an International Entrance Scholarship for international students who have completed their high school within NZ! Valued at up to $15,000, this scholarship is designed to support talented individuals in their pursuit of undergraduate programs at either the Hamilton or Tauranga campus.
