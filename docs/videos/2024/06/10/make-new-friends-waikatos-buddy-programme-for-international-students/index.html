Description

Starting university in a new country can be amazing, at the University of Waikato, we want you to feel welcome and supported from the very beginning. That's where the incredible International Buddy Programme comes in!

The Buddy Programme pairs you with a friendly, experienced student who shares similar interests. They'll be your insider guide to thriving at the University of Waikato, both inside and outside the classroom.

Visit our website for more information:
https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/international/why-study-at-waikato/international-buddy-programme/
