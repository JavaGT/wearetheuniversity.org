Description

Starting university in a new country is an adventure! But wouldn't it be awesome to connect with other international students before you even arrive?

Goin' is our social network for international students! This app lets you connect with fellow international students from all over the world, before you even arrive on campus!

Goin' makes your transition to Waikato smoother and more enjoyable. You'll already feel like part of the community before you even arrive.

Visit our website for more information: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/int/
