It’s Never Too Late
Feeling like you’re ready for a fresh start? Our 12-week New Start Programme is here to help you make that leap.
Tailored for those who’ve been out of school for a while
, this program is all about building new skills, shifting careers, and reigniting your passion. Wherever you are on your journey, we’ve got the support and resources you need to reach your goals.
Discover what’s possible with New Start:
