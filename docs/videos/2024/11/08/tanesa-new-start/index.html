It’s Never Too Late

Feeling like you’re ready for a fresh start? Our 12-week New Start Program is here to help you make that leap. Designed specifically for those already in the workforce, this program enhances skills, shifts careers, and reignites passion. No matter where you are in your journey, it offers the support and resources you need to reach your goals.

Click this link to discover what’s possible with New Start: www.auckland.ac.nz/en/study/study-options/undergraduate-study-options/preparation-and-foundation-programmes/new-start.html
