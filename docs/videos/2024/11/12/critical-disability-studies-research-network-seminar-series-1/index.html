Critical Disability Studies Research Network Seminar Series 1 [1:58:34]

Watch on Youtube

University of Otago - Ōtākou Whakaihu Waka

Description

This two-hour seminar brings together trailblazing kaupapa Māori researchers in disability and health. It provides an exciting opportunity to learn from Associate Professor Tristram Ingham, Bernadette Jones, Associate Professor Meredith Perry (Otākou Whakaihu Waka l University of Otago), Dr. Kirsten Smiler, and Louise Were (Te Herenga Waka l University of Victoria Wellington).

In the first presentation, Dr. Kirsten Smiler and Louise Were shared their hopes, beliefs and love for their communities that guide them as Kairangahou and as Kaiaromatawai. In the second presentation, Associate Professor Tristram Ingham, Bernadette Jones, Associate Professor Meredith Perry discussed tāngata whaikaha Māori identity, intersectionality, and Invisibilisation.

Note: In these recordings, NZSL interpretations are only available for the first part of the presentation. We apologise for this.
