Hamish McKay is a former student of the University of Waikato and is now in the Forbes 30 under 30 for co-founding an e-commerce software in 2021 that let customers amend their orders after purchase. Twelve months later, he launched the software on the app store under the name Order Editing.

Hamish says the software gives customers the option to self-edit purchases and gives sellers the chance to upsell via a new channel â€“ saving on customer service costs.
