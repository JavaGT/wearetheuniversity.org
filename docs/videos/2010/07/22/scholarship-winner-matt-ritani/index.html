Description

Matt Ritani, originally from Wellington, was awarded a University of Auckland Scholarship and is studying for a Bachelor of Architectural Studies. He is currently living in one of the University's Halls of Residence.

Matt's scholarship covers his tuition and compulsory fees, a $5,000 annual living allowance, as well as two return flights home per year, for three years.

The University of Auckland awards more than 300 entry-level scholarships every year. Find out more at: www.auckland.ac.nz/entrylevelschols
