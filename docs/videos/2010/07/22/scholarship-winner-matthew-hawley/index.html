Description

Matthew Hawley, originally from Tauranga, was awarded a University of Auckland Scholarship and is currently studying for a Bachelor of Commerce and Bachelor of Laws conjoint.

Matthew's scholarship covers his tuition and compulsory fees, a $5,000 annual living allowance, as well as two return flights home per year, for three years.

The University of Auckland awards more than 300 entry-level scholarships every year. Find out more at: www.auckland.ac.nz/entrylevelschols
