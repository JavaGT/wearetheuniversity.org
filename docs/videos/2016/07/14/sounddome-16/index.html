SoundDome '16 [1:10]

Watch on Youtube

Creative Arts and Industries, the University of Auckland

Description

The SoundDome is 8m in diameter, 5m high, is fitted with state of the art audio technology, and 25 Genelec loudspeakers. It is designed specifically as an instrument for composing with three-dimensional space and as a relocatable venue for the presentation of innovative sound-based music.

SoundDome '16 presents two premier performances of groundbreaking acousmatic work (music for loudspeakers) by NZ composers John Coulter and David Downes, at the historic Kenneth Myers Centre.
