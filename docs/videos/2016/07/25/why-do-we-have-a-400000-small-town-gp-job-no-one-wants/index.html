Inaugural Professorial Lecture Series
Professor Ross Lawrenson, Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences

Professor Ross Lawrenson says it’s because we aren’t meeting our current medical workforce needs, and part of the reason for that lies in the way we’re training our doctors.

At his Inaugural Professorial Lecture Professor Lawrenson discussed the problem with the current medical education system and proposed some solutions.

For more information about our Inaugural Professorial Lecture Series please visit http://bit.ly/YYnOk2.
