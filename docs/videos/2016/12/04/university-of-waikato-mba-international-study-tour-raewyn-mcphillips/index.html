Description

One of the most popular highlights of the Waikato MBA is an international study tour, with 9-12 days of industry visits and cultural sight-seeing. This provides an opportunity for MBA students to learn first-hand about doing business in different cultures, and see how global theories work in practice.

This year's tour travelled to Delhi and Bangalore, India. We caught up with the tour to see how they enjoyed the experience.

For more information about this study tour please visit http://bit.ly/2g3yIqz.
