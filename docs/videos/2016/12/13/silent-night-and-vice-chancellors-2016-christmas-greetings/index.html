Description

On behalf of the entire University of Otago, Vice-Chancellor Professor Harlene Hayne offers students, staff and the wider community warm well wishes for the festive season. This year’s video features a moving rendition of the ever-popular Christmas carol Silent Night, performed at Marama Hall by the incredibly talented Lani Alo – an Otago music student.

To find out more about life at Otago, visit http://www.otago.ac.nz/otago-connection/index.html.
