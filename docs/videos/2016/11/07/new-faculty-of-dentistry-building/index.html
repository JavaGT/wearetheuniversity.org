Description

Learn more about the new University of Otago Faculty of Dentistry building project, featuring interviews with staff, students and neighbouring business owners. The new building will house services currently offered in the Walsh Building, including radiology, orthodontics, oral surgery, undergraduate clinics and paediatric dentistry. The remainder of the Walsh Building (8000sq m) will house teaching spaces, academic support, academic offices and research laboratories.
