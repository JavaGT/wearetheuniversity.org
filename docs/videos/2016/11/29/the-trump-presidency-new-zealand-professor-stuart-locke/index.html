How will the Trump presidency affect New Zealand?

We caught up with University of Waikato's Professor Stuart Locke, for a chat around his thoughts on how Trump's presidency will impact on New Zealand business and finance. Professor Locke believes Trump's presidency will be a good thing for financial markets. What do you think?
