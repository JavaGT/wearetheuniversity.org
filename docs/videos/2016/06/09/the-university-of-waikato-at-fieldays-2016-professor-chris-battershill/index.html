Description

The University of Waikato will once again host a stand at the New Zealand National Agricultural Fieldays – the largest agricultural event in the southern hemisphere.

This year our stand’s theme ‘Natural Capital and Environmental Enhancement’ will show how our partnerships and collaboration with industry are enabling practical research that will help solve the world’s big problems.

We spoke to Chair in Coastal Science, Professor Chris Battershill, about the algal agricultural system he will be showcasing at this year's event.

The 2016 Fieldays event takes place at Mystery Creek, Hamilton, from Wednesday 15 June – Saturday 18 June. http://bit.ly/On756R
