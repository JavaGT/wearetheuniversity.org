Description

New Zealand Champion Wakeboarder and Sir Edmund Hillary Scholar, Morgan Haakma, studies a Bachelor of Sport and Leisure Studies (BSPLS) at the University of Waikato.

We caught up with Morgan for a chat about how the BSPLS degree supports her passion and how the Sir Edmund Hillary Scholarship has enabled her to continue with high performance sport during her period of study.

To learn more about studying a Bachelor of Sport and Leisure Studies at the University of Waikato please visit: http://bit.ly/28JbZeE.
