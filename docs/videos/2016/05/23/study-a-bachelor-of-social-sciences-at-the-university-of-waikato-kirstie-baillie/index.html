Description

We caught up with University of Waikato Sir Edmund Hillary scholar and Bachelor of Social Sciences (Honours) graduate, Kristie Baillie, for a chat about why she chose the Bachelor of Social Sciences at Waikato.

Kristie gave us an insight into the volunteer work she has undertaken as a student under the Sir Edmund Hillary Scholarship programme and the work she plans to undertake in Nepal.
