Description

There’s no denying that technology disrupts business in a variety of ways. As such, organisations need to be able to change and adapt their practices in order to remain relevant and engaged in today’s business environment.

The University of Waikato’s one-year Master of Electronic Business is unique in structure, and features an internship programme that offers students an insight into the nature of e-commerce in modern business. This is a flexible degree that is available on campus or online, and enables the student to tailor their programme to their interest areas. Apply now for our July Intake! http://bit.ly/1WrqCZt
