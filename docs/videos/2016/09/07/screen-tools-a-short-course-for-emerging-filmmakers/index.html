Description

Study screen production in New Zealand at the University of Auckland. Running from 26 June - 21 July 2017, Screen Tools is a four-week short course that will give you practical production experience. You will work in a team following an industry-style production schedule to produce the pilot episode of a serial drama. The course is taught by John Callen from The Hobbit trilogy, and includes field trips to the Hobbiton Movie Set and the Academy AwardÂ® winning Weta Workshop.
