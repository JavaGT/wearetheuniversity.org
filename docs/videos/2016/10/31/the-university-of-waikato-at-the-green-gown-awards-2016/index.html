Description

The Green Gown Awards Australasia is the only Award Scheme dedicated to recognising excellence in sustainability within the tertiary education sector. The Awards are a prestigious recognition of sustainability best practice within the tertiary education sector.

The University of Waikato is a finalist in two categories: Continuous Improvement and Student Engagement.
