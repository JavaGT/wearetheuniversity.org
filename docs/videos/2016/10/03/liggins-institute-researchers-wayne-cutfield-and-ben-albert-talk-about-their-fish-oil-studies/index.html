Description

Feeding highly oxidised fish oil to pregnant rats caused almost 30% of their newborns to die in a 2016 study. Researchers Dr Ben Albert and Professor Wayne Cutfield of the Liggins Institute, University of Auckland, discuss the findings, and their earlier study, which found most fish oil supplements sold in New Zealand are oxidised ("off", or rancid) to varying degrees (four other independent studies in North America, Europe and Africa have also found oxidation of supplements).
