Description

The High Expectations programme with St Paul’s College was designed to bring to bear cutting edge research by the University of Auckland professor Christine Rubie-Davies.

In this video, we examine the first stage of building high expectations in an educational environment – building belief among educators in their own capacity to create positive change in their students’ academic outcomes.
