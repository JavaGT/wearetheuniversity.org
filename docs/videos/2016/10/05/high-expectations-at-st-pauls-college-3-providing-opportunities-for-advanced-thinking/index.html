Description

The High Expectations programme with St Paul’s College was designed to bring to bear cutting edge research by the University of Auckland professor Christine Rubie-Davies.

In this video we examine what high expectations teaching looks like in action, with St Paul’s teacher Geraint Tagaloa reworking his teaching style to create space for his students to demonstrate higher-level thinking.
