A student exchange can be a life changing experience. At the University of Waikato, we're proud to boast exchange partners in more than 20 countries.

You can study overseas for one semester or a full year and the points you earn during this time will be transferred over to your Waikato qualification.

Why not learn more about how you can add credit to your Waikato degree while studying overseas? To learn more about our exchange options, visit our website: http://bit.ly/2ddisTD
