Description

Waikato graduate Dolan Cox explains why he chose to study the 12-month Master of Business and Management (MBM) degree straight after his music degree.

Dolan says the MBM has given him the capability to become a “world-class leader” in any industry by developing his professional business skills – such as strategy development, marketing and financial analysis.

“The MBM has absolutely inspired me to want to start my own business,” says Dolan. “I’d love to be my own boss, managing myself, so that’s definitely an option for the future that I probably wouldn’t have had the courage to take without the MBM.”

One of the highlights of the MBM is an Action Learning Project, where students are challenged to create their own start-up business venture from scratch.

Learn more about Waikato’s Master of Business and Management at:
http://www.waikato.ac.nz/go/mbm
