Here's an inside look at what it's like to be in our Te Ara ki Angitū: Pathways to Excellence programme.

In 2016, 20 students from South Waikato joined this new initiative. They each received a $5000 fees scholarship and every day they get access to a subsidised bus service and free mentoring support.

Find out more at www.waikato.ac.nz/go/te-ara-ki-angitu
