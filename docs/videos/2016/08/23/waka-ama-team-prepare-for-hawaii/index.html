Description

After taking out the Great Waka Ama Race in March, the Engineering faculty Waka Ama paddlers will race in the Queen Lili’uokalani Memorial Race off the island of Kona, Hawaii, against crews from all over the world. Competing from 30 August to 5 September, get an inside look at how they have been training through the winter months for the gruelling 30km race.
