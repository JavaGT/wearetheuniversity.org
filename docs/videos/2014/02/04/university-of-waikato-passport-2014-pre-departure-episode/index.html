Description

Welcome to the University of Waikato! We look forward to having all new international students here with us in 2014 and have put together this video to help you get started with your study and student life in New Zealand.

International Student Adviser, Huy Vu, talks about the important things you need to know before leaving your home country. This includes information about the important documents to bring with you, booking a shuttle to the University campus and student orientation events.

For more information, please see our website - http://bit.ly/M3votB.
