Discover the history of the University of Auckland's Reserves in Anawhata, Oratia and Huapai. Featuring interviews with Cate Macinnis-Ng from the School of Environment, Anawhata Reserve Historians John and Alan La Roche and University Reserves Coordinator Joanne Peace.
