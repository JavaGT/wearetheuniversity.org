Description

ChallENG is a series of four competitions for Part II Engineering students in 2014. The first ChallENG, designed by the Department of Chemical and Materials, was won in spectacular fashion by the team from Civil Engineering. The task? To build the highest and lightest structure using spaghetti, marshmallows, gummy bears, popcorn, skewers and chocolate.
