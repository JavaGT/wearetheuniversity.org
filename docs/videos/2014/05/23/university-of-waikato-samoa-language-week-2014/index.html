Description

We're proud to be supporting Vaiaso o le Gagana Samoa 2014 (Samoa Language Week) at the University of Waikato.

The University of Waikato has a commitment to building and enhancing the Pacific dimensions of our institution, and to represent a welcoming environment for Pacific staff and students.

Samoa Language Week runs from 26-30 May. To learn more about Pasifika at the University of Waikato, please visit: http://bit.ly/1qZuD8F
