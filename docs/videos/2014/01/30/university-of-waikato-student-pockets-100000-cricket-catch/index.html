University of Waikato Management student, Jatinder Singh, has become the second person to pocket $100,000 from a day out at the cricket after grabbing a one-handed catch in the crowd.

Jatinder caught the ball one-handed from Corey Anderson's bat in the 38th over of the second one-dayer between New Zealand and India, instantly earning a bumper pay day.

We caught up with Jatinder for chat about the big win and how life has been since.
