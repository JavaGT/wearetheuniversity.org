Description

Are you interested in studying or finding out more about property?
The property degree at the University of Auckland Business School introduces students to the industry and offers a wide range of specialised courses to prepare them to enter their chosen property profession.
The degree offers students a strong support network from the department, opportunities to interact with industry professionals, career opportunities in New Zealand and overseas, and various social activities and clubs.
Take a look at what some of our graduates and supporters have to say about the degree, and the benefits and opportunities presented to them through studying property at the University of Auckland.

Interested? Find out more here: http://www.bprop.auckland.ac.nz/
