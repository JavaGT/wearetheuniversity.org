University of Waikato Bachelor of Laws student, Michelle Chen, has recently been awarded $7000 in the inaugural round of the Prime Minister's Scholarships for Asia.

Michelle will go to Shanghai in February to study law and Chinese culture papers for a semester at Shanghai International Studies University. Michelle says the exchange will open her eyes to potential career opportunities once she has finished her degree.
