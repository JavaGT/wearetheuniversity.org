Description

Fergus Lee was working full-time when he started the two-year programme in 2012 and the following year accepted a promotion to Head of Capital Solutions at ASB Bank. The MBA gave him greater awareness and knowledge of other parts of the organisation and improved his business acumen overall.

Find out if the University of Auckland MBA is right for you. The Graduate School of Management runs information sessions about the Postgraduate Diploma of Business and MBA programmes at 5.30pm on the first Wednesday of every month.
