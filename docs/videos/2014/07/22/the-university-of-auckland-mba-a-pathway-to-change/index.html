Description

Janet McBain had carved a successful career in the hospitality industry and was managing three hotels with 200 staff when she signed up for the Executive MBA at the University of Auckland. By completing the programme, Janet proved to herself that she has strong management competencies and her decision-making is really robust. She has now started her own business, which is proving successful.

Find out if the University of Auckland MBA is right for you. The Graduate School of Management runs information sessions about the Postgraduate Diploma of Business and MBA programmes at 5.30pm on the first Wednesday of every month.

Find out more: http://mba.auckland.ac.nz
