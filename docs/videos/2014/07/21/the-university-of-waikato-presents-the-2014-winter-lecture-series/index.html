Description

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series run by the University of Waikato in August. It is a focused and relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for robust discussion about a topical issue or issues within the community.

The 2014 Winter Lecture Series kicks off on 6 August 2014 and runs each Wednesday evening throughout August, 6:00pm-7:00pm. All the lectures will take place in the Playhouse, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato, Knighton Road, Hamilton. Parking is available in Gate 2B, Knighton Road.

All lectures are free and open to the public.

To read more about these lectures (and watch teaser videos) please visit: http://bit.ly/10VuBQA.
