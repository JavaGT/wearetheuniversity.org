Description

The 2014 Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series offered in August. It's a relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for learning and heated discussion on various topics.

Join Computing and Mathematical Science Senior Lecturer and head of the University of Waikato's new Cyber Security Lab, Dr Ryan Ko, on Wednesday, 13 August, to learn how you can enhance your digital safety today and in the future.

Dr. Ko will discuss the cyber security market and how we as users can avoid high risk situations.

NetSafe Chief Executive and digital safety advocate Martin Cocker and online legal expert and Faculty of Law Senior Lecturer Wayne Rumbles will also be present during the lecture, share their knowledge and give advice on the subject matter.

For more information on The Winter Lecture Series, visit: http://bit.ly/10VuBQA

This lecture is free and open to the public.
Time: 6:00pm-7:00pm
Venue: The Playhouse, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato, Knighton Road, Hamilton. Parking in Gate 2B, Knighton Road.
