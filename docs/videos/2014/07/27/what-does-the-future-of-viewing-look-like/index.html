Description

The 2014 Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series offered in August. It's a relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for learning and heated discussion on various topics.

On Wednesday, 20 August (6 - 7pm) the public is welcome to join for a lecture on the future of film - viewing and film - going.

University of Waikato Screen and Media department's Associate Professor Geoff Lealand will discuss the topic with a panel of entertainment experts. You don't want to miss it.

For more information on The Winter Lecture Series, visit: http://bit.ly/10VuBQA

The lecture will take place in the Playhouse, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato, Knighton Road, Hamilton. Parking is available in Gate 2B, Knighton Road.
