Description

The 2014 Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series offered in August. It's a relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for learning and heated discussion on various topics.

This year's lecture series kicks off on Wednesday, 6 August on the topic of business and the future of businesses.

Waikato Management School sustainability academics Professor Juliet Roper and Associate Professor Eva Collins together with alumnus Chris Insley (independent director of 37 Degrees South) and online entrepreneur Steve Crowhurst ( owner of online menswear store Fallenfront.co.nz) will discuss the importance of sustainability and how business practices will change in the years to come.

For more information on The Winter Lecture Series, visit: http://bit.ly/10VuBQA

This lecture is free and open to the public.
Time: 6:00pm-7:00pm
Venue: The Playhouse, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato, Knighton Road, Hamilton. Parking in Gate 2B, Knighton Road.
