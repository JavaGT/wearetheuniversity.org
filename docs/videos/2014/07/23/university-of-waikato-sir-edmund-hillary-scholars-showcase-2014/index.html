Description

The University of Waikato Sir Edmund Hillary Scholars' Showcase 'Art Thou Arty' is a celebration of artistry by the University's Sir Edmund Hillary Scholars. The showcase will feature talented displays of music, dance, theatre, Maori performing art, graphic design, screen and media are revealed in this 90 minute celebration.

The showcase takes place at 7pm, Sunday 3 August, 2014, at the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts. Tickets are $10 at the door ($5 from each ticket will be donated to the Himalayan Trust).

To learn more about the University of Waikato's Sir Edmund Hillary Scholarship, please visit http://bit.ly/gXutfs.
