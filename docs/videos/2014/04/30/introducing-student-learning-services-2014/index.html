Description

We would like to introduce you to the 2014 team at the University of Waikato's Student Learning.

The Student Learning team offers a University-wide service to support students' academic learning needs. They offer individual consultations, drop-in sessions, doctoral writing conversations and workshops.

For more information about Student Learning services, please visit http://bit.ly/Op0dKz.
