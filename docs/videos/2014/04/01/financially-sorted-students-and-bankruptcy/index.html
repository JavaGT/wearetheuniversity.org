In this recent article (http://bit.ly/1jShiMi), it states there is an increasing number of students filing for bankruptcy to clear themselves of student debts. The University of Waikato Professor of Finance, Stuart Locke, looks at the many implications of this type of bankruptcy and points out a few things students should remember before heading down the bankruptcy route.
