Description

In this video we speak with Cambridge Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults (CELTA) graduate, Campbell Weal. Campbell undertook his qualification at the Waikato Pathways College and is now a tutor for the course.

Campbell explains why he chose the CELTA course and how it benefits those looking for a high quality English teaching qualification.

To learn more about CELTA, please visit http://bit.ly/WtJcAe.
