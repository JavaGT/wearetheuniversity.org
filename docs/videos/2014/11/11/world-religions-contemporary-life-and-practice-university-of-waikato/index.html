Description

This Summer School paper is a comparative examination of contemporary values and practices in the major world religions. This paper will involve a critical exploration of life-cycle rituals and beliefs, from birth to death, food and clothing preferences, festivals and other activities associated with what religions do.

To learn more about Studies in Religion, and studying at the University of Waikato this summer, please visit our website: http://bit.ly/1tO1jSV
