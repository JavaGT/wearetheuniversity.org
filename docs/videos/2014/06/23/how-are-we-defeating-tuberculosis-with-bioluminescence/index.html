Description

Dr Siouxsie Wiles is a microbiologist and senior research fellow at the University of Auckland, and recipient of the 2013 Prime Minister's Science Media Communications Prize.

Siouxsie and her colleagues at the University's Department of Molecular Medicine and Pathology and the Maurice Wilkins Centre for Molecular Biodiscovery are creating glow-in-the-dark bacteria in order to better understand how to prevent and fight diseases such as tuberculosis and hospital superbugs.
