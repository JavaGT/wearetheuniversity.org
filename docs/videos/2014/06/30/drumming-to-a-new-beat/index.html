Description

During the 26 years that Raenette has been teaching, she has developed a keen interest in helping children with complex learning difficulties.

Among the many teaching strategies she has for these students, one of her most powerful is the use of Drumbeat - a hand-drumming programme that promotes social interaction skills. Through the beat of a drum, Raenette has brought many children out of their shells and encouraged them to reach for the stars. Her inspirational and moving stories are endless.
