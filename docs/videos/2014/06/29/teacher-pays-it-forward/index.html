Papatoetoe High School's Assistant Head of Mathematics Ranee Prasad says her successful career is greatly owed to her own inspirational statistics teacher Gavin Hunter who "made things click" when she was a student at the same high school four years ago. Now, she is using his unique teaching style to pay it forward for her own students.
