Description

Five years ago Daniel swapped his electrical tools for paintbrushes, glitter and musical instruments. Why? To follow his passion to teach young children.

He is now a teacher at Mangere Bridge Kindergarten, part of the Auckland Kindergarten Association, and is deeply enthusiastic about challenging children's ideas and how they think the world works.
