Description

English teacher Kineret Yardena is pushing the boundaries of innovation with her students at St Cuthbert's College in Auckland.

A recent project saw her teenage students share their personal life journeys with residents at the neighbouring residential care home Elizabeth Knox, through powerful digital poems. The relationships that have been formed are influential and inspiring.
