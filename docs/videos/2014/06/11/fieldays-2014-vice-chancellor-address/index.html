Description

The University of Waikato welcomes the opportunity to once again play a key role in the National Agricultural Fieldays.

The University of Waikato has embraced the theme of this year's Fieldays -- 'Managing Resources for a Competitive Advantage' -- by focussing on one of the key resources in need of careful management, fresh water.

University of Waikato Vice-Chancellor, Professor Roy Crawford, explains how the University contributes to the agricultural sector in a major way.

For more information on The University's involvement with Fieldays, visit: http://bit.ly/On756R.
