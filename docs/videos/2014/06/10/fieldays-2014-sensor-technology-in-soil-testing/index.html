Description

In this video the University of Waikato's Professor Geoff Holmes, discusses his research around sensor technology in soil testing. Professor Holmes believes that sensor technology software being developed at the University will enable farmers the ability to test soil on farm and produce instant analysis to determine regions within the farm which require specific attention and nutrients.

This research will be showcased at the University of Waikato's Premier Site, PF10-12 at the 2014 National Agricultural Fieldays. For more information, please visit: http://bit.ly/On756R.
