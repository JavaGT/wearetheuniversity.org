SHINING A LIGHT ON PRENATAL VISUAL DEVELOPMENT [2:03]

Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

We know that babies like to look at faces, but what happens in the womb before they are born? Professor Vincent Reid, head of the School of Psychology at the University of Waikato, is an expert in prenatal visual development – an entirely new field of research. His work with Waikato District Health Board’s ultrasound clinic could open doors into understanding human development and fetal health. He spoke about his research for National Psychology Week 2021.
