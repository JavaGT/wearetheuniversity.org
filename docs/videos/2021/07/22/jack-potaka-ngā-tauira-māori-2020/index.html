Description

Jack Potaka affiliates to the iwi of Ngāti Hauiti and Ngāti Tama. With a dream to pursue a career in Foreign Affairs, he talks about the reasons why he has chosen to pursue a conjoint degree (Bachelor of Laws and a Bachelor of Arts).
