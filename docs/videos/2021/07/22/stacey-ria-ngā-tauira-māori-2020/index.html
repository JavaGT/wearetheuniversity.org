Description

Stacey is a descendant of Rongowhakāta, Ngāi Tūhoe and Ngāpuhi. Inspired by her parents who are university graduates, Stacey pursued a medical degree. She has aspirations to return to her homeland in Gisborne - Te Tairawhiti and work as a GP.
