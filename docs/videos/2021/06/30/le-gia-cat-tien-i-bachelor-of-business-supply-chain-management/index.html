Description

Joining us from Vietnam, international student Le Gia Cat Tien shares her experience of studying Supply Chain Management at the University of Waikato’s Management School, a Business School ranked in the top 1% globally.

“My parents helped me to choose the University of Waikato as New Zealand is very peaceful and the people are very friendly and nice. Supply Chain Management is a really interesting major with good prospects for my future. Waikato staff are really helpful and people here will help you if you ask. If you want to succeed, my advice is to keep improving your English and soft skills and be confident and be yourself.”
Gia Cat Tien (Kathryn) Le
Vietnam
