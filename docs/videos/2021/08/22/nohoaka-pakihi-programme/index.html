University of Otago - Ōtākou Whakaihu Waka

Description

Nohoaka Pakihi is an amazing opportunity for Y12 and Y13 Māori students in Aotearoa who are interested in pursuing business studies. This is a fully funded scholarship for successful applicants to attend a 4 day programme held on campus in Dunedin. Experience life in a residential college, meet Māori support staff and business academics and get a feel for what a student life is like at Otago University. For more information: https://www.otago.ac.nz/business/studentactivities/mbbc/
