Beverly Thompson is studying a Bachelor of Physiotherapy, and has been involved in the Socioeconomic Equity Project at the University of Otago. This project is about empowering Socioeconomic Equity in the Health Workforce. To find out more about the project, please visit https://www.otago.ac.nz/equity
