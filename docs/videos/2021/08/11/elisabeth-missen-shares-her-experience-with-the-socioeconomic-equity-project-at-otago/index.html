Elisabeth Missen is studying a Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery, and has been involved in the Socioeconomic Equity Project at the University of Otago. This project is about empowering Socioeconomic Equity in the Health Workforce. To find out more about the project, please visit https://www.otago.ac.nz/equity
