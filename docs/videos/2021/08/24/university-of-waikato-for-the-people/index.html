Description

Ko te tangata. For the people.
Since the day we opened over fifty years ago, this has been our guiding principle. We are in, of and for the community. Our students are bursting with ambition, and they have a desire to shape the world around them, to make their mark. To be the leaders the future needs.
To pursue a career with purpose, one that makes the world a better place, not just for them, but for everyone.
Today we’re engaged in research programmes tackling some of the biggest challenges the world faces. While preparing for those the future will throw at us.

Our academics are doing and sharing research that has an impact on a global stage.
And our students and graduates are a big part of that, making a difference to the world.
Here at the University of Waikato, we foster your ambitions; we support you to be who you are, and who you dream of becoming. We believe that you will make an impact..
