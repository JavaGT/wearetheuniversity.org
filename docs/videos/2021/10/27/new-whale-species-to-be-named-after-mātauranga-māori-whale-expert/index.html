Description

Whales are often named after Western scientists and after men, but that tradition is about to change, as a newly discovered species of whale will carry an Indigenous name and the name of a woman. Its scientific name will be Mesoplodon eueu, referring to its Indigenous roots in South Africa, and its common name Ramari’s beaked whale after Ramari Stewart, a Mātauranga Māori whale expert.
