Description

Ko te tangata. For the people.
Since the day we opened over fifty years ago, this has been our guiding principle. We are in, of and for the community.
For the innovators, the knowledge seekers, and those ready to pursue their dreams.

Our academics and graduates are doing and sharing research that has an impact on a global stage. And our students are a big part of that, making a difference to the world.
Here at the University of Waikato, we foster your ambitions; we support you to be who you are, and who you dream of becoming. We believe that you will make an impact.
