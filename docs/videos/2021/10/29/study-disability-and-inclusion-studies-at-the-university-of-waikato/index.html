The University of Waikato

Description

Develop knowledge and skills to transform society to be inclusive through studying Disability and Inclusion Studies at the University of Waikato. Be connected to national and international leaders in research, policy and practice informed by the lived experiences of people with disability. Our unique programme focuses on developing critical researchers and thinkers, who make a difference.

For more information and to apply visit: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/qualifications/master-of-disability-and-inclusion-studies
