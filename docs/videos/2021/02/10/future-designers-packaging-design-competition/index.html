Description

The School of Design Innovation is running a packaging design competition! We are challenging you to design a package for either your favourite snack to share or to protect eggs.

Submit photos or videos of your designs by the end of Term 2, Friday 30th June to YouTube, Instagram, or Twitter using #futuredesignersnz or email us at futuredesigners@vuw.ac.nz

Consider how to:
- how to open and close the package
- make it easier to eat
- it easier to share
- store leftovers

Don’t forget to think about what happens to your packing after the food is eaten, can it still be useful?

Read more: https://futuredesigners.nz/packaging-competition/

Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.

Study design at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-areas/design/overview
