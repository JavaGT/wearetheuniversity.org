Description

Living in a flat with a local student and other international students is an enriching experience. The team at University Flats (commonly referred to as UniFlats) manage approximately 160 flats that house mainly single semester international students.

Our flats are unique. Some are older, renovated houses and others are newer, purpose-built in a small complex.
