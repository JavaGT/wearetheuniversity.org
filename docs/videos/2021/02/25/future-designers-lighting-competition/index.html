Description

The School of Design Innovation is running a lighting design competition! We are challenging you to design a lampshade using milk or juice bottle plastic for a chance to win cash prizes.

Submit photos or videos of your designs to YouTube, Instagram, or Twitter using #futuredesignersnz by the end of end of New Zealand high school term 2, 2021

Check out our other videos for tips that will help you in this competition:
Making a switch - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lDXWkGoRzwE
Shaping milk bottle plastic - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=45WWgN_fF3Q

Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz/lighting-competition/

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.

Study design at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-areas/design/overview
