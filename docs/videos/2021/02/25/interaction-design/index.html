Description

Interaction Design is the design of interactions between users and physical or digital products. It’s one of the newest and fastest-growing fields of design. Hear what School of Design Innovation–Te Kura Hoahoa students Logan Kim, Alexandra Larkworthy, Gabrielle Pittar and Adam Ben-Dror say about the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) in Interaction Design studies.
