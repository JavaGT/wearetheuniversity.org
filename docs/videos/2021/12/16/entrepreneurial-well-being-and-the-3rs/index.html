Description

The life of an entrepreneur can be stressful, especially in the current environment full of lockdowns and uncertainty. Dr Amanda Williamson, Lecturer in Innovation and Strategy from the Waikato Management School, says that business owners can better manage stress by following the 3Rs: Respite, Reappraisal and Regime.
