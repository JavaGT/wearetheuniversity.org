Revitalising te reo Māori — Capital Thinking Globally Minded [2:53]

Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington

Description

Around 95 percent of New Zealanders speak English, but just 3 percent speak te reo Māori. Professor Rawinia Higgins, Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Māori) at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington, leads research into how we can revitalise te reo to strengthen and increase its role and usage.
