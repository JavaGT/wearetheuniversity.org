Reimagining justice — Capital Thinking. Globally Minded [3:35]

Watch on Youtube

Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington

Description

Prisons do not deter, rehabilitate, or fundamentally tackle crime, so is it time we transform the way we do justice? Professor Elizabeth Stanley, Director of the Institute of Criminology at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington, is looking at how a Justice Reinvestment approach can transform justice systems by redirecting criminal justice spending into social justice initiatives that strengthen communities, reduce social harms, and prevent offending.
