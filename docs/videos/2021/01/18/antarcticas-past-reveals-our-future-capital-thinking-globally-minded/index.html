Description

Has Antarctica reached the end of its shelf life? Yes, if we don’t cap global warming at 2 degrees or less, says Professor Tim Naish from Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington’s Antarctic Research Centre. One of the biggest questions in climate change science, and one which the Centre is focused on answering, is how much and how fast will the Antarctic ice sheet melt, and what will be the contribution to sea level rise.
