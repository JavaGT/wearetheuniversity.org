Ross Hemera helps create culturally-inclusive environments for pharmacists at Otago [5:13]

Watch on Youtube

University of Otago - Ōtākou Whakaihu Waka

Description

Artwork by Ross Hemera (Ngāi Tahu, Ngāti Māmoe, Waitaha) has transformed the School of Pharmacy – He Rau Kawakawa – training laboratory and Pharmacy Clinic into culturally-inclusive and unique learning environments. Through art, the school encourages engagement with te ao Māori and mātauranga Māori, with the ultimate aim to educate and train culturally-safe practitioners.

Find out more about Pharmacy at Otago: https://www.otago.ac.nz/pharmacy
