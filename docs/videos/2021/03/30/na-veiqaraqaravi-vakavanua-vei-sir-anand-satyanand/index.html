Ni as Bula Vinaka
Last week Thursday, we had the opportunity to host the Veiqaravi Vakavanua for our Chancellor Sir Anand Satyanand. Sir Anand has roots in the Pacific especially Fiji.
The success of this event would not have been possible without the hard work of the Fijian community and our Pacific Student Associations particularly our Waikato University Fijian Students Association ( WUFSA).
We are grateful to the Nakabea family from Fiji for organising the fine mats for the Chancellor.
Vinaka Vakalevu to Amanda from our Events team , Dr Keaka , Dr Apo and the Pacific at Waikato team for their work behind the scenes.
We hope you enjoyed the event as much as we enjoyed hosting it.
