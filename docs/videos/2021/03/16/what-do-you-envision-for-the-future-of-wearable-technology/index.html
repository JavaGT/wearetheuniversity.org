I envision wearable technology to become
more and more abundant in daily life. With
the miniaturization of technology a lot
of things will be more seamless. We have
a development of wearable technology becoming
invisible in a way. So we already have shirts
that monitor health and that help us with
safety on the street and this will become
completely normal technology. So we'll
have garments that can monitor our health,
that can be fitness trackers, that we don't
even see or notice. And that really we're
heading towards wearable technology as a
second skin to enhance our human bodies.
On the other hand I think we have a development of
garments that that support fashion statements that
help individual expressions. So just think
about the glamorous garments of rock stars
like laser jackets and motorized dresses. So I
think it's going to become more normal to have,
for daily wear, maybe subtler expressions like
clothes that slowly change their color according
to the the time of the day, or jewelry that
changes it changes its color to match your outfit.
I think we'll have clothes that
breathe according to the temperature
and and then we have this area of safety as well
which is developing rapidly. So safety gear for
cyclists, intelligent motorcycle helmets that will
help us navigate traffic better, and possibly we
might even one day have self-lacing shoes and
self-drying jackets like in back to the future.
