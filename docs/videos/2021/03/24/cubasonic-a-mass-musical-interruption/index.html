Description

Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington University Emeritus Professor of Music John Psathas has composed CubaSonic.

The mass sonic work will feature more than 300 musicians performing live, including from the University’s New Zealand School of Music—Te Kōkī and many leading musical groups, together with a pre-recorded soundtrack and a Tesla coil.

CubaSonic will be performed as part of CubaDupa 2021 at 5pm on Saturday 27 March and 2.30pm on Sunday 28 March.
