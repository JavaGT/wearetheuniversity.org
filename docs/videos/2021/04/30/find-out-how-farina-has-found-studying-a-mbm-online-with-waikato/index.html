Find out how Farina has found studying a MBM online with Waikato [2:08]

Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

Farina Renato lives in India and started studying a Master of Business and Management with us online in March 2021. She was anxious to study online as she hadn't learnt in this way before but since starting she has felt well-supported with her studies, has enjoyed the interactive elements and the highlight has been working on group projects with other students across the world.

"As an international student I couldn't have asked for a better online experience. The amount of support provided is unbelievable. All of the staff and faculty are extremely compassionate and accommodating."

If you would like to know more about starting your studies online with us, please visit waikato.ac.nz/go/internationaloffshore
