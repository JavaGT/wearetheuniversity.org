Description

Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington’s Ferrier Research Institute and Breast Cancer Foundation New Zealand share a vision of zero deaths from breast cancer. Since 2017 their exciting research partnership is supporting Ferrier’s scientists to develop an immunotherapy vaccine that could stop cancer returning after treatment. This could be manufactured right here in New Zealand. Hear from the scientists how the vaccine works and from breast cancer survivors about what this would mean to them.

For more information visit: www.wgtn.ac.nz/engage/giving/donate/areas/breast-cancer-research-programme
