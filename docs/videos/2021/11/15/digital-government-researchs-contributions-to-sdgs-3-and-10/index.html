Description

Professor Miriam Lips, Chair in Digital Government, explores research, which contributes to SDG 3 (Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages) and SDG 10 (Reduce inequality within and among countries). In particular, two recent research initiatives are presented: research into understanding and explaining the digital inclusion and exclusion of senior citizens, including recommendations on how to encourage digital inclusion; and research on the effects of Covid-19 on digital public services. This seminar is part of the Wellington School of Business and Government Funded Chairs Series on the Sustainable Development Goals.
