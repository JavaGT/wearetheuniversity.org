Description

The Master of Global Business (MGBus) is a professionally focused programme that allows you to build on an undergraduate degree in any field of study and gain valuable skills in global business strategy and analysis.

The programme is coursework-based and includes an industry consultancy project, where you’ll work with a New Zealand or international organisation to gain real-world experience. This programme is best suited to recent graduates who are looking to kick-start their career in global business and strategy.
