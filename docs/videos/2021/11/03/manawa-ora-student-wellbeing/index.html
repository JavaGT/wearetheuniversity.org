Kia ora. Welcome to Manawa ora—Student wellbeing
at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington.
We know that uni life can be challenging sometimes,
and that's why I want to help you stay well on
your journey. Welcome to the bubble–it's a place
where you can rest, make friends, and get away from
the pressures of study. Wellbeing leaders like
myself work hard to make sure this is an inclusive,
friendly, and welcoming space, full of fun things
to do. You can come along and join our peer-support
groups–like the cultural coffee club and story
craft–who meet up regularly to encourage and
support our students.
Wellington Uni's Rainbow and Inclusion service is part of Manawa Ora,
and works to support our LGBTQIA+ and takatāpui students.
We work with our community to make
sure our campuses are safe and inclusive spaces for
students of all genders, sexualities, and
sex characteristics.
Manawa Ora also provides guidance and events for refugee background students.
The advisor is available to meet with you help you find information, services, and give you tailored advice.
We also offer workshops from wellbeing leaders, who understand what it's like to be a student.
You can book online workshops, or attend one in person at our Wellington campuses.
There are online resources on our website, created by our wellbeing leaders.
Come in to see us and say "hi". We hope to see you soon. Ka kite anō!
