Description

Waikato Management School graduate Kym Bradbury (Bachelor of Communication with Honours) says her love of words, language and storytelling have taken her all over the world in her public relations career - from Hamilton to London. Today, she is is the Country Communications Manager for IKEA UK & Ireland; the world's largest home furnishing retailer.

Learn more about the Bachelor of Communication: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/qualifications/bachelor-of-communication
