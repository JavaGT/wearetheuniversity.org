Description

Waikato Management School graduate Luke Pharaoh (Bachelor of Management Studies, 2010) is the Funding Manager for Virgin StartUp, based in London, UK. He is passionate about empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs to start and thrive in their own business by supporting them with advice, mentoring, and start-up funding.

Learn more about the Bachelor of Management Studies with Honours: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/qualifications/bachelor-of-management-studies-with-honours
