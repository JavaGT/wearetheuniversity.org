Petera Hudson - 2021 Three Minute Thesis Doctoral Finals [3:30]

Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

Is Artificial Intelligence the new (r)evolution or the new coloniser for Indigenous peoples? 

Poor Māori statistical rates are now evolving in imperial dominated algorithms proving amplification of bias stereotypes in AI systems. This research will examine how my whānau wish to respond to colonial cultured technologies and find ways to ensure that AI does not become the new coloniser of Indigenous peoples.
