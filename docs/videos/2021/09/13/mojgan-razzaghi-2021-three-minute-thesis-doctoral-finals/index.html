Description

Estuarine restoration: can we put things right? 

Estuarine restoration by engineering interventions is occurring worldwide, with varying levels of success. My PhD research investigates how river re-diversion alters the physical health of estuaries, focusing on the morphodynamics and hydrodynamics of the Maketū estuary in the Bay
of Plenty in response to the partial re-diversion of the Kaituna River.
