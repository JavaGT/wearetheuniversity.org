Description

Have We Asked the Kids? Perceptions of Ethnicities in Global Animated Films by the Eyes of the Children 

Do children perceive ethnicities on media because of what they learnt from school? Or is it due to their family background knowledge? Maybe it relates to their surroundings, friends, and neighbours either? My research aims to shed light on children’s perceptions of cultural representations in animated movies and ethnic awareness.
