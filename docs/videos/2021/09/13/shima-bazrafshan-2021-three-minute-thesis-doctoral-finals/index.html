Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

Women Refugees, from Crises to Entrepreneurs 

Being constrained by the double bind of racism and sexism, women refugees have traditionally been defined as vulnerable and ‘double disadvantaged’. However, my research challenges the current image of them as crises and argues that they have their own resources and assets that help them be successful entrepreneurs.
