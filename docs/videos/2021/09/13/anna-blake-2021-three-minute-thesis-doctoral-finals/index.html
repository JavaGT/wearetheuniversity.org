Description

Voicing the Overwhelm: Acknowledging SENCOs in NZ Secondary Schools 

Two decades since the unofficial introduction of SENCOs (Special Education Needs Coordinators) in New Zealand Secondary Schools, and, as yet, we know very little about these individuals and the important roles they fulfil. This research highlights SENCO’s experiences to bring attention to our overwhelmed Special Education sector.
