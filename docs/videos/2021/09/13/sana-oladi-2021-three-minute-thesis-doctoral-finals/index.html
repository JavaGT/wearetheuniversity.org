Landing Safely from Accidental FallsÂ 

This research investigates whether motor analogies can be used to reduce the risk of injury from accidental falls by older people. These motor analogies are designed specifically to promote safe(r) landing and I am examining their efficacy using biomechanical (effect on body) and cognitive (effect on mind) measures.
