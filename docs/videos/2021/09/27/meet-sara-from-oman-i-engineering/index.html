The University of Waikato

Description

Sara Al Kindi, a Chemical and Biological Engineering student on the path to success, shares her Waikato story.

"I chose my degree because I would like to work in the dairy industry or in waste management with a focus on sustainability. I believe we need to make changes to how we are living now, to ensure we leave the world in a better place for future generations.

The university has a great reputation for its engineering qualifications and is ranked among the best in the world. It offers state of the art learning facilities, small class sizes and all the lecturers are supportive and helpful.

My advice to anyone thinking of coming to the University of Waikato is do it."

To find out more about studying Engineering, visit waikato.ac.nz/study/campaigns/engineering

Thank you to Sara for sharing your experiences.
