Description

Lisa Matisoo-Smith, Associate Professor of Anthropology
Lisa was recently involved in groundbreaking research proving that ancient Polynesian voyagers, not early Europeans, brought chickens to the Americas. The study is part of Lisas ongoing work into Pacific voyaging, in which she examines the ancient DNA of animals and humans to discover new truths about early explorations of the Pacific.
