Description

Raymond Miller, Associate Professor of Political Studies
Raymond discusses the intricacies of New Zealands political system, the changing faces of our political parties and the relationship between the media and politics in this country. He also reveals who he considers the best and worst Prime Minister in New Zealands political history.
