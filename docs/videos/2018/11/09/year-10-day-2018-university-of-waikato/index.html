We had over 300 Year 10 students on campus to experience what it's like to study at University, and join in lectures and activities to spark their passion for knowledge.

Waikato has a reputation for being the friendliest university in New Zealand. Our campuses in Hamilton and Tauranga are bursting with life and energy. With smaller class sizes, better access to lecturers, affordable living, a wide range of support services, excellent sports facilities and more than 60 student clubs, it's not surprising that our students are the happiest in New Zealand.
