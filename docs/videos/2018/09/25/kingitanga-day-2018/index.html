Kīngitanga Day 2018 marks 10 years of celebrating the unique relationships that the University of Waikato has with the Kīngitanga, Waikato-Tainui and 19 iwi of Te Ropu Manukura.

Kīngitanga Day is an annual event that honours these special relationships and celebrates the University’s unique identity, distinctive culture and leadership.

Te Whare Wānanga o Waikato – the University of Waikato has had strong connections with Kīngitanga and Waikato-Tainui since it was founded in 1964.

Find out more here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/news-opinion/media/2018/marking-a-decade-of-kingitanga-day
