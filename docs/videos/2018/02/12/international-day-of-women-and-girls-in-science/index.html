Description

In order to achieve full and equal access to and participation in science for women and girls, the United Nations General Assembly adopted resolution A/RES/70/212 declaring 11 February as the International Day of Women and Girls in Science.

At Waikato, we're proud to inspire, engage and recognise women in science. Every day, we're celebrating the achievements of our brightest female minds and we'd like you to meet some of them.
