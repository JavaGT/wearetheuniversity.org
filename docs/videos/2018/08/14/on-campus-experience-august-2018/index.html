Description

Still thinking about whether Otago is right for you? This video shows the group of secondary school pupils of Māori descent who took part in a week-long On Campus Experience this month, where they sampled living and learning at Otago University in a fully funded scholarship. How did they find the experience? Check out the video and find out!
