Description

"She is currently living in supermarket flowers that whisper buy me in their middle-class plastic..."

In celebration of World Poetry Day, we caught up with award-winning poet and University of Waikato Writing Studies lecturer, Dr Tracey Slaughter. She shared a reading of her beautiful poem 'She is currently living' with us.

Tracey was awarded second place winner in this year's Peter Porter Poetry Prize for her poem 'breather'.
