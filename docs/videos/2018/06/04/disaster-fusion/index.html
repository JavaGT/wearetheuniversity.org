Description

University of Otago researchers have produced findings into the “fusing” effects of the February 22 2011 earthquake on members of the Christchurch community.
The qualitative study out of Otago’s Department of Psychology involved 200 participants from Christchurch who were over 18 years old at the time of the earthquake and were in the city during the earthquake.
Lead author, PhD Candidate Keren Segal, says various levels of fusion can come from people going through a shared life-defining experience. Findings from the study show a link between people’s experience in the earthquake and their levels of fusion, or connection, to Christchurch.
