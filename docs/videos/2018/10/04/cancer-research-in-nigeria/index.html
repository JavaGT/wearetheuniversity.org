Description

Dr Avinash Sharma, alumnus of the University of Auckland, tells his story of working as a Global Cancer Disparities Fellow. He spent six months in Nigeria conducting novel research in breast and bowel cancer, and is now based at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Centre in New York.
