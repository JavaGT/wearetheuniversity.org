Description

Giving students a greater sense of possibility about their future is an excellent motivational technique and helps guide them in their subject choices. To build students' aspirations for their futures, Whangarei Boys’ High School leaders arranged educational visits to tertiary institutions and remote learning opportunities for students interested in taking classes not offered at the school.

For more information, visit http://teamsolutions.ac.nz.
