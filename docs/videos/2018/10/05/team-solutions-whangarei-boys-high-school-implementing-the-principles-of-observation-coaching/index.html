Description

By working with Team Solutions to develop a strong observation and shadow-coaching system, Whangarei Boys’ High School teachers were able to reflect on and progressively improve their teaching practice in ways that deeply engaged Māori boys in their learning.

For more information, visit http://teamsolutions.ac.nz.
