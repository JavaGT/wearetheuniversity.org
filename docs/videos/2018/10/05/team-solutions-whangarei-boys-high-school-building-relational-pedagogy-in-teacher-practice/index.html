Description

By working with Team Solutions facilitators to develop a more relational and reciprocal focus to teaching, Whangarei Boys’ High School was able to create a learning environment where teachers and students work collaboratively to develop a deeper understanding of new ideas.

For more information, visit http://teamsolutions.ac.nz.
