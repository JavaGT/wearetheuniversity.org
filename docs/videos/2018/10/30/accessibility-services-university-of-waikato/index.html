Accessibility Services -- Supporting students with impairments to be successful in their studies.
The Accessibility Services team at the University of Waikato can assist if you experience a specific learning impairment, sensory, physical mental health or a temporary illness/injury that could impact on your ability to study.
Accessibility Services is located at our Hamilton Campus in Level One of the Student Centre. We are open Monday - Friday 8:30am - 5:00pm.
Contact Us:
https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/accessibility-services
-- email accessibilityservices@waikato.ac.nz
-- or call 07 838 4711 or 838 4393
