The University of Waikato

Description

Accessibility Services at the University of Waikato are here to help if you experience a specific learning impairment, sensory, physical mental health or a temporary illness/injury that could impact on your ability to study.

The Accessibility Services team are located on Level One of the Student Centre at our Hamilton Campus and are open Monday - Friday 8:30am - 5:00pm.
