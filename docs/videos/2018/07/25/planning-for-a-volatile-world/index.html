The Executive Education team hosted 85 alumni and guests for an interactive presentation by Steve McCrone. Steve’s experience in bomb disposal in the New Zealand Army gave him a good understanding and a deep appreciation for leadership and decision making in stressful situations. He’s now applied some of that knowledge to challenge traditional management theory in fast changing and highly complex environments. Find out more about Steve and course he teaches for professionals.
