Description

Going Green is the University Sustainability programme, which has been running for 7 years.

As a responsible and environmentally aware organisation, we aim to raise awareness of sustainability, and encourage understanding and responsibility in the use of resources via; research, operational practices, partnerships, teaching and learning.

Please see our website for further information https://www.waikato.ac.nz/sustainability/
