Hand in Hand: Photographers and Painters Alike at Hocken Collections, Uare Taoka o Hākena [2:31]

Description

Frances Hodgkins’ early career in Dunedin, and the impact photography had on it, is the focus of the Hocken Collections, Uare Taoka o Hākena’s latest exhibition.

A crucial piece of evidence in the exhibition is a unique photograph of Mere Te Kaehe Karetai taken by her father, William Hodgkins, and a previously unpublished painted portrait of Mere Te Kaehe by Frances.
