Measuring Motion: Big Data in Agriculture [2:14]

Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

This year marks the 50th anniversary of the New Zealand Agricultural Fieldays, and the University of Waikato’s stand will be showcasing research on big data and artificial intelligence in the future of farming. We chat with Dr Lee Streeter about his Marsden Fast Start and MBIE Smart ideas funded research project that looks at how to accurately measure motion and reduce motion blur using time-of-flight photography. Dr Streeter will have cameras on stand this year’s event, come and visit us in the Pavilion. For more information please visit http://www.waikato.ac.nz/events/fieldays/
