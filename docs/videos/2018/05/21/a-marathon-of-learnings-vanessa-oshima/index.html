Description

For over five years University of Waikato Alumna Vanessa Oshima, who now lives in Tokyo, has been running every day to support those she loves who are battling cancer. After hearing her high school friend, former Matamata College student Caroline Steer received a breast cancer diagnosis, Vanessa made a promise she would run 5kms every day until Caroline was cancer free. Vanessa returned to the University in March this year, on day 2022 of her running 'streak', to share her story of challenges and learnings from this significant commitment - "A Marathon of Learnings"
