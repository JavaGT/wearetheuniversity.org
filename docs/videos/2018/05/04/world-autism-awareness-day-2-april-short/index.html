World Autism Awareness Day (2 April) (Short) [2:08]

The University of Waikato

Description

World Autism Awareness Day is an international campaign that aims to increase understanding and promote greater inclusion and participation of people with autism in society.

Senior Lecturer Dr Carol Hamilton talks about the unique attributes of people with autism, and how the University of Waikato aims to provide opportunities for students with autism to celebrate their difference and talents.

Master of Disability and Inclusion Studies (MDInS) student Kyle Hefferon talks about the difficulties autistic students experience in tertiary education, and how the MDInS degree recognises and supports her views and strengths.
