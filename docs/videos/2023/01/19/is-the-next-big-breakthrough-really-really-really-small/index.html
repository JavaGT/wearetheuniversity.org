Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington

Description

Associate Professor Natalie Plank’s specialises in the use of nanomaterials in technology. Nanomaterials are impossibly tiny—one nanometre is a billionth of a metre, or about 50,000 to 100,000 times smaller than the width of a human hair. Associate Professor Plank is fascinated with the practical applications of nanotechnology that are potentially life-saving. Her research focuses on creating incredibly fast, accurate, and sensitive sensors.
