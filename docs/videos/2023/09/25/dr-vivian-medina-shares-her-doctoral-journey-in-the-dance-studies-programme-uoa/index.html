Dr Vivian Medina shares her doctoral journey in the Dance Studies Programme #UoA [5:19]

Watch on Youtube

Creative Arts and Industries, the University of Auckland

Description

Dr Vivian Medina recently completed her Doctoral Study in the Dance Studies Programme, receiving Dean's List recognition for outstanding work in her research project. Learn more about her extensive journey, originating from Bogata, Colombia to now working with Touch Compass - Aotearoa's leading disability-led professional arts organisation!

Find out more about our Dance Studies Programme:
