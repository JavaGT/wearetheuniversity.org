Description

Start your journey to become a future accountant and business leader by studying for a Master of Professional Accounting at the University of Waikato in New Zealand.

This 12-month degree is ideal for people who have never studied accounting before and want to gain a postgraduate qualification that is internationally recognised by professional industry bodies.

You can also choose to study part-time for an extra 9 months and take your CA Professional Exams with Chartered Accountants Australia and New Zealand (CAANZ).
