As a registered nurse in Waikato Hospital's Emergency Department (ED), Rangi Harrison (Waikato-Tainui, Ngāti Kahungunu) is making a positive difference to the lives of people. She is among the first cohort of students to graduate with a Bachelor of Nursing (Hons) degree, and is gearing up to do her PhD in Health Science in 2024.
