Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington

Description

Every two years, Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington acknowledges a selection of top-achieving alumni who have made a major contribution in their field. Recipients of the awards in 2023 were venture capitalist and global agritech leader Arama Kukutai, Solicitor General of New Zealand Una Jagose, esteemed poet and author Tusiata Avia, and drug harm reduction advocate Wendy Allison. On October 19, we proudly celebrated their achievements alongside alumni, supporters and friends.
