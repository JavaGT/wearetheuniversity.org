The University of Waikato

Description

Do you enjoy writing and talking to people? If you want a career that’s focused on communicating ideas and building relationships, then consider studying Public Relations (PR) at the University of Waikato.

You’ll develop well-rounded communication skills in everything from media relations and crisis management, through to PR campaigns, social media, events management, speech writing, sponsorship and fundraising.

Waikato’s PR programme is the only one in New Zealand and Australia that is accredited by the Public Relations Society of America (PRSA), which means it’s globally recognised and gives our graduates a competitive advantage when looking for jobs. Learn more at waikato.ac.nz
