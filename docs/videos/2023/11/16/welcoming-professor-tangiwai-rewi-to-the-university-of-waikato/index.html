I te 6 o Noema, i pōwhiri ai Te Whare Wānanga o Waikato i Te Amokapua, Ahorangi Tangiwai Rewi ki Te Pua Wānanga ki te Ao.

On 6 November we welcomed Professor Tangiwai Rewi to the University of Waikato as Dean of Te Pua Wānanga ki te Ao - Faculty of Māori and Indigenous Studies with a pōwhiri at The Pā.

Tangiwai (Waikato- Ngāti Tīpā, Ngāti Āmaru, Ngāti Tahinga) says joining Waikato is truly coming home. She's shared with us some reflections on her journey getting here, and her aspirations for the Faculty. We continue to celebrate her arrival and look forward to her getting to know our wider Waikato whānau.
