Our alumna, Dr Leen Al-Hadban, talks about the new University of Waikato and Hamilton Multicultural Services (HMS) Trust Award, which provides financial support for tuition and course-related costs to enrolled students from a migrant or refugee background. Eligibility criteria applies.
