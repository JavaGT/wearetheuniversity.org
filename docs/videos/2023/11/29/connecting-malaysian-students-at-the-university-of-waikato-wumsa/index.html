Description

Marsya and Sharifah, two Malaysian students studying at the University of Waikato, introduce the Waikato University Malaysian Association (WUMSA). They highlight the association's mission to provide a platform for Malaysian students to connect and support each other during their time in New Zealand. The video showcases footage of WUMSA events, the campus, local restaurants, community events, and places of prayer. Marsya and Sharifah also share their favorite experiences on campus, such as relaxing at the Pa, enjoying walks around the lakes, visiting Hamilton gardens and nearby beaches, and cycling along the river trails. Finally, they invite viewers who are interested in enrolling at the University of Waikato and joining WUMSA to find them on Facebook and Instagram.
