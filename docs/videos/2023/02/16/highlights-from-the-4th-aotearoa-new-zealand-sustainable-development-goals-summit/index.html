Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

The University of Waikato was proud to host the 4th Aotearoa Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Summit over two days in February 2023, with support from co-hosts Waikato Tainui, Te Pūkenga, and the Waikato Wellbeing Project.

The Summit brought together over 200 thought leaders to explore key issues related to sustainability and wellbeing within the Waikato Region using the 17 SDGs as a framework and with a particular focus on advancing Māori, Indigenous and community perspectives.
