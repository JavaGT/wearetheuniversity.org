Description

The University of Auckland Business School’s Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE) has programmes, events, workshops and facilities on offer through our innovation hubs that are freely available to all students and staff of the University of Auckland, no matter what they are studying or what their goals are. An entrepreneurial mindset and innovative capability are essential both to thrive in this new world of work, and to build the future that we want to live in.
Find out more at: http://www.cie.auckland.ac.nz
