Description

Dr Andrew Shepherd is Senior Lecturer in Theology and Public Issues.

Andrew believes that the historical Christian faith tradition offers significant wisdom to help us understand and respond – at personal, communal and societal levels – to the challenges our contemporary world faces. His research considers challenges such as environmental degradation and climate change, economic inequality, social polarisation, violence and militarism and the emergence of surveillance capitalism.

For more information about Andrew, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago720307.html
For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
