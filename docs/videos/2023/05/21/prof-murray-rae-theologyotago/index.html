Description

He was an architect before studying theology and continues to write on the interface between theology and architecture. He is also passionate about the work of Søren Kierkegaard, Christian Pacifism, the Bible and History, the centrality of the resurrection for Christian faith, and the development of Christian faith amongst Māori.

For more information about Murray, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago013660.html For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
