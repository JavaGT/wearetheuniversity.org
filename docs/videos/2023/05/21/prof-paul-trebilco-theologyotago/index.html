Description

Paul Trebilco is Professor of New Testament Studies.

He has written on early Christians in Ephesus and other cities in Western Asia Minor, the Pastoral Epistles, the Johannine Letters, issues of identity in early Christianity, and Diaspora Judaism. He teaches a range of courses on the New Testament – Interpreting the New Testament, Jesus in the New Testament, and the Pauline Epistles, focussing on Pauline Theology and Romans. He enjoys helping students to understand more of the wonderful richness of the New Testament and its world.

For more information about Paul, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago013675.html For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
