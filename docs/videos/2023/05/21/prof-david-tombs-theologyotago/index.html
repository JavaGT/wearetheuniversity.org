Description

David Tombs is the Howard Paterson Professor of Theology and Public Issues, and Director of the Centre for Theology and Public Issues.

His research focuses on public, contextual, and liberation theologies, and their relevance for public issues. He is interested in how theology can contribute to social justice and personal well-being in contemporary contexts.

For more information about David, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/tombs.html
For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
