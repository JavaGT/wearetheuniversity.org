Description

Lynne Taylor is the Jack Somerville Senior Lecturer in Pastoral Theology.

Her research focuses on faith formation, pastoral care, spirituality and wellbeing, and the mission and ministry of the Church. She is interested in the contributions that Christians and the Church can make to human flourishing.

For more information about Lynne, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago675910.html
For more information about Theology at Otago University, see http://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
