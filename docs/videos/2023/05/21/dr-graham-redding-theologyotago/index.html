Description

Graham Redding is Lecturer in Chaplaincy Studies.

His research focuses on chaplaincy, liturgical theology, and pastoral care. He is interested in the resonance between theology, ministry praxis, and liturgical expression across a range of chaplaincy and ministry settings.

For more information about Graham, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/graham-redding.html
For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
