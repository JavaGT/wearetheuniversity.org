Description

Katie Marcar is a Senior Teaching Fellow and Research Fellow in Biblical Languages.

Katie’s research focuses on 1 Peter and the Catholic Epistles. She is interested in understanding the New Testament in its context within Second Temple Judaism and the Greco-Roman world.

For more information about Katie, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago638365.html For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
