Description

Wayne Te Kaawa is Lecturer in Māori Theology.

He teaches three papers on Māori Theology, two of which are marae-based one-week intensives. His research interests are in the area of Māori engagement with Christianity and the Church and exegesis of biblical texts using mātauranga Māori.

For more information about Wayne, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago741193.html For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
