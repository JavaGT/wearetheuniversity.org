Description

Christopher Holmes is Professor of Systematic Theology.

Chris’s research focuses on the doctrine of God, the unity of Holy Scripture, and the integration of theology with the spiritual life. He pursues the theological task in a scripturally charged manner mindful of the catholic theological tradition, with a view to edifying the Christian community and contributing to the common good.

For more information about Chris, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago013674.html
For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
