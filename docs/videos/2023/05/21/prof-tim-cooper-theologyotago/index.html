Description

Tim Cooper is Professor of Church History.

His research focuses on seventeenth-century English religious history and on the Puritans in particular. He investigates how the lived daily experience of this tumultuous century shaped the thought of leading Puritan theologians and pastors.

For more information about Tim, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology/staff/otago013673.html
For more information about Theology at Otago University, see https://www.otago.ac.nz/theology
