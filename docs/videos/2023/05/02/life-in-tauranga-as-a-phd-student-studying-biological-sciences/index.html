Description

Roberta Marcoli moved to New Zealand from Italy to pursue postgraduate study. She developed a tool that monitors the health of Chinook salmon, a critical effort in the fight to prevent overfishing in the ocean, and is now working as a postdoc at James Cook University in Australia.
