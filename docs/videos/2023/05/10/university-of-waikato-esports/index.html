The University of Waikato

Description

Formed in 2019, University of Waikato Esports was established to enhance esports opportunities for students at the University of Waikato. The club is open to students and staff. Catering to competitive and social players, the Club is based at the Esports Arena on our Hamilton campus.

Games: University of Waikato Esports provides members with access to many esports games including League of Legends, Valorant, Fortnite, Overwatch and Rocket League.
