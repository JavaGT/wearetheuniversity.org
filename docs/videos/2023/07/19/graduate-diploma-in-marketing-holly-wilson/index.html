Description

Holly Wilson was one of the top-performing marketing students at Waikato Management School this trimester.

Holly and nine other students in the paper ‘Modern Professional Selling’ were given the opportunity to write and present a sales training manual to Spark New Zealand.

It’s thanks to our new partnership with Spark in Hamilton, the Spark Business Prize, that’s giving students real-world sales experience and the opportunity to land a job in the future.

Holly, who returned to study for her Graduate Diploma in Marketing, came third in the competition.
