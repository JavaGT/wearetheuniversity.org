Can art help us to see the bigger picture? [3:00]

Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington

Description

Associate Professor Su Ballard’s research examines the histories of nature in contemporary art with a particular focus on artists from Aotearoa New Zealand, Australia and the Asia-Pacific. She focuses on how artists respond to natural disasters, species extinction, and climate change.
