In April 2023, the University of Auckland Business School partnered with Auckland Business Chamber and National Business Review to host 'Embracing the revolution: ChatGPT and its impact on business communication.' The panel discussion event featured leading academics from the Business School and industries experts, who discussed new AI technologies and how they can be leveraged to drive innovation and growth in various industries.
