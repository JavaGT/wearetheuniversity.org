Are you interested in a well-paid, enjoyable career in business as a Chartered Accountant (CA)? 👩‍💼

Join us at The Future of Accounting 2023 on 16 August to find out what qualifications and skills you'll need to get started. 👍

Hear from industry experts at the ‘Big Four’ accounting firms, recent Waikato accounting graduates, the CEOs of Chiefs Rugby Club and Tainui Group Holdings, and other senior business leaders. 📢

Entry is free. It includes nibbles and an opportunity for networking.
