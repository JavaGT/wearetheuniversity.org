Description

Marketing students are getting real-world sales experience thanks to a unique partnership between Spark New Zealand and Waikato Management School.

The Spark Business Prize offers practical learning, industry engagement, and potential job opportunities to University of Waikato students.

It’s the first time Spark has collaborated with a New Zealand university and there’s scope to roll it out even further in the future.

Congrats to the top-performing students, Klara Allen, Zoe Oosten and Holly Wilson!

Klara came first and scored $3000, while Zoe received $2,500 for second place, and Holly secured $1,000 for third place.
