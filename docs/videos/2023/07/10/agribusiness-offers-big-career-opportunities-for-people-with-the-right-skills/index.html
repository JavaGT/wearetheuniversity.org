The University of Waikato

Description

If you're keen to work in New Zealand's agribusiness sector and help food producers sell their products in global markets, the University of Waikato is the ideal place to study and launch a career in this field. Gain the skills you need to advise farmers on profitability, sales and marketing, smart technologies, finance, sustainability, or managing supply chains from paddock to plate.
The Waikato and Bay of Plenty regions continue to grow as a hub for world-leading agricultural firms such as Fonterra, Gallagher, Zespri, RaboBank, Comvita, and Tatua Dairy Company.
Learn more at www.waikato.ac.nz/go/agribusiness
