The University of Waikato

Description

The Pā is a multifunctional building with a new marae, student hub, and spaces for meeting, learning, socialising and celebrating. It is where you will go on your first day of Orientation, and the day you walk across the stage for graduation. This video explains the vision of The Pā and the benefits that it will bring to students when it opens in 2023.
