Dr Chrissie Painting is researching the sex life of spiders.🕷️

Chrissie says for one of the American species, the male spontaneously dies after he mates - his heart literally stops beating 💓 - and he remains attached to the female, so she eats him. 😮

“But we know that there’s lots of variation along a continuum for this group of spiders.”

She and her research partners are going to be spending a lot of time with Dolomedes fishing spiders.

Chrissie is interested in finding out why animals are so variable in their behaviour and appearance.
