📢 Shoutout to Caroline Gill, Deputy Principal at Sacred Heart Girls' College in Hamilton, for her dedication to shaping the next generation of leaders! 🌟

Caroline's journey has been one of perseverance and resilience, having completed her undergraduate degree at the University of Waikato as a single mum, majoring in dance and drama. She later pursued a career as a dance teacher at secondary schools around Waikato and the Bay of Plenty, while completing a Master of Education degree at the University. 🎓

Her story is truly inspiring and we are proud of her drive and mahi, and for being a great role model for all of her girls. As one of 39 staff at Sacred Heart who are Waikato alumni, we love our connection with your school's staff and students, past and present. 👩‍🏫

Read more about Caroline's journey and be inspired 👉 https://www.waikato.ac.nz/news-opinion/media/2023/rhythm-and-resilience-a-deputy-principals-inspiring-journey-to-success
