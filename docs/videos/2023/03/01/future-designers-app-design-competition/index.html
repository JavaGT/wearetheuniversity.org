Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington

Description

The School of Design Innovation is running an app design competition!

We’re challenging you to design an app that you would like to use. It’s can be as serious or silly as you like you just need to:
-tell us what the app does
-make four screen mock-ups that illustrate its functionality or aesthetics

You can design your app on paper or using any software you like.

To win our extra challenge, try creating a clickable version of your app using Figma, a free web tool for prototyping design interfaces

Submit photos or videos of your designs by the end of Term 2, Friday 30th June to YouTube, Instagram, or Twitter using #futuredesignersnz or email us at futuredesigners@vuw.ac.nz

Read more: https://futuredesigners.nz/app-design-competition/

Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the Wellington School of Design Innovation.

Study design at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-...
