Description

Energy production is responsible for the vast majority of greenhouse gas emissions worldwide. Moving to more renewable energy production is crucial, but what does that look like?

Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington’s Andrew Wilks, Director of Sustainability, chats with Professor Alan Bret who shares with us some untapped locations for energy production and show us how we can move to a cleaner, greener future. They also visit Matiu/Sommes Island to look at the energy systems we have in play and how those systems create enough power for the historic island to function for multiple days.
