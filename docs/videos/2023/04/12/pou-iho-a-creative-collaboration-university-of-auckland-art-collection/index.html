Description

An overview of the 'Pou Iho' project, a sculpture commissioned by the University of Auckland Art Collection in conjunction with the Faculty of Engineering that speaks to the past, present, and future of the faculty and the University. The commission was led by esteemed MƒÅori weaver/artist, Elam alumna, and Waipapa Taumata Rau ex-academic staff member Dr Maureen Lander (MNZM). 'Pou Iho' was co-designed and co-created with eight Waipapa Taumata Rau students from a range of disciplines, including engineering, fine arts, and architecture. The video highlights the involvement and perspectives of the artist, the student makers, faculty staff, and Art Collection staff, and discusses the process and significance of 'Pou Iho' as both a physical artefact and as a collaborative project.
