Description

Get a head start in your accounting career with our NZ-exclusive Chartered Accountants (CA) pathway. The Master of Professional Accounting (MPA) will equip you with the skills and knowledge you need to manage complex accounting and business issues and fast track your career as a certified Chartered Accountant.
