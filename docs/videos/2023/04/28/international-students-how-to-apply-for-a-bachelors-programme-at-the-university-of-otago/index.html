Description

Learn how to apply for your Bachelor’s programme as an international student at the University of Otago, New Zealand’s 1st university.

Discover New Zealand while studying on one of the most beautiful campuses in the world, gain a world-class education, and cultivate your academic passions with unmatched teaching excellence.

In this video, you will learn how to successfully apply to the University of Otago, with tips and tricks on:
- What to prepare for your Bachelor's application
- A step-by-step application guide
- How to create an eVision account
- How to complete and submit your application
- And other important information that will be useful for you to check out, to prepare for a successful application!

We can't wait to see you at the University of Otago!

Learn more: www.otago.ac.nz/international/future-students/
