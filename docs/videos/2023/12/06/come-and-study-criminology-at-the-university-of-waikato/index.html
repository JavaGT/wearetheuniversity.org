Exciting News! Introducing the New Criminology Major with Te Kura Aronui the School of Social Sciences at the University of Waikato.

By choosing to study with us, you will have the chance to develop critical thinking skills and gain practical knowledge that will empower you to navigate the intricate world of criminal justice.
