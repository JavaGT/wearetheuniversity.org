Description

Lushomo Thebe moved with her family from Zambia to New Zealand as a 10-year-old. After five years of study at the University of Waikato, she graduated in October 2023 with a Bachelor of Laws and Bachelor of Business. As Waikato Students' Union president and University Council member, Lushomo has made a huge contribution to Waikato University. Congratulations to Lushomo and all our graduates! Read more here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/news-opinion/media/2023/wsu-leader-celebrates-her-graduation
