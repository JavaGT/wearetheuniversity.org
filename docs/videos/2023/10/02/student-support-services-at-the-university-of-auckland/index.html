Description

Join Lou, the campus Kererū, with their crew as they glide you through the support services on offer for free for students at Waipapa Taumata Rau, the University of Auckland.

We offer free tech support workshops, free career workshops and events, free confidential counselling, and free academic and study workshops — like how to write an essay.

We have specialised programmes and groups if you are part of the LGBTIQA+ community, Māori and Pacific Leadership programmes, workshops for students with English as a second language, support services for neurodiverse learners, and much, much more!

Find out more about student support services: https://auckland.ac.nz/student-support

Watch the full Waipapa Taumata Rau, University of Auckland application help video series: https://auckland.ac.nz/uni101
