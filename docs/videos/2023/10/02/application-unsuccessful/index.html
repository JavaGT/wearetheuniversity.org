Description

Join Lou, the campus Kererū, for a kōrero about what happens if you don’t make it into your preferred degree programme.

If you are declined because you didn’t meet the entry requirements, we have a number of alternative pathways, including one-year foundation certificates that will help you meet the requirements and prepare you for a bachelors degree.

We still want you to be part of our community and are excited about the options we have to help you into your preferred degree programme.

Check out our preparation programmes on our website: https://auckland.ac.nz/preparation-programmes

Watch the full Waipapa Taumata Rau, University of Auckland application help video series: https://auckland.ac.nz/uni101
