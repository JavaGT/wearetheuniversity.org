Join Lou, the campus Kererū, for a kōrero about where to get help if you get stuck with your application to Waipapa Taumata Rau, the University of Auckland.

If you’re feeling confused or having trouble with your application, a good place to start is by visiting the AskAuckland section of our website. Simply type in your question, and your answer should pop up.
We also have a UoA Assistant you can chat to on our website that will direct you to the right resources.

Or if you’re wanting to speak to a human, you can reach out to our friendly team by phone or visit the Student Hubs in-person at one of our campuses.

For all our contact details, visit our website: https://auckland.ac.nz/connect

Watch the full Waipapa Taumata Rau, University of Auckland application help video series: https://auckland.ac.nz/uni101
