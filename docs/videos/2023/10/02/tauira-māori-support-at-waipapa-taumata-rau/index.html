Kia ora, Ko Lou te Kererū ahau. Ko tāku, he whakaatu ki a koutou, ngā kaupapa tautoko, mō ngā Tauira Māori.
Meet Lou, the campus Kererū, here to give you a view of our services offered specifically to our tauira Māori.

Ko tētahi o ngā rōpū tautoko i ngā ākonga Māori ko Tuākana. I raro i te korowai manaaki o Tuakana, ka whakaritea he wāhi mahi ki te taha o o hoa ako, he whakawhanaungatanga, tētahi ki tētahi hoki. Mā tēnei kaupapa e tūhono atu i ngā teina, ngā ākonga hōu, me ngā tuākana. Me te aha, ka honohonotia ngā tauira Māori, me ngā tauira Pacifika ki ngā kaimahi, e rite ana ki te tautoko i a rātau.
Our University-wide learning community, enhancing academic success, is called Tuākana. These spaces offer small-group learning, whakawhanaungatanga, wānanga, face-to-face meetings and workshops. These help connect Māori and Pacific students with senior Māori and Pacific students (tuākana), academic teaching staff, and key people across the University.

Find out more about tauira Māori support at Waipapa Taumata Rau: https://auckland.ac.nz/maori

Watch the full Waipapa Taumata Rau, University of Auckland application help video series: https://auckland.ac.nz/uni101
