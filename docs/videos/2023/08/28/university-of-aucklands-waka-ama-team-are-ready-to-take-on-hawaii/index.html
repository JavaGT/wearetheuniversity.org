Description

The University of Auckland Engineering Eagles Waka Ama team is heading to the global stage! Last week we introduced you to the team who are heading to Hawai’i this week to compete in the Queen Lili'uokalani race, today they’re sharing their thoughts on what it means to be part of the team. We’re looking forward to seeing the team paddle their way to greatness and will be sharing their journey over the coming days.
