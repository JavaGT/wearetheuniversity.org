Description

University researchers Dr Amy Chan and Dr Stuti Misra would like to find out what pollens are in the air and in what quantities and how this correlates with allergic diseases such as asthma, eczema and allergic eye diseases using a pollen trap installed on the roof of Auckland Museum.
