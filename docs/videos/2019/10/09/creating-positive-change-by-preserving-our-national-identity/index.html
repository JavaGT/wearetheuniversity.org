Description

Puawai provides a platform for Māori history and storytelling. Puawai studied Screen and Media Studies and English Literature, and completed her Masters in 2001. Now she leads the Māori Curatorial Team at Te Papa and supports Mātauranga Māori across our national museum. It’s a busy and stimulating job that is taking her around the world.
