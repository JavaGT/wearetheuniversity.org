Description

Thomas’ dream is to help every Kiwi kid participate in sport. Thomas is a Bachelor of Management Studies graduate and a Hillary Scholar, who founded the organisation The Waterboy. This not-for-profit aims to break down barriers to give Kiwis an opportunity to take part in sport, as a means to build stronger people and stronger communities. He has the best of both worlds – a job he loves while making a difference.
