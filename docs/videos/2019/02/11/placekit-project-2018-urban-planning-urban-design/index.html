Description

Our Urban Planning and Urban Design students collaborated with the Auckland Council's Auckland Design Office and Auckland Transport on the Alfred Street ‘PlaceKit’ project which are composed of colourful, bespoke seats and planter boxes to make public spaces more pedestrian-friendly.

Through this project and planting session, our students had the opportunity to extend their learning beyond the classroom and gain experience on a real-life project in the public realm.

For more information on how you can help design and shape the future of our urban spaces, visit http://bit.ly/UrbPlanDes
