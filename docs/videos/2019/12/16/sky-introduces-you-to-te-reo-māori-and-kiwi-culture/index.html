Description

When you arrive in New Zealand you might hear a few unfamiliar words. We have a few slang words that are unique to 'Kiwi's'. You will also hear Te Reo Māori being spoken - our indigenous native language in New Zealand. Sky will talk you through a few key words to help you familiarise yourself. Check out NauMaiNZ for more information on New Zealand language and culture - https://naumainz.studyinnewzealand.govt.nz/help-and-advice/culture-and-lifestyle
