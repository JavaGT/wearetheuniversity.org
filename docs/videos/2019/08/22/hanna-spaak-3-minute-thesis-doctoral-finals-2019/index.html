HANNA SPAAK, School of Science

*Nature’s Sweet Seduction: Revealing New Zealand’s Nectar Potential*

This study aims to reveal that floral nectar of New Zealand native trees vary in quality and quantity. The results will help us better understand the complex physiology of nectar production, optimise honey production and bird conservation.

Find out more about the 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
