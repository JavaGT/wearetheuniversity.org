Description

Since 2003, Flight Structures have been designing, building and certifying aviation equipment. Every year they hire University of Waikato engineering interns and graduates.

Work-integrated learning (WIL) create a link between academic learning and the practical applications of this learning in the workplace. Students acquire, understand and apply essential professional skills that will prepare them for the future workforce, while allowing them to demonstrate their graduate knowledge to employers. Organisations will benefit from fresh perspectives and ideas, an additional pair of hands, short cutting future recruitment and providing opportunities for growth. Work-integrated learning is a win-win for organisations and students.

Find out more about our Work Integrated Learning here:https://www.waikato.ac.nz/work-integrated-learning/
