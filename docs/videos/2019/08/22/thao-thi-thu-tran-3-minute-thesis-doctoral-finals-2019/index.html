THAO THI THU TRAN, Waikato Management School

*Is gender equality still a myth? An analysis of women’s career in the Vietnamese academia from multiple lenses*

Vietnamese women are still excluded from power despite social and economic developments in the country. My study examines women’s careers in recent Vietnamese academia through the lenses of the
liberal and postcolonial feminism.
