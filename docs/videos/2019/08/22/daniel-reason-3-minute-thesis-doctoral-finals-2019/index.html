DANIEL REASON, School of Science

*A PhD in Medicinal Cannabis*

With medicinal cannabis coming to New Zealand, my thesis focuses on developing and optimising methods for the extraction and purification of cannabinoids. The goal is to see these methods used in the production of approved pharmaceutical ingredients for medicinal cannabis products at an affordable price for patients.

Find out more about 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
