SWATHI RANGARAJAN, School of Education

*Scaffolding learning in a technology education classroom*

Technology education is a relatively recent introduction to the curriculum and critical for promoting 21st century skills. My research explores how primary teachers in the technology classroom in New Zealand use higher order questions to gauge student learning and scaffold the next learning steps for the students.

Find out more about the 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
