*Super gonorrhoea; Time to clap back*

Among all sexually transmitted infections, gonorrhoea has the highest rates of antibiotic-resistance, creating a demand for new antimicrobial treatments. The aim of my research is to characterise a novel antibiotic target in the gonorrhoea bacteria using biochemical techniques. These results will lay down the foundations for future research into new treatments for gonorrhoea.
