ZAIRA NAJAM, Waikato Management School

*If you can’t measure, you can’t manage: Poverty*

This research measures and compares poverty trends at the district level in Pakistan using conventional and non-conventional poverty indices. Although these two indices are considered different, they are actually part of the same pattern, thus stressing the need for extensive trend analysis to better measure and manage poverty.

Find out more about the 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
