Description

JULIANA BROWN, School of Psychology

*Gender, Sex, and Sexuality Diversity within the University of Waikato Campus Space*

Research worldwide highlights the marginalisation experienced by gender, sex and sexuality diverse staff and students at universities. My research examines how welcoming the University of Waikato is for these communities.

Find out more about the 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
