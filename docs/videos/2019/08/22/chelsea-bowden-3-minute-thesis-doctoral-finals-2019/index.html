CHELSEA BOWDEN, School of Arts

*Queer Art Failure in Transgender Fiction*
My research identifies problematic patterns in fictional texts about queer gender and seeks to expose issues in representation, narrative and form in order to encourage more complex thinking about the multi-faceted nature of gender identity.

Find out more about the 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
