GRACE-LYNN CHONG, School of Science
*The Fish On Our Dish. What’s the catch?*
Are we just eating fish or are we consuming much more than that? What is invisible to the naked eye but may caused much harm and damage to our body? My research looks into the pollutants that accumulate in the fish that we eat, namely Methylmercury.

Find out more about the 3 Minute Thesis here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/research-degrees/workshops-events/3mt-doctoral
