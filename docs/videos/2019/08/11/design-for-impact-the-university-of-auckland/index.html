Recently, the role of designers in
society has shifted quite considerably.
Before, a designer would receive a brief and they
would create a product of some sort, or a service.
Now, designers are really helping
people to analyse organisations,
relationships, interactions, and help
to improve them in all kinds of ways.
And most importantly, they're paying a lot more
attention to the impact design has on society.
Designers have to understand the
relationship between design and
impact as a kind of an ongoing continuous loop.
So being able to understand how previous
designs have had an impact in the systems
that they operate helps us understand how we
can create better interventions in the future.
