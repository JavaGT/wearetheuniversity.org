Pacific Clubs on Campus

The University of Waikato is home to many Pacific Student Associations that look to enhance the overall student experience of our Pacific students by providing a culturally safe environment that welcomes our students from all across the Pacific. Our Student Associations have Annual Events which include celebrating the 7 Pacific Language Weeks and attending National Tertiary Conferences and Independence Day celebrations and are a constant feature in our campus wide events.

If you would like to join one of our Pacific Student Associations you can find out more at https://www.waikato.ac.nz/students/pacific-support/clubs
