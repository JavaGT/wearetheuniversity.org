Description

University of Waikato and Centres of Asia Pacific Excellence champion global learning in New Zealand's classrooms. Check out the new website here: https://teachapac.nz

The Centre for Asia Pacific Excellence's (CAPEs) program aims to positively impact the education of New Zealand students by enabling teachers to understand and impart knowledge of global competence, with a specific focus on Asia Pacific capability building. In conjunction with the University of Waikato, the TeachAPac website has been developed. It’s a website for all teachers in New Zealand schools and is informed by current national and international research evidence on global competence, global citizenship, and intercultural learning.

The website grows teachers’ understanding of the global competence they and their learners will need in order to interact with people from the Asia-Pacific and be good global citizens. It connects teachers to curated resources that support the integration of Asia-Pacific and Global Competence related learning into current and new curricula.
