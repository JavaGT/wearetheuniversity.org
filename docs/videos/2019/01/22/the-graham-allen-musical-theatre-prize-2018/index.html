Lili Taylor, 2nd Place
Pianist: Richard Marrett

The Graham Allen Prizes in Musical Theatre were established in 2014. The prize is set up in memory of Graham (Gumby) Allen and funded by his family and friends. Graham had a long history of performing in, directing and producing shows and had a particular love for the genre of musical theatre where he excelled in character performances. He always actively encouraged young people to try their hand at musical theatre and enjoyed the camaraderie of the casts in the multitude of performances in which he appeared.
