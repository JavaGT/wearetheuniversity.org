Study the Waikato MBM for a transformative business education

Oormi Nair, originally from India, talks about her experience of studying for the Master of Business and Management (MBM) at the University of Waikato, which prepared her for a successful career as a planning analyst at DeLaval, a a robotic milking systems manufacturer.
