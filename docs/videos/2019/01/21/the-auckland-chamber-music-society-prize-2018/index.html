Winner, 2018 Auckland Chamber Music Society Prize

Presented in association with Chamber Music Zealand, the Auckland Chamber Music Society Prize is awarded to the best chamber music ensemble at the School of Music. After an intensive week of chamber music examinations, up to four ensembles are selected to compete in the final.
