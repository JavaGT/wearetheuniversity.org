Description

The prestigious Kupe Leadership Scholarship offers a unique opportunity for exceptional students from around New Zealand to enrich a year of their postgraduate studies. At the launch for the 2019 Kupe Leadership Scholarships, Sir Peter Gluckman issued a challenge to the inaugural cohort of scholars as they embark on their year-long programme, which encompasses a leadership development programme, one-to-one mentoring with a respected leader from their field and the opportunity to attend a number of exclusive events and presentations.
