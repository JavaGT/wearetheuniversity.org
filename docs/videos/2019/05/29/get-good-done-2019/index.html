Description

Get Good Done is a free weekend idea hackathon run by the Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship at the University of Auckland. It is a chance for students to create ideas to solve the biggest environmental, social, and cultural issues of our time in line with the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals.
Find out more at: https://www.cie.auckland.ac.nz/programmes/get-good-done

Filmed and edited by University of Auckland students Cameron Irwin and Elise Dalrymple-Keast.
