Description

Meet Nicholas Soler from India. Nicholas is a Screen and Media Studies student studying at Waikato who hopes to reach his dream of working for Weta Digital. Waikato provides hands-on practical programmes that set graduates, like Nicholas, up to be highly employable. “Waikato definitely gave me the ability to learn practically with continuous support from my professors”. Nicholas is working part-time for a start-up company in Hamilton City while he studies and is looking forward to getting his three-year post study work visa after he graduates.

Read more stories about our international students at Waikato here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/success-stories?type=international
