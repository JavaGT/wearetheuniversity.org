Description

Business alumna and Executive Coach, Judith Jamieson, gives a brief introduction to Liz Wiseman’s theory on ‘Multipliers’, based on the bestselling book Multipliers: How the Best Leaders Make Everyone Smarter. Judith facilitated the first alumni careers seminar for 2019 at the Business School on this topic. She has been an executive coach for 15 years and founded Thriveatwork in 2017, which offers leadership development and executive coaching to leaders.
