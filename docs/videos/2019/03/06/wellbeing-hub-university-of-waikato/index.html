Welcome to the Wellbeing Hub! Drop in, chill out and carry on.

The Wellbeing Hub is your new space on campus to have a cup of tea, play some board games and have space to breathe from the hustle and bustle of campus life!

Head over to The Station on 164 Hillcrest Road, Hamilton NZ and check it out from 10am-2pm during the weekdays.

Make sure to follow them on Facebook www.facebook.com/wellbeingwaikatouni and Instagram www.instagram.com/wellbeinghubwaikatouni, and keep an eye out for the drop in classes that will be starting in March!
