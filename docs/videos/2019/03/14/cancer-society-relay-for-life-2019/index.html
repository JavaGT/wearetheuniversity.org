Description

Staff and students are encouraged to sign up for this year’s Cancer Society Otago Student Relay for Life – being held from 6pm on Saturday 30 March to 6am on Sunday 31 March.

The overnight team event aims to raise awareness and funds for cancer research, education and support programmes in the Otago and Southland regions.
