Description

This collaboration between the Auckland Philharmonia Orchestra and the University of Auckland School of Music provides young composers at both secondary and tertiary level with the opportunity to workshop, perform and record their original works.

Here's a video of our 2017 School of Music composers speaking about having their work performed and recorded. This was a part of the Inspire Partnership Programme.

School of Music, University of Auckland: www.music.auckland.ac.nz

Auckland Philharmonia Orchestra: www.apo.co.nz
