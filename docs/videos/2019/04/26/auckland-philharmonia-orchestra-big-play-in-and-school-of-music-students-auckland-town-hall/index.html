Description

Once a year, the Auckland Philharmonia Orchestra (APO) welcomes students from our School of Music, players from Auckland Youth Orchestra, as well as advanced music students currently at secondary school to join a workshop and performance at the Auckland Town Hall. Participants are seated next to APO musicians, paired one-to-one for an intense mentoring experience. The evening concludes with a free public performance. Here is a video from the Big Play In 2017.

This event was part of the Inspire Partnership Programme.
This programme is all about enhancing and nurturing aspiring young musicians through a partnership between the Auckland Philharmonia Orchestra and University of Auckland School of Music.

School of Music, University of Auckland: www.music.auckland.ac.nz

Auckland Philharmonia Orchestra: www.apo.co.nz
