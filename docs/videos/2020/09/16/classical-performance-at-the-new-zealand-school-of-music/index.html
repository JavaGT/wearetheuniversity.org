Description

Follow your dream and become an accomplished classical musician or gain the skills to teach others. You'll have the opportunity to learn from or work with some of New Zealand’s most acclaimed performers and teachers, including members of the New Zealand Symphony Orchestra (NZSO), and chamber musicians who perform and record internationally.

Discover the Classical Performance programme at the New Zealand School of Music—Te Kōkī, at Victoria University of Wellington: www.wgtn.ac.nz/nzsm
