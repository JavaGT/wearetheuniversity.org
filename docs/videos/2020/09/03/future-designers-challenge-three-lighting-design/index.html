Update: This Challenge has closed. Thank you everyone for interest!

This month we're challenging you to design lamps using milk bottle plastic or juice container plastic.

You can win some really great prizes! First place is $500. Just submit your designs on Instagram, YouTube, or Twitter using the #futuredesignersNZ

Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.

Study design at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-areas/design/overview
