Internationally sought-after judge recognised by University of Waikato for services to Aotearoa and Pacific nations – Judge Craig Coxhead

Bachelor of Social Sciences 1992, Bachelor of Laws 1995, Master of Laws 2000

Judge Craig Coxhead has dedicated his career to helping hapū and whānau to find legal solutions that will have a positive impact for generations to come. For that, he’ll receive a Distinguished Alumni Award from the University of Waikato.
