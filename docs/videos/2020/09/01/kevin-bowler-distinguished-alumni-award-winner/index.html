Marketing and leadership guru recognised by the University of Waikato for achievements – Kevin Bowler

Bachelor of Management Studies 1986

Kevin Bowler has more than 30 years’ experience in marketing and business leadership spanning consumer-packaged goods, technology, and travel brands in New Zealand and internationally. Now he has been recognised by the University of Waikato with a Distinguished Alumni Award for his successes.
