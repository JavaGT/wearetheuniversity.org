Join us online for Kīngitanga Day 2020! Hear from the University's Deputy Vice-Chancellor Māori, Sarah-Jane Tiakiwai, about Kīngitanga Day.

Each year we honour the University's unique history and relationship to the Kīngitanga. Jam-packed with presentations, kapa haka, and live feeds to the in-person events, make sure you tune in to the livestream from 8:40am this Kīngitanga Day.

Find out more here: https://events.waikato.ac.nz/events/kingitanga-day/
