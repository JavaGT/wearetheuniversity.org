A new approach to mental health in nursing | University of Waikato [1:50]

Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

With mental health needs rising sharply and services already stretched, we are responding to the challenge with the launch of the new Bachelor of Nursing in 2021, integrating a focus on mental health and addictions throughout the three-year degree.

Leading the development of the mental health focus of the new Waikato nursing programme is Tony O’Brien, a leading expert in mental health nursing. Thanks in large part to Tony’s contribution, the degree will be a significant departure from traditional approaches, with the Waikato programme recognising the need to take a more holistic view of a person and their life when providing nursing services.

Read the full story here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/news-opinion/media/2020/a-new-approach-to-mental-health-in-nursing

Find out about the Bachelor of Nursing here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/campaigns/nursing/
