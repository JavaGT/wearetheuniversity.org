Description

What’s it really like to work in politics? Our Parliamentary Internship course (POLS 428) gives postgraduate political science students the opportunity to go behind the scenes and work closely with an MP.

Master of Political Science student Bonnie Hayvice was an intern for Green Party MP Chlöe Swarbrick. In this video, Bonnie and Chlöe share their experiences of the Parliamentary Internship.

Find out more about postgraduate studies in Political Science: http://bit.ly/pg-political-science or about the course: http://bit.ly/parliament-internships
