Description

What is the price of not responding to the climate crisis? Chair in the Economics of Disasters in Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington's School of Economics and Finance, Professor Ilan Noy’s research is calculating the cost of climate change and looking at how we can prevent, prepare for and mitigate its economic impacts.
