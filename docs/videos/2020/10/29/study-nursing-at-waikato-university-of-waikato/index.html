Description

We are proud to launch our progressive new nursing degree which was developed in partnership the Waikato District Health Board (WDHB) and combines high academic standards with hands-on learning in a range of healthcare settings around the Waikato.

This is a truly hands-on programme with more clinical hours than any other provider and a special focus on mental health and addictions.

Nursing students will divide their time between our Hamilton campus and real health care settings around the Waikato, including Waikato Hospital.

Find out more here: bit.ly/waikatonursing
