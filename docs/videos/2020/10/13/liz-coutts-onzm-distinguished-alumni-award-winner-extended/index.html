Trail-blazing board director receives University of Waikato Distinguished Alumni Award – Liz Coutts ONZM

Bachelor of Management Studies 1981

At the age of 31, Liz Coutts took on her first CEO role. Three years later she became a director for Trust Bank New Zealand and she hasn’t looked back. Now she is being recognised by the University of Waikato with a Distinguished Alumni Award for her successes and contributions that she has made to the community.

Visit https://alumni.waikato.ac.nz/distinguished-alumni to learn more.
