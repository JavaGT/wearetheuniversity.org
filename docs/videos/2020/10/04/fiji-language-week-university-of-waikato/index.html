Ni sa Bula Vinaka

The 2020 Fijian Language theme is ‘Noqu Vosa, Noqu iSema Bula’ in English, “My language, my living link”.
It reflects our students constant connection between their language and everyday cultural identity.
This week is looking to be a great celebration of Fijian Language Week - invite a friend and get involved.
