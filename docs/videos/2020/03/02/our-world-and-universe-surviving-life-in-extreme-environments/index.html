Description

How can we adapt to survive in an increasingly warming world? Focusing on mitochondria, Associate Professor Tony Hickey explores the biological mechanisms that allow extraordinary marine life to thrive in even the harshest of environments and what this could tell us about our own adaptation to climate change.
