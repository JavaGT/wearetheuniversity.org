Description

Helping community thrive is at the heart of Anna’s passion to help enact change in the country, and through the University she is already applying her skills at Porirua Community Law as an intern. As Vice-President of the country’s only Asian Law Students’ Association (ALSA), she is also helping create a supportive community in a city Anna says is full of people always willing to help out.
