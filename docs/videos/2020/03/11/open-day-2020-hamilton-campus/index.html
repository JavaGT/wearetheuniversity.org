Description

Whether you’re leaving high school, considering a return to tertiary study or looking to study for the first time, we’ll have something for everyone at Open Day including:
+ Introductory lectures on courses and qualifications, accommodation, scholarships and how to get prepared for University
+ Live entertainment and fun activities throughout the day
+ Opportunities to meet current students and chat to them about their experiences
+ Tours of the campus and Halls of Residence
+ Information sessions for parents, caregivers and careers advisers
+ Drop in sessions with advisors so you can get advice on study options, pathway programmes and the enrolment process.

Join us at our Hamilton Campus Open Day on 31 July 2020!

Find out more here: waikato.ac.nz/go/openday
