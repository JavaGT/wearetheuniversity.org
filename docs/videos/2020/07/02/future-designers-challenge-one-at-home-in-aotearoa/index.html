Update: This Challenge has closed. Thank you everyone for interest!

Participate in the Future Designers Challenge! We are challenging you to join in the rich tradition of designing window displays. Design a window display that makes the viewer smile and connects us as a community.

Submit your designs by 1st July 2020 on Youtube, Instagram or Twitter using the hashtag #FutureDesignersNZ. Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz/challenges/challenge-1-at-home-in-aotearoa/

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by School of Design Innovation, Victoria University of Wellington. https://futuredesigners.nz

Study design at Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-areas/design/overview
