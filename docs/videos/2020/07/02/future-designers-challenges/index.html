Future Designers is a design community for high school students & teachers hosted by School of Design Innovation, Victoria University of Wellington. Study design with us: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-areas/design/overview

Kia ora! We are part of the Future Designers Programme Ka Tū Kaihoahoa ā Mua at School of Design Innovation, Te Kura Hoahoa, Victoria University of Wellington, Te Herenga Waka.

We are reaching out to connect to our Aotearoa Art and Design community and to support students and high school teachers. This will be a chance for all of you in high school and are interested in Art and Design, to participate in our challenges and to win some amazing prizes. Our first challenge will start on the 1st of June 2020.

If you have any burning questions, post them in the comments below or tag us at #FutureDesignersNZ. We will be happy to provide the best possible answer. We are looking forward to hearing from you!

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation. https://futuredesigners.nz
