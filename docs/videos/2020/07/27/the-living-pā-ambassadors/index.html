Description

Our Living Pā ambassadors share our ambitions to apply the Living Building ChallengeTM, the most rigorous environmental performance standard globally, and join an elite list of one of the world’s most environmentally responsible buildings. The Living Pā will be a multi-purpose hub that embodies our commitment to growing mātauranga Māori (Māori knowledge), securing wellbeing for Papatūānuku (the natural environment) and for future generations.
