Description

We’re now in # COVID19 Level 2, which means that we’ll start welcoming you back to campus! But to keep all of our community safe, there will be a few changes to life on campus.

We are introducing a QR code contact tracing system to keep track of where our students, staff and visitors have been on our campuses. Whether it’s to go to class, get a coffee, to the library, to a bathroom or to Unirec, by checking in everywhere you go, it means we can let you know if you may have been exposed to a confirmed case of COVID-19 on campus.

#CheckInBeforeYouStepIn on our website here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/covid-19/check-in-before-you-step-in

Find the most up to date information about the University's response to COVID-19 including frequently asked questions here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/covid-19/
