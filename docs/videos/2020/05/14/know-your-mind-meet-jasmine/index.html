A love of music is what drew Jasmine Hall to study at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington. The 21-year-old student enrolled in a Bachelor of Music, majoring in piano performance, but her other love—mathematics—was never far from her thoughts. She also sat in on classes in Discrete Maths and the subject "clicked straight away".

"I realised that, although I enjoyed my music study, I wanted to major in maths and keep music for outside of university."
