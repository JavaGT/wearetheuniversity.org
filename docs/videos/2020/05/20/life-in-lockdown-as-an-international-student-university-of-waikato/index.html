Hear how life in lockdown and studying online has been for International Student Beatriec.

The University of Waikato moved its classes online as part of New Zealand's response to managing COVID-19. Beatriec shares her story of what her day looks like during this time. Find out how she has found studying online and how the university has helped her feel supported.
