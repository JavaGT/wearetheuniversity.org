Description

Alapan, an International Student from India, shares his experience of life in lockdown.

Alapan has been studying towards an MBM at the University of Waikato for the past 12 months. He was looking forward to graduating during our Autumn graduation. Covid-19 put a hold to those plans and instead Alapan had the unique experience of graduating virtually from home. Alapan was especially delighted with his personal email from the university and the virtual celebrations shared on social media. "We love celebrating student success here at the University of Waikato" says Alapan.

Find out more about studying at the University of Waikato here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/international
