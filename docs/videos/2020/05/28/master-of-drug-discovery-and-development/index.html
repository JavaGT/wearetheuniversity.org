Description

Research in drug discovery and development enables the identification of new drug targets and therapeutics. The Master of Drug Discovery and Development is a full year 180-point programme offered at Victoria University of Wellington in a collaboration with the Centre for Biodiscovery, the Ferrier Research Institute and the Schools of Biological Sciences and Chemical and Physical Sciences.
