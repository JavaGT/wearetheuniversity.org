Update: This Challenge has closed. Thank you everyone for interest!

Participate in the Future Designers Challenge! We are challenging you to create your very own looping animation! You have 48 frames in which to create your animated masterpiece.

Submit your designs by 1st September 2020 on Youtube, Instagram or Twitter using the hashtag #FutureDesignersNZ. Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.

Study design at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-areas/design/overview
