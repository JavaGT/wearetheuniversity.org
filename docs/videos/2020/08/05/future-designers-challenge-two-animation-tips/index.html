Hard at work on your Future Designers looping animation challenge? Here are some animation tips that might help!

For all the details about the challenge, check out this video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lw_vSxcI8YA

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.
