Dr Htin Lin Aung started his journey here in Foundation Year which gave him the building blocks to lead him onto greater things. He now is making a difference to communities researching tuberculosis and working as the Sir Charles Hercus Health Research Fellow at the department of Microbiology and Immunology here at Otago.
