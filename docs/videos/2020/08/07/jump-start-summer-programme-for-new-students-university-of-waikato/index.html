Description

Are you new to University? Would you like to start your studies early? Do you want to make Uni friends and boost your study skills? Join our Jumpstart programme and get started with your degree earlier!

You can take the 1st paper in your degree and get set-up for the rest of your studies; find your way around campus, and make Uni friends all before the end of summer. There will be several 100-level (15 point) papers you can choose from. These papers will suit any new student, but particularly those who have lower NCEA credits (or equivalent) in core areas such as literacy, maths, and science.

This programme is also great for those who would like to make friends and ease into understanding the Uni environment and learn core Uni study skills. Alongside the paper you choose, you will attend sessions teaching you important tertiary study skills, how to use the Uni technology and so much more - all aimed at helping you be successful at University.

Find out more about Jumpstart here: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/study/campaigns/jump-start/
