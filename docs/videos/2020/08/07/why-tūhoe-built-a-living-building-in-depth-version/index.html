Description

At Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington, Kirsti Luke (CEO, Te Uru Taumatua) and Tāmati Kruger (Chair, Te Urewera Board and Te Uru Taumatua) talk about why Tūhoe built Te Kura Whare, the first fully certified living building in the Southern Hemisphere. They also reflect on the symbolism of living buildings and the impacts upon Tūhoe.
