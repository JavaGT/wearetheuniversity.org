Inaugural Staff Sustainability Excellence Award winner, Professor Brenda Vale (Victoria University of Wellington's School of Architecture) speaks about her sustainability journey. From designing and building the UK's first 'autonomous house' with her husband in the early 1970s, to the couple publishing a 'real guide to sustainable living' book in 2009 titled Time to eat the dog?
