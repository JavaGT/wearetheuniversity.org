Description

Dr Rebecca Priestley is an Associate Professor in the Centre for Science in Society at Victoria University. In this short clip, Rebecca talks about “Skua,” a used goods recycling facility at McMurdo Station, on Ross Island, Antarctica. This clip is an excerpt from a video lecture from Antarctic Science and Culture, a fully online course which features video lectures recorded on location in Antarctica. To find out more, go to: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/courses/SCIS/313/
