The petition that sparked te reo Māori revitalisation [5:03]

Description

Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Māori), Professor Rawinia Higgins speaks about the importance of the Māori Language Petition that was delivered to Parliament in 1972 to te reo Māori revitalisation, and the important role Victoria University students played in this journey. Professor Higgins also comments on the future of te reo Māori in Aotearoa.
