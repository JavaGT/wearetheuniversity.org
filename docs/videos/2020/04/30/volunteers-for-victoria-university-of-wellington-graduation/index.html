When you first arrive at the Michael Fowler Centre, go to the Frank Taplin room to sign in.

collect the reception pack before starting.
The pack includes a cellphone

envelopes of tickets to be picked up

Your role is to give out any tickets been

As a Ground Floor Usher, you
provide directions and answer questions

As a Dresser, you help staff with their academic dress and make sure they are dressed correctly.
