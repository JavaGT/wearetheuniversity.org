Description

The School of Chemical and Physical Sciences at Victoria University of Wellington offers students the chance to study in a truly international environment. Located in the heart of Wellington, New Zealand’s largest scientific community, we attract the best and brightest from all over the world. Combining our expertise in chemistry and physics gives us an edge in materials science research, and collaborations take our discoveries from the lab to the world.
