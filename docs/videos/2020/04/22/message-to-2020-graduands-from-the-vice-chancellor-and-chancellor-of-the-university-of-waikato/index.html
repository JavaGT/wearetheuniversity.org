Congratulations to our Waikato Graduates of the Class of 2020!

We're celebrating and congratulating all of our Waikato graduates who were scheduled to graduate in Hamilton at Claudelands and Te Kohinga Mārama Marae, and in Tauranga.

Although we can't celebrate and commemorate you in person, our Vice-Chancellor Professor Neil Quigley, and Chancellor Chancellor Rt Hon Sir Anand Satyanand, share their message of congratulations with you all.
