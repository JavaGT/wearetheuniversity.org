Description

The Boon on Campus festival featured five artists from Waikato and Bay of Plenty, who painted six murals around the University of Waikato - Hamilton campus ranging between 2m-19m long.

The murals bring a fresh and vibrant look to some of the University’s most visible teaching buildings, which largely have plain concrete facades, reflecting the era in which they were built.
