Description

This fly-through, created by CCM Architects, shows the University of Otago’s new $26 million Music, Theatre and Performing Arts Centre which will be up and running for Semester One 2020 on the Dunedin Campus.

The complex includes a new two-storey recording studio building with nine purpose-built studios and music teaching/practice rooms.

The two-storey main studio houses a spacious control room, two isolation booths for recording and the principal recording space - capable of accommodating an orchestra and of hosting performances for an audience of more than 100.
