Description

If you are a new Pacific student starting with us this year than our Pacific Orientation is a MUST!

We will traditionally welcome you with a Kava ceremony hosted by our Tongan students association and give you the chance to meet your Pacific Support team led by Aunty Keaka- Assistant Vice Chancellor Pacific.

Your Pacific Support team will include cousins, aunties and uncles from:
- Pacific Student Associations
- Student Learning
- Student Health
- Accessibility
- Scholarships
- Careers
- Accommodation
- Pacific Divisional Support
- Library

You will also get a tour of the Conch - your Pacific space - plus get the best tips on how to do well at University from our student leaders.

All of this and LUNCH is on us.

Your aiga, ohana are invited as well- just let us know by emailing pacificsupport@waikato.ac.nz.

See you then
