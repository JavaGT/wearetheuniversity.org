Description

Listen to Tyrone McAuley—Chief Operating Officer of PikPok, one New Zealand's largest game development companies speak about his experiences as an engineering graduate.

Oral narratives is a series of educational videos that have been created to inspire New Zealand's Māori and Pasifika tertiary education students—showcasing actual student assignments that have embraced cultural frameworks. Join us as we journey into the world of risk taking, colouring outside the lines, creative experimentation, and storytelling.
