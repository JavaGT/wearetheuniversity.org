Description

Te Kāhui Pīrere is a programme designed to support Māori students transition from the comforts of home into their new university life. Unique to the University of Waikato, Te Kāhui Pīrere equips tauira with academic support, advice and assistance, networking, goal setting and more!

Find out more: https://www.waikato.ac.nz/maori/Te-Kaahui-Piirere
