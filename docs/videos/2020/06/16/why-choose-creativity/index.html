We often describe ourselves as imaginative,

in fact, humans are predisposed to be creative to

Creativity drives innovation.
From the radical visionaries of Silicon
Valley to life-saving medical research
and that ability to change the world or
to adapt to changes we can't control will
be integral to our success in this
time of technological revolution.
So how do we prepare your generation
to make the most of that new world?
The creative arts are our secret weapon.
Take the STEM subjects—science,
technology, engineering, mathematics.
When you incorporate the
creative arts into teaching them,
students' results improve in every subject.
We need to embed creativity into
our education system from preschool
to university so that as a creative
nation we can invent a fantastic future.
