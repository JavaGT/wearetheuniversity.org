Description

As we live through this extraordinary experience, one of the most important things we've needed is information. Thankfully, during this time, our health professionals, our media, our community, and our country’s experts have had trusted leaders they can turn to.

They are sharing the knowledge needed to help protect New Zealanders and keep our country moving even when it felt like the world around us stopped.

It is an honour and a privilege for Otago to be playing its part.
