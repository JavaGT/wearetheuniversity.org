I'm Brad Jackson and I'm the Head of School of Government at Victoria University of Wellington.
The painting is called Christ Driving the Money Changers from the Temple.
It was painted by Michael Smither in 1972.
Back in 2002, I was Head of School of Management at Victoria University
and that painting was the painting that sat opposite my office as Head of School.
Intriguingly enough, after I conducted my interview for the Fletcher Building Chair at
the University of Auckland, I was attending a Michael Smither retrospective
at the Auckland Art Gallery and I was looking at that very same painting
because it was on loan from the Victoria collection when the Dean phoned me up to offer me the job there.
Seven or eight years later, I'm back at Victoria and I was delighted to see that
painting being put outside my office as Head of School of Government on the
8th floor and so this painting is following me and got a lot of very good memories associated with it.
