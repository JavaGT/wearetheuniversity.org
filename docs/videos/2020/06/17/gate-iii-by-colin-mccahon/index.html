Description

Gate III by Colin McCahon is one of the largest and most important artworks in the Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington Art Collection. Hear Emeritus Professor Tim Beaglehole talk about his purchase of this painting, which is currently situated in the foyer of Rutherford House.
