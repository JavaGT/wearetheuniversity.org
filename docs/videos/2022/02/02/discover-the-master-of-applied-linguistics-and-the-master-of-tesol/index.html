Description

Dr Jonathan Newton dives into two of our Master's options: The Master of Applied Linguistics and the Master of Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL).
The Master of Applied Linguistics will develop your knowledge and expertise in Applied Linguistics, and you will design research projects related to your own professional needs and interests.
The Master of TESOL will develop your expertise in teaching English—or any language—to speakers of other languages.
