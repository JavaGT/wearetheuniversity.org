Description

Wellington Faculty of Education's Dr Jae Major introduces the Graduate Diploma of Teaching, the professional practice programme preparing people to become the next generation of teachers in Aotearoa.
At Te Herenga Waka, you can choose to focus on Early Childhood, Primary, or Secondary school teaching in your Graduate Diploma of Teaching.
Find out more at https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/teacher-education
