Description

Take your degree and experience to the next level and become an expert in your area of interest in education with a Master of Education.

This programme is a great opportunity for educators, policymakers, and those in related fields to complete a postgraduate qualification that can be designed to suit a specific field of interest, and fitted around work commitments.
