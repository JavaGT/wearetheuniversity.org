Description

The School of Design Innovation is running an interaction design competition. We are challenging you to create a digital version of a physical interaction such as a high five, a tap on the shoulder, or a hug. You can submit screen mock-ups, a video or a storyboard of the interaction, you don't have to actually make it work.

Submit photos or videos of your designs to YouTube, Instagram, or Twitter using #futuredesignersnz for full details about the competition, go to our website: https://futuredesigners.nz/interaction-design/

Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.

Study design at Te Herenga Waka—Victoria University of Wellington: https://www.wgtn.ac.nz/explore/study-...
