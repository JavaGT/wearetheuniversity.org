Description

Executive and Professional Development’s Transitioning to C-Suite Programme - Strategic Supply Chain Management specialisation develops mid-career professionals from various parts of the supply chain into strategic supply chain leaders. Tava Olsen, Director of the Centre for Supply Chain Management, discusses the need for the programme to develop the supply chain ecosystem in New Zealand and the four key research themes the centre focuses on. Andrew Kyle, Officer Commanding Technical Support with the Royal New Zealand Air Force, talks about the skills and experience he took from the programme.
