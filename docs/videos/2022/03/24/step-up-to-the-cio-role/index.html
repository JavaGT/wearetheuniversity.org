Description

The Transitioning to C-Suite Programme - Strategic Information Technology specialisation from the University of Auckland’s Executive and Professional Development team, prepares future IT leaders to be CIOs in today’s world. Dawie Olivier, CIO at Westpac and member of our CIO Advisory Group, talks about the contribution the programme has made to his business, while Patrea Jones, Platform Owner – Account & Pricing and Database, ASB, discusses the value she found in the programme.
