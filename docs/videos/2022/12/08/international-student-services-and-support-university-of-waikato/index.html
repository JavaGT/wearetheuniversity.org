Description

Throughout your studies at Waikato, you will be supported by our dedicated International Student Services Team along with our student health, careers, sport and wellbeing and accommodation teams.

Those students who choose to start with us online will have access to virtual support services to ensure they have everything they need to adapt and succeed while off-campus.

Check out our online programmes - https://www.waikato.ac.nz/go/internat...

Find out if Waikato is the right fit for you by exploring our Virtual Open Day - waikato.ac.nz/international/why-study-at-waikato/virtual-experience-tour.
