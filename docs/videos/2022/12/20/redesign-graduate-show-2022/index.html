Creative Arts and Industries, the University of Auckland

Description

The Design Programme at Waipapa Taumata Rau University of Auckland challenges traditionally siloed design disciplines, exploring the power of design thinking across various industries. Design is one of the only disciplines that bridges many fields, and it is at this intersection that great things are possible.

The ReDesign Graduate Show exhibition features work by our first graduating students. It is a key opportunity for the public to view our students' investigations into various social, environmental and political issues.

Browse the online portfolio of work and research from graduating Design students: https://redesign.ac.nz/
