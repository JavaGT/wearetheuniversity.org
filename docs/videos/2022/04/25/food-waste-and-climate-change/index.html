University of Otago - Ōtākou Whakaihu Waka

Description

This informational video was created as part of a Food Waste Innovation seed funded project, conducted by the Department of Sociology (supervised by Professor Hugh Campbell) and Generation Zero (Finn Campbell and Lydia Le Gros). The premise of the project was to create a resource targeted towards University of Otago students, emphasising food waste as not just a financial and social issue, but as an environmental and climate change issue. In doing so, the goal of this video is to engage students, particularly those who are concerned about climate change, to engage with the problem of food waste as well.
