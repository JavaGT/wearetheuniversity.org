Description

He Kaupapa Hononga is a network of University of Otago researchers and collaborators interested in climate change and its impacts. We support interdisciplinary research that utilises and integrates knowledge from a range of sources, including mātauranga Māori - because climate change is a complicated, multifaceted process that requires a united approach at many different levels to achieve the best outcomes possible. Come join us. https://www.otago.ac.nz/he-kaupapa-hononga
