The University of Waikato

Description

Brand new in 2022, Selwyn Street Studios offers self-catered living close to the University's Tauranga campus. Suitable for students at all levels including school leavers, international and postgraduate students, the studio style rooms come fully furnished and include their own en suite bathroom.

Selwyn Street Studios is the perfect place to settle into university life, gain independence and make friends in a safe environment.
