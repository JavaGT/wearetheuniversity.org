Description

For the Furious Frames Competition, we are challenging you to create your very own looping animation! You have 48 frames in which to create your animated masterpiece. There are $850 of prizes up for grabs

DEADLINE: 12th December 2022

Submit your designs on Youtube, Instagram or Twitter using the hashtag #FutureDesignersNZ. Detailed instructions, terms & conditions and info about prizes on https://futuredesigners.nz

Future Designers is a design community for New Zealand high school students & teachers hosted by the School of Design Innovation.
