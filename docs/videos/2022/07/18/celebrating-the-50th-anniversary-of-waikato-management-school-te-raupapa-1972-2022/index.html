Celebrating the 50th anniversary of Waikato Management School (Te Raupapa): 1972-2022 [2:14]

Watch on Youtube

The University of Waikato

Description

Since 1972, Waikato Management School (Te Raupapa) has always been a trailblazer in management and business education. Fifty years on and 24,000 graduates later, we are proud that same spirit of innovation remains with us today.

This year WMS was named #1 in New Zealand for Business & Economics (Times Higher Education Rankings), and we are positioned in the top 1% of business schools globally with our prestigious Triple Crown Accreditation.

Our alumni include a host of successful CEOs and iwi leaders, the current prime minister of New Zealand, and many other fantastic people making a positive contribution to society.
