Te Taiata o Te Rangi grew up in Te Teko where she attended Edgecumbe College. After getting a call from her Uncle about the opportunities she would have by getting a kickstart to go to University, she decided this would be an awesome way for her to navigate her way through things before semester started and make some new friends. This is her experience.
