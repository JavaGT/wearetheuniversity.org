Description

Four University of Otago students with a passion for music were selected as the inaugural SIX60 Scholarship recipients – they share their musical talent with us, and what it’s like to live together in the famous 660 Castle Street flat.

Find out more about the University of Otago SIX60 Scholarship and see if you are eligible – closing date is 31 July. https://bit.ly/SIX60Scholarship
