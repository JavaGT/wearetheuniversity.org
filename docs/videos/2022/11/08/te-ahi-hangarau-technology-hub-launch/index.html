Description

Te Ahi Hangarau is the Business School’s new 5G technology hub, Te Ahi Hangarau, which has been built in partnership with Vodafone, and administered by the Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship. The name gifted to our 5G technology hub comprises of ‘Ahi’ which means fire and ‘Hangarau’ translates as technology. Through offering students at the University of Auckland the opportunity to explore new technologies at Te Ahi Hangarau, we aim to ignite inspiration for problem-solving and help create tech-savvy and future-ready graduates. Learn more at: https://www.cie.auckland.ac.nz/locations/technology-hub/
