Description

Hear from Sianiti, our Pacific Engagement Manager talk about the Pacific at Waikato student experience and the support that is avaialble to Pacific students. Just some of the ways that Pacific students are supported and celebrated by the University of Waikato include Pacific student associations, scholarships, transition to study programmes, a dedicated Pacific student space (The Conch) and Pacific Support Staff.
