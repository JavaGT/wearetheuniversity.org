The University of Waikato

Description

Tauira Māori are visible and actively engaged in all aspects of University life, through Māori student rōpū across the University, Puutikitiki, our Māori student space and a dedicated leadership role in the Waikato Students’ Union. Māori Ki Waikato caters for our first year tauira through to our PHD tauira.

This video will take you through the support available to Māori Students from the beginning to the end to ensure your time here at Te Whare Wānanga o Waikato is a success.
