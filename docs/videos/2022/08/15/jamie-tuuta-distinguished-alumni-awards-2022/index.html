Description

At the age of 19 Jamie Tuuta (Ngāti Mutunga, Ngāti Tama, Te Ati Awa, Ngāti Maru, Taranaki Tuturu) was the rangitahi representative on the Ngāti Mutunga board and by 34 years old he was the Māori trustee and CEO of Te Tumu Paeroa.

For services to his communities, he is a recipient of a Distinguished Alumni Award.
