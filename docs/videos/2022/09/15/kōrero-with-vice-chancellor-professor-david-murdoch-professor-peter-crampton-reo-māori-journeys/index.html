University of Otago - Ōtākou Whakaihu Waka

Description

The challenge to speak te reo Māori has been taken on by Vice-Chancellor, Professor David Murdoch and former Pro-Vice-Chancellor of Health Sciences, Professor Peter Crampton. In our recent kōrero with them, we discussed their reo Māori journeys, thoughts on the role that tauiwi have in language preservation and more.
