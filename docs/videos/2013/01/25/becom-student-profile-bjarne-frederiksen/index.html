Description

We chat to University of Waikato student, Bjarne Frederiksen, about the Waikato Management School's Online Summer School papers in e-commerce. Bjarne explains why the Bachelor of Electronic Commerce degree is useful in today's technologically advanced society and why it is so crucial graduates remain at the cutting edge of technology in order to be successful in e-commerce business.

For more information about the Waikato Management School e-commerce papers, please see http://bit.ly/RB68du.
