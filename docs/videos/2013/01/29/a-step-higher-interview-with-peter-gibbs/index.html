Description

The Sir Edmund Hillary Scholarship Programme educates future leaders at the University of Waikato within the values of New Zealand's greatest adventurer, Sir Edmund Hillary.

We are all inspired by the view that "Together, we believe the impossible is a summit to be conquered". The Step Higher award gives selected University of Waikato Sir Edmund Hillary scholars the chance to go to Nepal to see some of the work Sir Ed did in the Nepalese communities, undertake humanitarian work with schools and villages, and brave the elements of an enduring trek through the Khumbu Valley in Nepal.

In this video, we chat to Calder & Lawson Group Tour Leader, Peter Gibbs, about the upcoming trip and what our scholars will experience as they negotiate the rocky heights of the Everest Region. This trip is sponsored by the Compass Group.
