Description

The diversity of worldviews is often lost in the polarities of sameness and difference. What about differences within categories deemed to be the same or similarities across categories? What about the spaces in between?

In this video, we chat to the University of Waikato's Professor Debashish Munshi, about his upcoming Inaugural Professorial Lecture which will explore the many stimulating colours that lie trapped in the shades of grey between and around the black and white of issues.

The lecture takes place from 6pm on October 29 at the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts. The lecture is free and open to the public. For more information, please visit: http://bit.ly/YYnOk2.
