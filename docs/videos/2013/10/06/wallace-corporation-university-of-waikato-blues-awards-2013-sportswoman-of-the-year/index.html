Description

The Wallace Corporation University of Waikato Blues (Blues) are held in October each year, and is one of Waikato University's strongest traditions, dating back to 1971, with the tradition of the "cultural" blue joining the sporting blue in 1990.

This year the Sportswoman of the Year award was presented to University of Waikato's Natalie Dodd. Natalie was selected for the New Zealand White Ferns women's cricketing team in 2010.

We caught up with her for a chat around her win and the challenges she's been up against on the road to success.

For more information around the Wallace Corporation University of Waikato Blues Awards along with all prize winners, please see http://bit.ly/SGSQ2D.
