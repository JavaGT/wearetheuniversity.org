Description

The University of Waikato was once again a strategic partner of Te Matatini - New Zealand's national kapa haka competition and premier Māori cultural performing arts festival being held in Rotorua this month.

The University is proud to have Waikato University staff members and students involved with the kapa haka groups that are performing at this event, along with other staff members who will be judging.

In this short video we chat with University of Waikato staff members at the festival and ask them what it means to be involved in this premier Māori cultural event.

For more information about Te Matatini 2013, please see http://bit.ly/i2Yjf4.
