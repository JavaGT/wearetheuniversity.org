Description

We would like to welcome new students to the University of Waikato. In this video, we chat to Sharon and Alison, the friendly Careers Advisers from the University of Waikato Careers & Employment Centre, about the free careers service available for students on campus. They also talk about the upcoming careers fairs and employment presentations about to take place on campus. Don't miss these events!

For more information, please visit the Careers & Employment Centre website: http://bit.ly/O284Kq.
