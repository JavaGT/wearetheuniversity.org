Description

Don't think you're the only one wondering what is needed on the first day of university at Waikato. This is a common question and many find themselves wondering where to start and what to bring for the first day of class.

In this video, University of Waikato Student Recruitment Adviser, Hannah Porter, gives a few tips and advice around starting university and the things you should know for the first week of study. We hope you find this useful.

For more information about Academic Orientation, please see the University of Waikato website: http://bit.ly/12l3b8c.
