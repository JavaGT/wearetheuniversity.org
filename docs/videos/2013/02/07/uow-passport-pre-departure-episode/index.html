Description

We would like to welcome all international students to the University of Waikato! As a new international student, there are a few things you should know prior to leaving your home country.

International Student Advisor, Huy Vu, provides us with a few tips and advice around arrival and student orientation. He also provides some very useful information for arrival on campus, dates of important orientation events and general information to help make your transition to Waikato as easy as possible.

We hope you find this video useful. Please feel free to contact us via email (info@waikato.ac.nz) or through our international Facebook page (http://on.fb.me/X6bJff) if you have any questions. We look forward to seeing you here at Waikato!
