Description

The University of Waikato Careers Fair once again took place on the Village Green. This year we saw a variety of companies come along to support the event and talk to students about graduate opportunities available.

The day provided a chance for graduate students to network with professionals in their chosen industry and chat to companies about graduate programmes on offer.

We caught up with Waikato Career Advisers, Sharon Jefferies and Alison Crean to find out how the event went.
