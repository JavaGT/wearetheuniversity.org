Description

Find out about the history of The University of Auckland's Summer Shakespeare productions. This video features interviews with Emeritus Professor Nicholas Tarling - Department of History, Professor Tom Bishop -- Department of English and Former Director of Maidment Theatre, Paul Minifie. Film and music by students of The University of Auckland. Live footage from the Summer Shakespeare 50th anniversary production of King Lear.

Thanks to The University of Auckland Library Special Collections for access to archive material.

Discover more about the history of The University of Auckland: http://www.auckland.ac.nz/history
