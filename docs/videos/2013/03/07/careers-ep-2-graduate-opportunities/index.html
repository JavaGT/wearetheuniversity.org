Description

Welcome back! This month is a very busy month for Employer Presentations and Career Expos taking place on campus.

We caught up with Sharon Jefferies from the university's Careers & Employment Centre to chat about some of the graduate events coming up. Sharon explains how important these events are in connecting with future employers and expanding your career opportunities.

For more information about graduate events on campus, please see the Careers & Employment Centre's Facebook page here: http://on.fb.me/108tEC1.
