Description

For Māori secondary school students interested in property and related careers, this video includes interviews with employers, students and iwi representatives, and explains the advantages of a property degree from the University of Auckland Business School.

In an environment where the Māori economy is already estimated to be about $40billion and rapidly expanding, iwi and hapu face the huge responsibility of both asset retention and maximising returns from both agricultural and off-farm investments, many of which are facilitated from Treaty settlements.

It is increasingly important to have top-quality Māori with property degrees available to provide up-to-date guidance and expertise along with cultural knowledge and background.

Find out more: http://www.business.auckland.ac.nz/uoa/home/about/od-our-departments/od-property
