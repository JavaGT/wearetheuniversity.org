Description

David Bryant is the information systems manager at New Zealand-owned company Tasti Products. Having just embarked on his MBA after completing a two-year Postgraduate Diploma in Business, David is currently working on a project with a team of other MBA students to help an Auckland-based firm find an international location to manufacture its product. Find out if the University of Auckland MBA pathways are right for you. The Graduate School of Management runs information sessions about the Postgraduate Diploma of Business and MBA programmes at 5.30pm on the first Wednesday of every month.

Find out more: http://mba.auckland.ac.nz
