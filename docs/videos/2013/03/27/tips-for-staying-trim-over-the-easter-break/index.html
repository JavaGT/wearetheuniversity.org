Description

Inclined to over-indulge in chocolate and hot cross buns over Easter? I think we're all guilty here, right?

We caught up with the helpful team at the University of Waikato's Rec Centre for a few exercise and nutrition tips around staying fit and healthy during the Easter period. Exercise Consultants Maria Jarvis and Ryan Overmayer demonstrate a few easy exercises that can be done at home, or while away on holiday, and also a few tips for healthy eating.
