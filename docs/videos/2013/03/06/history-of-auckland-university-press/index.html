Description

Find out about the history of Auckland University Press, New Zealand's leading scholarly publisher. This video features interviews with the Director of Auckland University Press -- Sam Elworthy, Former Registrar of The University of Auckland Warwick Nicoll, and former Managing Director of Penguin NZ and Scholastic NZ -- Graham Beattie. Film and music by students of The University of Auckland.

Discover more about the history of The University of Auckland: http://www.auckland.ac.nz/history
