Description

The University of Waikato will once again play a major part at this year's New Zealand Agricultural Fieldays.

We caught up with Director of the Carbon Dating Lab at the University of Waikato, Dr. Alan Hogg, for a quick chat around the work he's undertaking in carbon dating sub-fossil swamp Kauri in the Northland area.

Dr. Hogg explains some of the most interesting aspects to his work and how the carbon dating process is used in science today.

To learn more about this research, please visit the University of Waikato at this year's New Zealand Agricultural Fieldays, site PF10-12 or see http://bit.ly/On756R.
