Description

Singer, songwriter and cruiseship headliner, Ash Puriri, graduated yesterday at Waikato University's Te Kohinga Mārama marae.

Ash of Ngāti Kahungunu, Ngāti Rongomai-wahine and Ngāti Porou graduated with a Masters in Management Studies. There was plenty of whānau there to support him including his four children aged 11 to 16.

We caught up with Ash for a quick chat around his success as an entertainer and how The University of Waikato has set him up in his career.
