Description

The Great Waka Ama Race 2013 saw 15 University teams battle it out on the Waitemata harbour on Saturday 6 April. Faculties, halls and students clubs raced 8 km from Okahu Bay to Kohimarama and back in smooth and sunny conditions.
Amongst the top teams was the NZ Navy with a student and alumni team who were first across the line. The first student team was Grafton Hall, followed by Huia Hall in 2nd Place and Nga Tauira Maori in 3rd.
Over 150 supporters showed up to the Royal Akarana Yacht Club to watch the teams cross the finish line and celebrate at prize giving.
