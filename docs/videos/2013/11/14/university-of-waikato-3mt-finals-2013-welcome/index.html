Description

The 3MT competition, held during Postgraduate Month in October, is for Higher Degree candidates enrolled in the PhD, MPhil, EdD, DMA or SJD programmes. The competition challenges candidates to present a compelling oration on their thesis and its significance in just three minutes in language appropriate to an intelligent but non-specialist audience.

In this video, University of Waikato Deputy Vice-Chancellor, Professor Alister Jones, welcomes guests to the evening.
