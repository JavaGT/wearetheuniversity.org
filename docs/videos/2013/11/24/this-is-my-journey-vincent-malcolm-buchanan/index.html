Description

University of Waikato PhD student and staff member, Vincent Malcolm-Buchanan, is now completing his fifth degree. In this video interview, Vincent explains why higher education is so special to him and his whanau, and why he would recommend postgraduate study to other Māori and mature students.

Follow Vince's journey and find out what the University has to offer for Māori students looking for a world-class degree.
