Description

Stride for Success 2013 saw hundreds of student runners take to the pavements on Sunday 5 May. The course was a 2km circuit around University grounds and Albert Park and participants could run as individuals in the 10km Race or rally together a team and compete in the 3-HOUR relay style event. The running village was in the Alumni Marquee where the runners enjoyed music, a free BBQ and the battle for best dressed team. Congratulations to all those who took part this year!
