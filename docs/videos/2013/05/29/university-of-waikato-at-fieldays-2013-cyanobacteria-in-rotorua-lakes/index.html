Description

University of Waikato's Professor Hamilton is working with Bay of Plenty Regional Council, Rotorua District Council and Te Arawa Lakes Trust, to assist in the restoration of the Rotorua lakes by providing science to assist a large number of different restoration initiatives.

To learn more about this research, visit the University of Waikato's stand at the 2013 National Fieldays, site PF10-12. For more information, please see http://bit.ly/On756R.
