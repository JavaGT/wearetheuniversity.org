Description

Find out about the history of The University of Auckland's Art Collection. This video features interviews with Linda Tyler - Director of the Centre for Art Studies, Emeritus Professor Wystan Curnow - Department of English, and Stephanie McKenzie - Former Project Assistant, Cultural Collections Project.
Film and music by students of The University of Auckland.

Discover more about the history of The University of Auckland: http://www.auckland.ac.nz/history
