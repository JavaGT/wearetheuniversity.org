Description

Next month the Southern Hemisphere's largest agricultural showpiece kicks off for the 45th time and the University of Waikato is welcoming the opportunity to once again show off its innovative and groundbreaking agricultural research at Fieldays.

The Universities NZ - Fieldays Week Business Networking Event takes place from 4-8pm, 11 June 2013 at the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts. The event is designed to enable agribusiness managers, entrepreneurs and researchers to network with researchers from all eight New Zealand Universities showcasing their latest Agribusiness and Agritech research, as well as other conference partners including Callaghan Innovation.

Keynote speakers will include: Hon Steven Joyce, Minister for Economic Development, Wayne McNee, Director-General, Ministry for Primary Industries and Sarah Kennedy, Managing Director, Fonterra Nutrition.

There will also be a range of entertainment on the night including the finals of the inaugural Waikato Milk Cocktail Competition sponsored by the Lawrenson Group and Hamilton's popular bluegrass band The Trenwith Boys.
