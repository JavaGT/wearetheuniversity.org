Description

Overlooking one of the world's first ever marine reserves, The University of Auckland's Leigh Marine Laboratory is situated on the northeast coast of New Zealand, about 100 kilometres north of the city of Auckland. For more than 50 years the lab has been a place where marine science students and scientists are able to research in an undisturbed environment.

Meet some of our young scientists at the Leigh Marine Lab, learn about their exciting research and its impact on the health of our oceans, and the success of our marine industries.

If you would like to support the work of our young scientists at the Leigh Marine Laboratory please donate now: http://www.marine.auckland.ac.nz/donate
