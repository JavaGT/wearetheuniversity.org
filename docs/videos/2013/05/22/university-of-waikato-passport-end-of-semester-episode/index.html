Description

It's nearing the end of Semester A folks, that means everyone is going to be very busy with exams and preparing for the trip back home (if you're leaving us this semester). International Student Advisor, Huy Vu, gives some friendly advice around end of Semester A activities for international students and preparing for exams.

Don't forget if you have any questions to email info@waikato.ac.nz or head to the International Services Office on Level 2 of the Student Centre.
