Subjects within the School of Arts at the University of Waikato focus on the humanities - the human condition, and include literature, languages, linguistics and the arts, and the School of Arts at Waikato has a strong profile in terms of teaching and research in these areas.
Lecturers and researchers from this School talk to us about the Arts, the uniqueness of studying the arts at Waikato, and what some past graduates have gone on to achieve.
