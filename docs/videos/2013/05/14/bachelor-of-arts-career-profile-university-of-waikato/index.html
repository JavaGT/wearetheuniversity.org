Jeremy Johnson is a Director of local web company called Rocketspark, a company that creates software for business to use to build their own website.

Jeremy studied a Bachelor of Arts at Waikato University, majoring in Screen and Media Studies with a supporting subject of Computer Science.

We talked to Jeremy about his career, his time at Waikato University, what his current job involves on a daily basis and why he is encouraging entrepreneurs.
