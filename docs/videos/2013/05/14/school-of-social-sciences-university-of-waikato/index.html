Description

We live in fluid, exciting and challenging times. Our degrees will equip students to survive and even to thrive in our future world.

The School of Social Sciences at the University of Waikato in New Zealand is large and diverse in its offerings. Not all of our subjects are conventionally classified as 'social sciences'. Convention be blowed! Our combination of subjects has the potential to make the School more truly interdisciplinary, multidisciplinary and transdisciplinary than any other comparable grouping in New Zealand.
