Description

The 360° Leadership Programme is a development programme designed for current students in their first or second year of study at The University of Auckland. The programme is for students who want to grow their leadership capabilities and apply what they learn in a real world context. Watch the video below to hear from students who've taken part in the programme.

Find out more: http://www.auckland.ac.nz/leadership
