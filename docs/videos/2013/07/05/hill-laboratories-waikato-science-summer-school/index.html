Description

The Hill Laboratories Waikato Science Summer School aims to provide outstanding Year 12 science students in secondary schools with the opportunity to experience the wide range of science and engineering subjects available at the University of Waikato, and to show how these various areas of study are closely integrated.

Supported by Rotary and sponsored by Hill Laboratories, the event runs each December for current Year 12 students from Rotary District 9930 and includes an overnight field trip, followed by two days in the science and engineering laboratories at Waikato University. Applications are now open for the 2013 event. Apply online at http://bit.ly/16RUSQE before 23 August 2013.
