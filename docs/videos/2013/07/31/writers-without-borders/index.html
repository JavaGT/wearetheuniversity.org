Description

A group of renowned and accomplished writers are gathering at the University of Waikato on Thursday 1 August for a lounge-style discussion on their current work and the community is invited to join them.

We caught up with University of Waikato Creative Writing Lecturer, Tracey Slaughter, and visiting Distinguished Professor from the University of Connecticat, USA, Dr Lynn Bloom, for a chat about Writers without Borders and what it offers for writers.

Writers without borders will be held at Te Whare, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts at the University of Waikato on Thursday 1 August, 6.30pm. All are welcome.
