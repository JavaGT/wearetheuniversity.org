Description

The Winter Lecture Series is an annual free public lecture series run by the University of Waikato in August each year. It is a focused and relevant seminar series that provides an opportunity for robust discussion about a topical issue or issues within the community.

The role of a university within its community is to be a critic and conscience of society and to share knowledge. The 2013 Winter Lecture Series runs each Wednesday evening throughout August, 6-7pm. All lectures are free and open to the public.

For more information about this year's lectures, please see http://bit.ly/10VuBQA.
