Description

The Lord of the Rings and the Hobbit movies have put the Waikato in the limelight like never before. But what exactly are the benefits to the region and how do we continue to capitalise on this? And what did people really think of the movie?

Join Manager of Matamata PR Association, Sue Whiting; the University of Waikato's Dr Carolyn Michelle who is researching reactions to the Hobbit movie; and Hamilton and Waikato Tourism CEO Kiri Goulter, on how the Hobbit movie is changing lives and making us money.

Free public lecture, Wednesday 7 August.
Time: 6-7pm
Venue: Dr John Gallagher Concert Chamber, Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts, University of Waikato. Parking in Gate 2B, Knighton Rd.
For more information please see http://bit.ly/10VuBQA.
