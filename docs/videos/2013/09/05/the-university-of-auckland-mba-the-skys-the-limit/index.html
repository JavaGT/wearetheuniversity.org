Description

Roger Duncan, Technical Support Manager at Air Nelson, was ready to commit to his MBA journey. He chose to complete his MBA at The University of Auckland because he knew he would be involved with a quality cohort. They provided Roger the diverse learning experience that he was searching for. Find out if the University of Auckland MBA is right for you. The Graduate School of Management runs information sessions about the Postgraduate Diploma in Business and MBA programmes at 5.30pm on the first Wednesday of every month.
