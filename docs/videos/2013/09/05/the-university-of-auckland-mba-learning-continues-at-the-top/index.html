Dianne McAteer has held extensive senior leadership roles, gaining a great deal of business experience on the way. Despite this, Dianne recognised that a University of Auckland MBA will provide the intellectual stretch and value that she desired. Find out if the University of Auckland MBA is right for you. The Graduate School of Management runs information sessions about the Postgraduate Diploma in Business and MBA programmes at 5.30pm on the first Wednesday of every month.
