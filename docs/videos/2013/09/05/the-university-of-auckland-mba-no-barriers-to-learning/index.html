Description

After more than ten years in management, Damian Hoorn was considering an MBA to support his new and challenging responsibilities as well as dealing with different business problems. With a final encouragement from his friends, Damian pursued his University of Auckland MBA. Find out if the University of Auckland MBA is right for you. The Graduate School of Management runs information sessions about the Postgraduate Diploma in Business and MBA programmes at 5.30pm on the first Wednesday of every month.
Find out more: http://www.mba.auckland.ac.nz
