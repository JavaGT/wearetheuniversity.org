Description

Some of the fastest cars to come out of the University of Waikato hit the track today at the annual Mechatronics Cup competition. Fourth-year electronics students have designed and built digitally-controlled slot cars which raced off in a competition of speed, reliability and stickability.

We spoke to University of Waikato Professor of Electronics, Jonathon Scott, who says students have spent about four weeks designing and building their creations and while they'll be judged on speed "they'll get extra points for any clever things they incorporate into their cars".

To learn more about studying electronics at the University of Waikato, please see http://bit.ly/15FQAvD.
