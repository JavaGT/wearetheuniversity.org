In love with Shirley Temple: NZ's early fascination with the child star [2:20]

Description

Associate Professor Geoff Lealand from the Screen and Media programme University of Waikato discovered a large old scrapbook in a Christchurch antique shop which features news-clippings about Shirley Temple 'double' competitions in New Zealand in 1935.

This discovery has led to further research and interview with elderly ladies who were involved in the Christchurch and Auckland competitions and he talks to us about his research; cultural memory and fandom, Shirley Temple as child star and commodity, the relationships between Hollywood and a small nation like New Zealand.
