Description

The University of Waikato welcomes the opportunity to once again play a key role at the National Agricultural Fieldays at Mystery Creek this week.

As a globally connected university, we embrace this year's Fieldays theme, Getting down to Business in the Global Community, as an opportunity to emphasise the international work our researchers do.

University of Waikato Vice-Chancellor, Professor Roy Crawford, speaks about the university's long standing partnership with Fieldays and the benefits of having strong global connections in the research we conduct.

You can read more about the University of Waikato at Fieldays 2013 here: http://bit.ly/On756R.
