Description

Finance is an international discipline and it is difficult to view any business news and not see finance as a predominant theme. At the University of Waikato there are optional streams available and a core set of papers covering corporate finance (how companies make financial decisions) and investments (how the stock market and other financial markets price securities).

Finance major combines well with other majors offered in the Management School and with disciplines from outside the Faculty such as a foreign language or mathematics and computing.

As one commentator observed, " it is money that makes the world go round".

In this video, Professor Stuart Locke talks about the university's FINA 101 paper, Financial Literacy and Wealth Creation, for B Semester 2013, and the elements this paper will cover.

For more information about studying finance at the University of Waikato, please see http://bit.ly/12i9vwc.
