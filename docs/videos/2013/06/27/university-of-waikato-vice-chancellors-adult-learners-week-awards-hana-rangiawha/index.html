Description

The Vice-Chancellor's Adult Learners' Week Awards are intended to encourage more adults to access learning opportunities and to celebrate the efforts, academic and personal achievements, and contributions of adult learners.

In this video we chat with 2012 award recipient, Hana Rangiawha, about winning the award and how it has helped in her career.

The 2013 Adult Learners' Week Awards nominations open on July 15 and close August 12. You can find more information here: http://bit.ly/QdLsrT.
