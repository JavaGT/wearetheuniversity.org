The University of Waikato is proud to be a strategic partner of the New Zealand Agricultural Fieldays.

The University of Waikato has supported Fieldays since our first Vice-Chancellor, Sir Don Llewellyn, helped establish the agricultural showpiece in the 1960s. We have been a strategic partner for seven years and the agricultural expo is an important opportunity to showcase the University of Waikato's research and expertise to the community and to show how our graduates and academics are making an impact locally, nationally and internationally.

As a globally connected university, we embraced this year's Fieldays theme, Getting down to Business in the Global Community, as an opportunity to emphasise the international work our researchers do.

This video showcases a few of the highlights of the 2013 event.
