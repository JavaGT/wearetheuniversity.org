Description

The University of Waikato will once again play a major part at this year's New Zealand Agricultural Fieldays.

In this video we talk to University of Waikato PhD student, Jim Bier, about the work he's doing with Professor Johan Verbeek in using low value animal protein sources, such as bloodmeal, to develop useful biodegradable bioplastic.

Jim talks about the environmental benefits the bioplastic offers compared with traditional plastic products and where the project is heading in future based on worldwide demand.

To learn more about this research, please visit the University of Waikato at this year's New Zealand Agricultural Fieldays, site PF10-12 or see http://bit.ly/On756R.
