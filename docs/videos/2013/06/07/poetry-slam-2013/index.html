Description

Friday 17 May saw our student talents on show at the annual University of Auckland Poetry Slam. Emotion ran high as 10 students slammed whimsical, sassy, honest and political poetry to an audience of 400. The top three places were taken out by Rewa Worley, Hussam Al-diery and Maria Yeonhee Ji. Congratulations to everyone who took part!
