Description

As a globally connected university, we embrace this year's Fieldays theme, Getting down to Business in the Global Community, as an opportunity to emphasise the international work our researchers do.

Included in this year's showcase of international research is the work Waikato scientists have been undertaking in Antarctica. We interviewed University of Waikato PhD student, Stephen Archer, about his research in Antarctica and the interest this has been attracting at the stand.

You can read more about the University of Waikato at Fieldays 2013 here: http://bit.ly/On756R.
