Description

While loyalty cards with brands and retail outlets may seem a very appealing reward to the everyday consumer, it is important to understand the information around loyalty schemes and think seriously about the implications involved.

University of Waikato Professor of Finance, Stuart Locke, discusses loyalty schemes in this video blog.
