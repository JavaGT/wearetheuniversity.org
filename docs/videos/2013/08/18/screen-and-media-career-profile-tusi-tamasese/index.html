Description

Tusi Tamasese is the acclaimed director of The Orator (O Le Tulafale), the first feature length movie shot in Samoa, in Samoan and with a Samoan cast and story. Tusi came to New Zealand at the age of 18 and studied at the University of Waikato, gaining a bachelor's degree in social sciences. He talks to us about his journey, his award winning film, 'The Orator' and gives advice for budding film writers and directors.
