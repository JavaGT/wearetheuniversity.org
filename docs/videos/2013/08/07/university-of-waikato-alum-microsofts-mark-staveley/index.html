Description

Microsoft Senior Software Development Engineer and University of Waikato alum, Mark Staveley, was back on Waikato campus this week to meet with Computer Science students.

Mark undertook his Masters degree at the University of Waikato and acknowledges a large part of his success in software development comes from the foundation he received during his time studying at the University of Waikato.

Over the past 18 months Mark has been secretly working as a senior software development test engineer on Microsoft's recently-unveiled Xbox One. On May 21 he was able to reveal his work on the new console to his friends and family.

We caught up with Mark during his short visit back to campus to see what was so special to him about Waikato.
