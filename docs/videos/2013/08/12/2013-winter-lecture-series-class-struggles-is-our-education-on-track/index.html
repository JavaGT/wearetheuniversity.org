Description

What does success look like for our schools? And how should your kids be learning to give them the best start in life? Three leading education experts offer insights into current learning trends. Join Dr Noeline Wright (digital learning); Associate Professor Dr Deborah Fraser (creativity in schools) and Dr Bill Ussher (education trends) at our free public lecture on 21 August at the Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts.

The role of a university within its community is to be a critic and conscience of society and to share knowledge. The 2013 Winter Lecture Series runs each Wednesday evening throughout August, 6-7pm. All lectures are free and open to the public.
