Description

In this video University of Waikato Professor of Finance, Stuart Locke, discusses the issues around retirement with specific focus on three keys areas:

1. Entitlement and eligibility for the pension
2. Budget and your day-to-day spend during retirement
3. Your personal finance plan and the goals and objections you have for retirement

For more information about studying finance at Waikato's Management School, please see http://bit.ly/12dN9P5.
