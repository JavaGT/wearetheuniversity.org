Description

We caught up with the ladies from the University of Waikato's Career Development Services following their move to the University's Gateway building. You can now find their office in the Gateway Building at Gate 5, off Hillcrest Road.

We also introduce a new member to the careers team and a reminder about Ernst & Young's upcoming employer presentation on campus.
