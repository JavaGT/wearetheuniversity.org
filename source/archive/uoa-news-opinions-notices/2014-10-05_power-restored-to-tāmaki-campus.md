Power restored to Tāmaki Campus
===============================

Update Tuesday 7 October 6.05am
-------------------------------

* * *

**Tāmaki Campus**

Power was restored to the Tāmaki Campus at 1.20am this morning. The Campus is now open and fully-operational.

We apologise for any inconvenience, and thank you for your patience.
