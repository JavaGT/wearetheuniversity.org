Tāmaki bus for students to depart from Princes Street
=====================================================

**Tāmaki student shuttle bus to depart from Princes Street**

Students who travel to the Tāmaki campus are advised that the bus will now collect students on Princes Street, near the corner of Princes Street and Alfred Street.

**What: Tāmaki student shuttle bus new departure point  
****Where:** cnr Princes Street and Alfred Street  
**When:** effective immediately until further notice
