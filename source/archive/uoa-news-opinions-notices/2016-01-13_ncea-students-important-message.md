NCEA students – important message
=================================

If you are contacting the University regarding your NCEA results, please be advised that once the results are available to the University, the assessment of your application will begin and decisions will be made as soon as possible.

Please continue to check your online Application for Admission for updates.

### How to check your application status

All you need to do is sign into your [Application for Admission](https://apply.auckland.ac.nz/). A summary page will appear that shows the status of your application(s).
