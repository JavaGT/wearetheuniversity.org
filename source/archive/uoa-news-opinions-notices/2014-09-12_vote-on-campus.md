Vote on Campus
==============

![Vote on Campus - AUSA Clubspace (above the Quad) from 15-20 September](https://www.auckland.ac.nz/en/about/news-events-and-notices/notices/notices-2014/vote-on-campus/_jcr_content/par/textimage/image.img.jpg/1410492175546.jpg "Vote on Campus")

For the first week after mid-semester break, there will be a voting booth for the general election in the AUSA Clubspace which is situated above the Quad.  

Students and staff can advance vote on campus during the whole week from the 15 to the 20 September and vote on campus on election day as well.
