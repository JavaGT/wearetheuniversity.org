Brett Hudson welcomes new funding for Churton Park School

Brett Hudson, National List MP based in Ōhāriu, is delighted with the decision to provide extra funding for infrastructure at Churton Park School.

“Today’s announcement from Hon Hekia Parata will provide Churton Park School with $6 million to replace six classrooms and the library,” Mr Hudson said.

“Providing modern, warm and dry learning environments is conducive to students achieving their potential. This National-led government is committed to modernising education infrastructure and ensuring students can succeed.”
