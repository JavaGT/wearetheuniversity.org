New research on the impact of debt on doctors
=============================================

**Tuesday, 29 March 2005, 3:36 pm**  
**Press Release: [New Zealand University Student's Association](https://info.scoop.co.nz/New_Zealand_University_Student's_Association)**

New research on the impact of student debt on doctors to be released

The New Zealand Medical Association (NZMA), the New Zealand University Students’ Association (NZUSA) and the New Zealand Medical Students’ Association (NZMSA) will release new research into the effect of student debt on doctors this Thursday at Victoria University’s Pipitea Campus.

This is the first time that doctors’ student debt has been measured, and its impact assessed.

Media are invited to attend the launch of this research.

When: 11-12 am, Thursday, 31 March 2005

Where: Lecture Theatre 4  
First Floor  
Old Government Buildings  
Wellington
