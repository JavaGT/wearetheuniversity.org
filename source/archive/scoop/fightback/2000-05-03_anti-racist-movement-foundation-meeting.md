Anti Racist Movement Foundation Meeting
=======================================

**Wednesday, 3 May 2000, 2:36 pm**  
**Press Release: [Fightback](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Fightback)**

  
NEVER AGAIN! Zero Tolerance for Holocaust Denial- It's time to organise against racism in all its forms!

(also to discuss the shooting of student Steven Wallace)

Meeting at 5.30pm, Thursday May 4th GURU's Lounge, WSU Student Union Building, University of Waikato

Speakers from Waikato Students Union Socialist Workers Organisation the Alliance concerned groups on Campus and the local Trade Unions

contact Joe at 025 6169177 or Michelle Whittaker at the Waikato Students Union
