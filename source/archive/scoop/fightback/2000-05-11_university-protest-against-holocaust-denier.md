University Protest Against Holocaust Denier
===========================================

**Thursday, 11 May 2000, 5:22 pm**  
**Press Release: [Fightback](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Fightback)**

Students, staff and the wider community united at the University of Waikato campus today to challenge University management's appeasement of Holocaust Denier Frans Kupka.
