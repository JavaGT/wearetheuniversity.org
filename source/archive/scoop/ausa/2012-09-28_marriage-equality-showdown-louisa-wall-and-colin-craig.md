Marriage Equality Showdown: Louisa Wall and Colin Craig
=======================================================

**Friday, 28 September 2012, 3:13 pm**  
**Press Release: [AUSA](https://info.scoop.co.nz/AUSA)**

**Hunger Games Marriage Equality Edition the Final Showdown between Louisa Wall and Colin Craig**

On Tuesday 2nd of October, the Auckland University Students Association will be hosting a Marriage Equality debate as part of Politics Week.

The debate will be held in Library Basement 28 in City Campus at 7pm. The physical address is:  
_Level G,  
General Library,  
5 Alfred St,  
Auckland_

The speakers confirmed are the following:  
• Louisa Wall, Labour MP and sponsor of the ‘Marriage Equality’ Bill  
• Colin Craig, leader of the conservative party  
• Levi Joule, Queer Rights Officer of AUSA  
• Matt Flanagan, ethicist and theologian and blogger at MandM  
• Bonnie Hartfield, co-chairperson of Legaliselove  
• Joe Moeono, Chaplain of the University of Auckland
