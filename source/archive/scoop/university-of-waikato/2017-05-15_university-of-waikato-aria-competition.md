University of Waikato Aria Competition
======================================

**Monday, 15 May 2017, 4:40 pm**  
**Press Release: [University of Waikato](https://info.scoop.co.nz/University_of_Waikato)**

University of Waikato Aria Competition

Saturday 20th May, 7pm - University of Waikato Aria Competition 2017  
Join the Waikato Conservatorium of Music for the finals of our annual Opera Aria Competition this weekend, an operatic version of “The Voice”.

The evening will feature some of the Conservatorium's budding young vocalists, singing much loved and well-known arias, as they vie for prizes sponsored by the Greenlea Foundation Trust. Adjudicators include Rosemary Barnes (Auckland), Dame Malvina Major, Stephanie Acraman (HOD Voice Waikato Conservatorium).

  
Dr John Gallagher Concert Chamber  
Gallagher Academy of Performing Arts  
University of Waikato  
$10 entry at the door, free for students with a valid ID, children free of charge
