Candlelight vigil at Waikato Uni
================================

**Saturday, 14 September 2013, 2:08 pm**  
**Press Release: [University of Waikato](https://info.scoop.co.nz/University_of_Waikato)**

Peace Performance Night and Candlelight vigil at Waikato Uni!

Waikato Students will be hosting a peace performance night and candlelight vigil on the 20th of September at Namaste kitchen, Waikato Uni from 5:30 to 8 pm.

The night will include performances of poems, songs, speeches that inspire and motivate peace and then a candlelight vigil to stand in solidarity with the oppressed and more unfortunate worldwide.

If you're into Peace, Justice, human rights, then this event is for you! All welcome, both students and non students!

The event is being hosted by University of Waikato Students for Justice in the Middle East, Waikato Musicians Club and new University of Waikato Amnesty group. It is free but koha is welcome
