**Friday, 12 May 2000, 3:41 pm**  
**Press Release: [Association of University Staff](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Association_of_University_Staff)**

MASSEY GOES GLOBAL  
The Fiji Cabinet has just announced that Massey University will set up a campus in the Western Division for local students. Massey will introduce degree and diploma courses and develop courses and programmes in consultation with Government departments. Further information can be found at http://www.fijilive.com/news/12s.shtml
