Don Brash to Give Public Speech in Pukekohe
===========================================

**Friday, 19 August 2011, 2:08 pm**  
**Press Release: [ACT New Zealand](https://info.scoop.co.nz/ACT_New_Zealand)**

**Don Brash to Give Public Speech in Pukekohe**

_7.30pm Monday 22 August, Franklin: The Centre, 12 Massey Ave, Pukekohe_

ACT Party Hunua candidate Ian Cummings is very pleased to announce that ACT Leader Don Brash will be giving a public speech in the Hunua electorate at Franklin: The Centre in Pukekohe on Monday 22 August at 7.30pm.

Dr Brash will be speaking about his plan to move New Zealand forward.

“We warmly invite all members of the community to come down to meet Dr Brash and myself, discuss the Party’s priorities and hear how a strong ACT Party presence in Parliament is vital for taking New Zealand forward,” Mr Cummings said.

Mr Cummings’ ACT Party candidacy was announced on the 16th of August. He is General Manager of Wiri Oil Services and a Pukekohe local resident.
