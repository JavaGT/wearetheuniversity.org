Locke to receive peace petition at Parliament
=============================================

**Wednesday, 19 February 2003, 9:10 am**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

Locke to receive peace petition at Parliament

Green Foreign Affairs spokesperson Keith Locke will be presented with the final batch of signatures in the national "No New Zealand Support for the War on Terrorism" petition at 12.30pm today on the steps of Parliament.

The rally will also hear from Rev Dr Margaret Mayman, St Andrews on the Terrace; Dr Ramon Das, Lecturer in Morality and International Relations, Victoria University and Grace Millar, peace activist.
