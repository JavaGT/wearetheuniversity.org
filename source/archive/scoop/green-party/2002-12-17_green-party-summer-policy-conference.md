Green Party Summer Policy Conference
====================================

**Tuesday, 17 December 2002, 2:36 pm**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

  
Green Party Summer Policy Conference

The Greens will hold their annual summer policy conference in Masterton over the 24th-26th January.

Members will be discussing the party's strategic vision, as well as workshopping a wide range of policies, from constitutional reform to ground-based pest control.

Venue: Solway College, Masterton.

Sessions open to media:

Friday 24th January from 5.30pm (evening speakers include Jane Kelsey and Moana Jackson)

Sunday: open to media all day (speeches from leaders)
