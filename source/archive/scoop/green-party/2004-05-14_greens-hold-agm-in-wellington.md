Greens hold AGM in Wellington
=============================

**Friday, 14 May 2004, 12:37 am**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

Peace, poverty and power: Greens hold AGM in Wellington

The Green Party is holding its Annual General meeting and conference at Victoria University, Wellington over 5th - 7th June. Topics up for discussion range from tackling poverty in New Zealand to "clean" energy solutions. Activities include a tour of the Wellington heritage area threatened by the latest outbreak of motorway madness on Sunday and, on Monday, the launch of a Food Revolution.

Venue: Union Hall, Victoria University, Kelburn, Wellington.
