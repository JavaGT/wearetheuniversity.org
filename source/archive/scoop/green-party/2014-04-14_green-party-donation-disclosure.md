Green Party donation disclosure
===============================

**Monday, 14 April 2014, 2:49 pm**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

Green Party donation disclosure

  
The Green Party has received donations totalling sixty thousand dollars from businessman Philip Mills.

These donations have been submitted and gone through the formal disclosure processes of the Electoral Commission.
