Locke to speak at Hamilton peace rally
======================================

**Friday, 4 April 2003, 10:38 am**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

Locke to speak at Hamilton peace rally

Green MP and Foreign Affairs spokesperson, Keith Locke will speak at Saturday's peace rally in Hamilton.  
The march will start at St Peter's Cathedral and proceed to Garden Place, where a rally will be held.

  
Time: March starts 1pm, Saturday 5th April.  
Place: St Peter's Cathedral, Victoria Street, Hamilton.
