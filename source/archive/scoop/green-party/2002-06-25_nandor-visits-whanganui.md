Nandor Visits Whanganui
=======================

**Tuesday, 25 June 2002, 11:15 am**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

Green MP Nandor Tanczos will visit Whanganui on Friday 28 June.

Nandor arrives at around 10am and will officially open the 'Green House', a new campaign action space and resource centre at 31 Taupo Quay.

At 11am he will visit the Tearaway Student Newspaper office which is situated above the Green House.

Nandor will speak at Majestic Square from 12 noon. His address is entitled 'Building an EcoNation: the Green Vision for People, Planet and Politics'.

After lunch with youth organization representatives, Nandor will visit the Youth Advice Centre, 62 Taupo Quay from about 3pm.

The Youth Advice Centre is under threat of closure due to lack of funding. Nandor will speak with young people at the centre and discuss with staff options for on-going support for the young people who use the centre.
