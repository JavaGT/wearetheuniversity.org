United youth voice transcends politics
======================================

**Tuesday, 8 July 2003, 4:03 pm**  
**Press Release: [Green Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Green_Party)**

United youth voice transcends politics

Green MP Nandor Tanczos today congratulated the United Future Party on the launch of their new youth wing.

"It is fantastic to see United Future recognising that youth are the future of New Zealand," said Nandor, the Green spokesperson for Tertiary Education.

"I would like to congratulate student activist Greg Henderson on his new role and hope to see a renewed focus on tertiary education issues from the United Future Party."
