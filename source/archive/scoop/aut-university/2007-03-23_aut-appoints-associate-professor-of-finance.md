AUT appoints Associate Professor of Finance
===========================================

**Friday, 23 March 2007, 11:54 am**  
**Press Release: [Auckland University of Technology](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Auckland_University_of_Technology)**

AUT appoints Associate Professor of Finance

Peiming Wang PhD (University of British Columbia, Canada)

Dr Peiming Wang has been appointed to AUT University as Associate Professor of Finance in the Faculty of Business.

Associate Professor comes to AUT from 12-years at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore where he was director of the Nanyang International EMBA programme at Nanyang Business School.

He is published in world-class journals, including the Economics Letters, the Journal of Business and Economic Statistics and Biometrics and is widely acknowledged by peers with more than 70 citations of his research papers in the Web of Science.

His paper 'Relative Access to Credit, Relative Wealth and FDI: Firm-Level Evidence from Japanese FDI into the United States' has just been accepted for publication in the Journal of Economic Integration.
