Getting to Carbon Zero
======================

**Friday, 12 April 2019, 11:26 am**  
**Press Release: [Auckland University of Technology](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Auckland_University_of_Technology)**

April 12, 2019

Getting to Carbon Zero – can the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals Help? How?

New think tank The Helen Clark Foundation is to host a panel discussion on this topic with former PM Helen Clark, Oxfam's Dr Jo Spratt, and AUT's Dr David Hall.

  
The event is open to the public and free to attend. It will be held at AUT in the Sir Paul Reeves Building, 2 Governor Fitzroy Place, Auckland, in the 'wave room' (WG 308) on May 8 from 10am to 11am.

Media and the public should register by filling out this form: https://carbon-zero-sdgs.lilregie.com/booking/attendees/new

The Helen Clark Foundation is an independent public policy think tank located at the Auckland University of Technology. Launched in March 2019, the foundation issues research and discussion papers on economic, social and environmental issues.
