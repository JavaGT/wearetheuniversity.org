AuSM Orientation Festival 2007 line up announced!!
==================================================

**Monday, 15 January 2007, 11:42 am**  
**Press Release: [Auckland University of Technology](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Auckland_University_of_Technology)**

AuSM Orientation Festival 2007 line up announced!!!

AUT University : Monday 19th February to Friday 2 March 2007 Spread over two campuses and two weeks, the 2007 AuSM Orientation Festival at the AUT University aims to provide entertainment and enlightenment to a diverse range of students.

Thus the range of performers crosses boundaries from Drum n Bass (Concord Dawn) RnB (Spacifix) and Reggae (Katchafire) to Punk Rock (The Bleeders), Indie (from the USA, The Bellrays) and no Wave (The Mintchicks). But there's more to an AuSM Orientation than just bands, also part of the programme are DJ's (Dubhead, Logikal and Dunc tha Funk), a Hypnotist Show (Guy Cater) and even a Foam Party or two.

All events are free to AUT students (with ID); public tickets to major events are available from Real Groovy Records and the door

Website via www.orientation.org.nz choose the AuSM link:
