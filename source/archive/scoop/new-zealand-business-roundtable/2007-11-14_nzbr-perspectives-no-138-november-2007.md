**Educational Rewards**  
**_By Paul E. Peterson and Matthew M. Chingos_**  
14 November 2007  
_For-profit management of public schools is still in its infancy, and many wonder whether it can have a positive effect on student learning. In Philadelphia, that idea has been put to the test. The results, as we report in a paper issued last Friday by the Harvard Program on Education Policy and Governance, would not surprise Adam Smith._
