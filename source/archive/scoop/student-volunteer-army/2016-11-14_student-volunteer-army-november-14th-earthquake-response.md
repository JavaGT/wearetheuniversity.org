Student Volunteer Army November 14th Earthquake Response
========================================================

**Monday, 14 November 2016, 5:12 pm**  
**Press Release: [Student Volunteer Army](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Student_Volunteer_Army)**

**Student Volunteer Army November 14th Earthquake Response**

In reaction to the recent earthquakes, the University of Canterbury Student Volunteer Army (SVA) has activated it's Emergency Earthquake Response Plan.

Our volunteers are on stand-by to assist where needed and we will be utilizing our Facebook page to communicate with members of the public, including updates from Civil Defence.

SVA Vice President Isabelle Smith has stated 'The SVA is active and here to help, please do not hesitate to get in touch'

The SVA would like to encourage any members of the public who are in need of assistance or who know of anyone that may need assistance to contact us via email at info@sva.org.nz or message us on our Facebook page (**www.facebook.com/**StudentVolun).
