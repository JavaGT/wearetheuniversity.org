Temporary portfolio allocations
===============================

**Tuesday, 7 October 2014, 3:07 pm**  
**Press Release: [New Zealand Labour Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/New_Zealand_Labour_Party)**

Temporary portfolio allocations

Acting Labour Leader David Parker has temporarily allocated the following outstanding portfolios created by non-returning MPs:

  
Environment – Phil Twyford

Climate Change – Nanaia Mahuta

Tertiary Education – Chris Hipkins

State Services – Chris Hipkins

Disarmament – Phil Goff

Consumer Affairs – Phil Goff

Statistics – Phil Goff

Tourism – Damien O’Connor

Women's Affairs – Sue Moroney
