Steve Chadwick congratulates Film Festival winner
=================================================

**Saturday, 9 May 2009, 1:49 pm**  
**Press Release: [New Zealand Labour Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/New_Zealand_Labour_Party)**

8 May 2009 Media Statement

Steve Chadwick congratulates International Film Festival winner

Labour spokesperson for Arts, Culture and Heritage Steve Chadwick congratulates all the winners of the University of Northampton’s International Community Film Festival 2008.

Among the winners for Special Commendation was New Zealand Director Chris Taylor for his film ‘Te Whare’.

“The film festival is a tough competition and it is outstanding to see New Zealand performing so well on the world stage. Congratulations Chris and everybody who worked on the film”, said Steve Chadwick

The University of Northampton's International Community Film Festival 2008 attracted over 100 films exploring themes of conflict, violence and peace.
