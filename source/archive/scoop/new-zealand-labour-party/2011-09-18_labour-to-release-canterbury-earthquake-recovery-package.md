Labour to release Canterbury Earthquake Recovery Package
========================================================

**Sunday, 18 September 2011, 2:17 pm**  
**Press Release: [New Zealand Labour Party](https://info.scoop.co.nz/New_Zealand_Labour_Party)**

Phil GOFF  
Labour Leader  

**Labour to release Canterbury Earthquake Recovery Package**  
  
Labour Leader Phil Goff, Earthquake Recovery Spokesperson Clayton Cosgrove and the team of Christchurch MPs will release a new policy tomorrow designed to kick-start the Canterbury recovery and give certainty to Cantabrians as they rebuild their lives and businesses.
