VUW Domestic Fee Setting Today
==============================

**Monday, 31 October 2005, 10:29 am**  
**Press Release: [Victoria University Students Association](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Victoria_University_Students_Association)**

VUW Domestic Fee Setting Today

The Victoria University Council will be meeting at today to set the 2006 domestic tuition fees.

The meeting will be held in the Pankhurst Seminar Room PA01, Karori Campus (former Wellington College of Education) at 2pm.
