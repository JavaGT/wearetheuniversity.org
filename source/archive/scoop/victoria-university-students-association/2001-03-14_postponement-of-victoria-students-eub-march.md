Postponement Of Victoria Students’ EUB March
============================================

**Wednesday, 14 March 2001, 12:54 am**  
**Press Release: [Victoria University Students Association](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Victoria_University_Students_Association)**

Postponement Of Victoria University Students’ March To Demand The Reinstatement Of Emergency Unemployment Benefit

Students were to march to Parliament this Wednesday 14 March to demand the reinstatement of the Emergency Unemployment Benefit for students over the summer.

Due to bad weather this march has been postponed to Wednesday 21st of March.

Students at Victoria University send messages of support to other campuses nation-wide who are campaigning for the EUB today.
