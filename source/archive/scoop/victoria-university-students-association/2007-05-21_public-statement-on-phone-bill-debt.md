Public statement on phone bill debt
===================================

**Monday, 21 May 2007, 4:09 pm**  
**Press Release: [Victoria University Students Association](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Victoria_University_Students_Association)**

Victoria University of Wellington Students’ Association  
Te Roopu Tauira a te Whare Wānanga o te Ūpoko o te Ika a Māui Inc.

  
Media Release  
Monday, 21 May 2007

Attention: Education and Political reporters  
For Immediate release

Public statement on phone bill debt

The phone bill debt owed to VUWSA by the former Women’s Rights Officer has been repaid in full. This matter is now closed and there will be no further comment with regard to this debt.

Geoff Hayward  
President  
Victoria University of Wellington Students' Association
