Public Lecture: "What do we mean by the word 'health'"?
=======================================================

**Tuesday, 6 December 2016, 11:43 am**  
**Press Release: [University of Otago](https://info.scoop.co.nz/University_of_Otago)**

Please find attached and in text an invitation to a public lecture **tonight** in Wellington by Professor Sir David Skegg on politics and public health:

[UOWDeans\_Open\_Lecture\_David\_Skegg\_\_Final.pdf](http://img.scoop.co.nz/media/pdfs/1612/UOWDeans_Open_Lecture_David_Skegg__Final.pdf)

University of Otago, Wellington, Dean’s Open Lecture Series 2016: Professor Sir David Skegg, Politics and Public Health: “What do we mean by the word ‘health’? To what extent do New Zealanders enjoy good health?“

From 1992 to 1995 David Skegg chaired a Public Health Commission, and in this talk, he will discuss why we have not made more progress over the last two decades.

Wednesday 6 December | 6.00pm Nordmeyer Lecture Theatre | Level D | University of Otago, Wellington 23a Mein Street | Newtown | Wellington. More information here http://www.otago.ac.nz/wellington/news/events/otago624382.html
