University of Otago, Christchurch, Mid-Winter Dialogues
=======================================================

**Monday, 23 May 2011, 11:06 am**  
**Press Release: [University of Otago](https://info.scoop.co.nz/University_of_Otago)**

Following are the details of this year's University of Otago, Christchurch's, Mid-Winter Dialogues:  
Programme also attached.  
NOTE: Because our Rolleston Lecture Theatre is not available, the Dialogues are being held at:  
**CHRISTCHURCH NETBALL CENTRE, 455 HAGLEY AVE**

Friday July 1, 12.30pm to 1.30pm  
**The Honorable Tony Ryall  
Minister of Health  
**

Friday July 15, 12.30pm to 1.30pm  
**The Honorable Wayne Mapp  
Minister of Science and Innovation**

Friday July 22, 12.30pm to 1.30pm  
**Professor Sir Peter Gluckman  
Chief Science Advisor to the Prime Minister**

Friday August 5, 12.30pm to 1.30pm  
**The Honorable Dr Pita Sharples  
Minister of Maori Affairs, speaking about his tertiary education experiences**

Friday August 26, 12.30pm to 1.30pm  
**Dr Kevin Woods  
The new Director General of Health**  
Dialogues are chaired by Dean Peter Joyce. Question and answer session in final 15 minutes.  
Christchurch Netball Centre is a five minute walk from Christchurch Hospital. Parking available.
