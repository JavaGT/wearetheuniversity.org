Wellington campus closed Monday July 22
=======================================

**Monday, 22 July 2013, 8:45 am**  
**Press Release: [Massey University](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Massey_University)**

**Wellington campus closed Monday July 22**

Massey University’s Wellington campus will be closed tomorrow (Monday, July 22) as a precautionary measure, to allow time to assess campus buildings following today’s earthquakes.

Staff and students who need to gain access to campus buildings are asked to report to the facilities management helpdesk on the ground floor of block 5. There will be staff available to escort you to your area from 9.30am.

A further announcement regarding access to the campus on Tuesday will be made at 4pm tomorrow afternoon.

Further updates will be posted on the University’s website www.massey.ac.nz and Facebook page https://www.facebook.com/masseyuniversity
