Macmillan Brown Lecture postponed
=================================

**Tuesday, 27 October 2015, 4:46 pm**  
**Press Release: [University of Canterbury](https://info.scoop.co.nz/University_of_Canterbury)**

**Macmillan Brown Lecture postponed**

We regret the Macmillan Brown Lecture scheduled for this evening has been postponed due to a sudden illness.

Dr Colin Tukuitonga’s lecture ‘The future of Pacific regionalism: Challenges and prospects’ will be rescheduled.

Director of the Macmillan Brown Centre for Pacific Studies Professor Steven Ratuva apologises for any inconvenience.
