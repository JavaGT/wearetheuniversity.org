Former K9 Natural CEO guest speaker at entré launch
===================================================

**Wednesday, 11 March 2015, 12:06 pm**  
**Press Release: [University of Canterbury](https://info.scoop.co.nz/University_of_Canterbury)**

**Former K9 Natural CEO guest speaker at University of Canterbury entré launch**

**March 11, 2015**

University of Canterbury alumni and former chief executive of K9 Natural will be the guest speaker at University of Canterbury’s entré 2015 launch on campus tomorrow night (March 12).

Entré is a student-founded not-for-profit University of Canterbury company, run by students for all tertiary students in the Canterbury region. The students run several major business competition events during the year, including the most prestigious $85,000 Challenge.

Smith, a former international currency trader, returned to New Zealand to manage K9 Natural which was named by Deloitte in 2011 the fastest growing manufacturer in the country.
