**Tuesday, 24 March 2015, 10:09 am**  
**Press Release: [Academic Quality Agency for NZ Universities](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Academic_Quality_Agency_for_NZ_Universities)**

University of Canterbury academic audit report

  
The Academic Quality Agency for New Zealand Universities is today releasing the report of its most recent academic audit of the University of Canterbury.

This audit is the fourth of the Cycle 5 academic audits of New Zealand universities to be conducted by AQA. Cycle 5 audits cover all aspects of an institution which are related to teaching, learning and student support.

. The full report can be found on the AQA website: www.aqa.ac.nz/canterburycycle5.
