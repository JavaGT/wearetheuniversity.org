Auckland University of Technology academic audit report
=======================================================

**Monday, 18 April 2016, 3:37 pm**  
**Press Release: [Academic Quality Agency for NZ Universities](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Academic_Quality_Agency_for_NZ_Universities)**

18 April 2016: Auckland University of Technology academic audit report

The Academic Quality Agency for New Zealand Universities is today releasing the report of its most recent academic audit of the Auckland University of Technology.

This audit is the sixth of the Cycle 5 academic audits of New Zealand universities to be conducted by AQA. Cycle 5 audits cover all aspects of a university which are related to teaching, learning and student support.

Please find attached a copy of the Executive Summary. The full report can be found on the AQA website www.aqa.ac.nz/autcycle5
