Vice Chancellor To Be Dunked On Friday
======================================

**Wednesday, 20 February 2002, 12:20 am**  
**Press Release: [Lincoln University Students Association](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Lincoln_University_Students_Association)**

On Friday at 3:00 the Lincoln University Vice Chancellor (Frank Wood) and the Lincoln University Students Association President (Greg Henderson) will be dunked in a large dunking bucket as part of their Orientation week celebrations.

\"We at the students association are very happy that the Vice Chancellor is brave enough to face the students," said Henderson. "We both need to be accountable to the students and this is the perfect opportunity."

Lincoln University is holding thir Orientation this week with a theme of circus and the dunking is a part of the week long activities. A carnaval will be held on Friday with all members of the public welcome. Zed will be playing at Lincoln University on Thursday night.
