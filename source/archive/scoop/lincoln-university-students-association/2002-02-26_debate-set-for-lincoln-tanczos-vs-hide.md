Debate Set For Lincoln - Tanczos vs Hide
========================================

**Tuesday, 26 February 2002, 9:39 am**  
**Press Release: [Lincoln University Students Association](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Lincoln_University_Students_Association)**

Today at 1:00 Lincoln University will see a debate held between Green Party member Nandor Tanczos and ACT Part member Rodney Hide.

"We are looking forward to seeing these two men from different sides of the political spectrum debate against each other on many issues facing New Zealand students," said Students' Association President Greg Henderson.

"The issues that I see discussed today will vary from Genetic Modification through to Tertiary Education, issues that all affect students at Lincoln University," Henderson continued.

"We look forward to a lively debate and questions from the audience where they can," said Henderson. "All members of the public are invited."
