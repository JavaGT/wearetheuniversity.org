Fonterra CEO Speculation
========================

**Wednesday, 9 April 2003, 10:35 am**  
**Press Release: [Auckland University](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Auckland_University)**

Fonterra CEO Speculation

There has been media speculation that the Vice-Chancellor of The University of Auckland, Dr John Hood, is a 'front runner' to become CEO of Fonterra.

Dr Hood today said he was not a candidate for the position.
