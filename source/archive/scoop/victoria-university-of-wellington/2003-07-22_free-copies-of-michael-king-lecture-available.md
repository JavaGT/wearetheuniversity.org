Free Copies Of Michael King Lecture Available
=============================================

**Tuesday, 22 July 2003, 3:33 pm**  
**Press Release: [Victoria University of Wellington](https://info.scoop.co.nz/Victoria_University_of_Wellington)**

Free Copies Of Michael King Lecture Available

Waikato University is offering free copies of a lecture by historian Michael King entitled A Conversation with M y Uncle: Frank Sargeson and Hamilton.

It contains the text of the inaugural Frank Sargeson Memorial Lecture delivered by Dr King at the University in March this year.

Requests for copies can be directed to:

The Secretary Department of English The University of Waikato Private Bag 3105 Hamilton

Email: mailto:English@waikato.ac.nz
