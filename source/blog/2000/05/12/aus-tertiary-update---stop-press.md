<p>MASSEY GOES GLOBAL<br>The
Fiji Cabinet has just announced that Massey University will
set up a campus in the Western Division for local students. 
Massey will introduce degree and diploma courses and develop
courses and programmes in consultation with Government
departments.  Further information can be found at
http://www.fijilive.com/news/12s.shtml<p>
