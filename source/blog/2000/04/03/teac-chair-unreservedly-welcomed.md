<p><img align="left" width="65" height="65" src="http://www.aus.ac.nz/graphics/auslogo.gif" alt="AUS website" border="0"><br>The Association of
University Staff (AUS) unreservedly welcomes the appointment
of Dr Norman Kingsbury as chair of the Tertiary Education
Advisory Committee.</p>

<p>“Dr Kingsbury has a great wealth of
knowledge and experience which will provide a very sound
foundation to TEAC,” said Neville Blampied, AUS President in
Christchurch today.<p>

<p>“Dr Kingsbury has an intimate
knowledge of how universities, other tertiary institutions
and the Ministry work, and is universally respected within
the sector.<p>

<p>“He understands the different values,
culture, nature and purposes of universities and other
tertiary education sector institutions exceptionally well,
and will bring a humane, principled and flexible perspective
to the task of developing a strategic vision for the sector.<p>

<p>We warmly welcome the opportunity to work with the TEAC
under Norman Kingsbury's leadership,” said Mr
Blampied.</p>
